The proposal for the implementation of public-private partnership shall consist of a technical and economic rationale for the implementation of public-private partnership, the content of which is determined by the Procedure for analysing the effectiveness of the implementation of the public-private partnership, approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Procedure for the analysis of the public-private partnership implementation efficiency shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine regarding peculiarities specified by this Law. The measures provided for in Part 2 of this Article shall be implemented within a period not exceeding six months from the date of the decision on the expediency of private partnership. Article 15. Article 20. The final assessment shall be conducted for the objects: owned by the state – by the body authorised by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, which was not involved in the preparation and/or implementation of the project carried out on the terms of public-private partnership; owned by the Autonomous Republic of Crimea – by the body authorised by the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, and if such a body is not determined — by the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea; owned by the communal sector – by the body authorised by the local authority in accordance with the powers under the Law of Ukraine On Local Self-Government in Ukraine. Based on the results of the final assessment of the public-private partnership implementation, a report is prepared and sent for analysis to the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of public-private partnership.
