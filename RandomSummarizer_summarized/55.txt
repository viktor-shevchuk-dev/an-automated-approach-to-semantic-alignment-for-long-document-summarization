Under the terms of production-sharing agreements, the area of sites shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Disputes arising in case of refusal to issue a special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil shall be resolved in court. Compensation of losses of the parties related to the revocation of a special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil shall be provided by the parties in accordance with the agreement on the conditions of use of oil and gas potential subsoil. The winner of the tender for a special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil shall conclude an agreement on the conditions of use of oil and gas potential subsoil provided by the tender with the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of geological study and rational use of subsoil. Commercial development of the field shall be carried out after submission of the protocol of approval of the commercial development of the field (deposit) project, approved by the user of oil and gas potential subsoil to the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of geological study and rational use of subsoil. The owner of a special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil shall also have the right to enter into voluntary insurance agreements. Article 46.
