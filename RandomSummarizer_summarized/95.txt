Article 5. Article 6. Article 13. Information on goods (work, service) 1. The scientific and technical information shall be a public domain unless otherwise determined by the laws of Ukraine. Information may not be used for calling to overthrow the constitutional order, infringing the territorial integrity of Ukraine, propaganda of war, violence, cruelty, incitement to ethnic, racial, religious hatred, performing terrorist attacks, encroachment on the human rights and freedoms. It shall not deprive the officer and officials of the right to defend the honour, dignity and goodwill in court.
