This Law regulates the relations connected with the actions in the field of municipal electric transport in which the customers of transport services, carriers and passengers participate. According to the types of transport connection, links, routes are divided into urban, suburban and intercity. Government regulation of actions in the field of municipal electric transport and its development 1. Obligations and rights of the customer of transport services 1. Tariff policy in the field of municipal electric transport is based on principles of ensuring the availability of transport services, creating conditions for sustainable and safe passenger transportation. It is prohibited to disconnect the urban transport facilities from the electricity supplies during passenger transportation except for eliminating the consequences of the accident in electricity supplies systems.
