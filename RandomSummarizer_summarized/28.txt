Technology transfer entities 1. Interaction of technology transfer entities 1. Article 7. The authorised body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer shall participate in the development and implementation of state policy related to the acquisition and transfer of property rights to technologies and/or their components developed for budget funds, contribute to the development of domestic technologies, the production of domestic competitive products. The procedure for monitoring and the form of statistical reporting on the transfer and use of technologies and/or their components shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. State expert examination shall be carried out for: 1) technologies and/or their components that are purchased for budget funds; 2) technologies and/or their components developed or purchased for budget funds, which are transferred to legal entities registered in other countries, or to foreign individuals or stateless persons.
