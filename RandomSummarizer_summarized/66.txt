Article 7. It is prohibited to require from customers the decision of the architectural and town planning council for further consideration and approval of design documentation. Such certificate is invalid unless signed by the architect – the author of the design or the person authorised by them. The state architectural and construction control is carried out by the state architectural and construction control authorities defined by Article 6 of the Law of Ukraine “On Regulation of City Planning Activity”. The design organisation that developed the design documentation, as well as the chief architect and the chief engineer of the project are responsible for the compliance of the design documentation with the initial design data, the requirements of regulations and rules.
