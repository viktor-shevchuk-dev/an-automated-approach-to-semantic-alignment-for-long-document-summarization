Licences in the field of nuclear energy use shall be issued by the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety using the forms of a special pattern approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Licences issued by the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety are valid for a period of not less than three years. Licences for the performance of certain activities in the field of nuclear energy use may be reissued by the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety at the request of a licensee. A licence to perform activities at the stages of construction and commissioning (after loading nuclear materials into a nuclear facility), operation or decommissioning of a nuclear facility and at the stage of operation or closure of radioactive waste storage facility may be revoked only after complete removal of nuclear materials and radioactive waste or after issuance of a licence to perform activities at such facility or storage facility to another licensee.
