This Law is signed by the President of Ukraine, Viktor Yanukovych. The President of Ukraine, Viktor Yanukovych, has signed into law a bill on the closure and decommissioning of the Chornobyl nuclear power plant (NPP). The Chornobyl nuclear power plant (NPP) is to be decommissioned by the end of 2014, subject to the following conditions: Employees of Chornobyl NPP who entered a job before 15 December 2000 and retire from employment due to pre-schedule decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP shall be guaranteed the right to receive unemployment benefits in the amount of 100 per cent of the average salary at the last place of work (calculated for the year).
