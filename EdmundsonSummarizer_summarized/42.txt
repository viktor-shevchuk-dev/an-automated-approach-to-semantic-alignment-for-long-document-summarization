LAW OF UKRAINE The Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine holds: 1. To approve the State Programme for Adaptation of Ukrainian Legislation to the Legislation of the European Union (attached hereto). 2. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall: 1) bring its regulatory acts in line with this Law; 2) ensure that the regulatory acts provided for by this Law are adopted, within their competence; 3) ensure that ministries and other central executive authorities of Ukraine bring their regulatory acts in line with this Law; 4) provide for the funds for financing the State Programme for Adaptation of Ukrainian Legislation to the Legislation of the European Union in the draft laws on the State Budget of Ukraine for a relevant year. 3. This Law shall enter into force on the day of its publication. Elements of the nation-wide information network on European law may include: creation of the Internet portal; creation of the electronic system of the European Union documentation translated into Ukrainian, including the acquis communautaire acts; creation of a specialised library of the European law and its branches in the regions of Ukraine; ensuring access of the participants in the adaptation process to the databases of the European Union legislation; ensuring subscription to the official publications of the European Union.
