The freight forwarder must obtain the customer's consent to deviate from the customer's instructions, in particular in the event of a threat of damage to cargo, human safety and danger to the environment if it is necessary. The freight forwarder has the right to act at his sole discretion, notifying the client of his actions as soon as it is possible, only if it is not possible to inform about it in advance or if the response to such request is not received by the freight forwarder in due time.
