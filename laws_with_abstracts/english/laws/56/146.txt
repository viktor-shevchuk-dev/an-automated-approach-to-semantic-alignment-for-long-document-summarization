In order to obtain such approval, the relevant legal or natural person (applicant) shall submit to the authority exercising state regulation of financial services markets the information provided by the regulatory acts of the said authority, in particular on its own financial situation and business reputation, as well as ownership structure (for legal entities). The financial situation of the applicant shall be cumulative indicators reflecting its real and potential financial capabilities, including the level of liquidity, solvency and financial stability, security of working capital (equity) and their effective use, as well as assessing the applicant's ability to provide additional financial support to the financial institution in the future.
