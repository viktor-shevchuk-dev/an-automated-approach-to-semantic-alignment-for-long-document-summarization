5. The National Bank of Ukraine and National Securities and Stock Market Commission shall be obliged to hold operational meetings at least once a month or more often at the request of one of the heads of these bodies for the purpose of cooperation and coordination of their activities. Based on the results of these meetings, appropriate protocols shall be drawn up and/or interdepartmental agreements laid down. The decisions that are contained in the specified protocols and agreements shall be mandatory for consideration and implementation by each of the authorities exercising the state regulation of financial services markets.
