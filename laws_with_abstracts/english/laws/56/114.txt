The electronic copy of the agreement and its annexes (if any) shall be considered received by the client if the agreement is sent by mutual consent of the financial services provider and the client or at the client's choice to the client's e-mail address, which allows setting the date of dispatch. The agreement concluded in the form of electronic document and its annexes (if any) shall contain information about the client, including the contact details provided by him. The provisions of this paragraph shall not apply to the agreement referred to in clause 4 of this part.
