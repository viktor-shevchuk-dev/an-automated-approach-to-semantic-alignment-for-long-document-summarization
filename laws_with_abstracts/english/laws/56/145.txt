5. A legal or natural person intending to acquire the significant holding in the financial institution (other than a corporate investment fund) or increase it in such a way that the said person or entity will directly or indirectly own or control 10, 25, 50 and 75 percent of the authorised (joint-stock) capital of such financial institution or the voting rights of acquired shares (interests) in the bodies of the financial institution, must obtain the written consent of the authority exercising state regulation of financial services markets, unless otherwise provided by laws regulating certain financial services markets.
