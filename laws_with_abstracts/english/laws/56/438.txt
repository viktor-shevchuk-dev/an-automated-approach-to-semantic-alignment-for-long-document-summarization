5. For the purposes of state regulation and supervision, the National Bank of Ukraine shall have the right to receive information from the state bodies and other entities, including confidential information, regarding the financial/property status of participants in non-banking financial institutions, legal entities that are not financial institutions, but have the right to provide certain financial services, and persons acquiring or increasing significant holding in such institutions or legal entities, their business reputation, sources of funds that will be used to form the capital of non-banking financial institutions and legal entities that are not financial institutions, but have the right to provide selected financial services.
