The decision of the National Bank of Ukraine on the application of enforcement actions to the participants in the markets of non-banking financial services (except for consumers of financial services) in the form of a fine shall come into force on the day following the day when such decision is made. If the decision of the National Bank of Ukraine on the application of enforcement actions to participants in the markets of non-banking financial services (except for consumers of financial services) in the form of fine within a month from the date of entry into force was not implemented or was not appealed in court, this decision shall acquire the status of the executive document, is subject to execution by the National Bank of Ukraine in accordance with the requirements of the Law of Ukraine "On Enforcement Proceedings" and shall be transferred to the bodies of the State Enforcement Service for compulsory execution in accordance with the legislation.
