4. Activities of a telecommunication operator on public charitable fundraising from subscribers via charitable telecommunication messages carried out on the basis of an agreement concluded between a telecommunication operator and a non-profit organisation, including a charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions) shall not be considered financial services; or a territorial community, as well as the operations of a telecommunication operator, to transfer such funds to a non-profit organisation, including a charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions), or a territorial community shall not be considered financial services.
