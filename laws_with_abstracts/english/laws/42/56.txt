In pursuance of the Decree of the President of Ukraine No. 145 of 9 February 1999 “On Measures to Improve the Law-making Activities of Executive Authorities”, the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine launched a unified system of planning, co-ordination and control over legislative drafting activities and work on the adaptation of legislation of the executive authorities, and approved the Concept of Adaptation of Ukrainian Legislation to the Legislation of the European Union. Furthermore, the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine annually develops and approves the plan of work on the adaptation of legislation by providing for expenditures for financing the measures on implementing it in the draft State Budget of Ukraine for a relevant year.
