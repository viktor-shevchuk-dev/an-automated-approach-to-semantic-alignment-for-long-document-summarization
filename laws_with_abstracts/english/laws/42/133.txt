2. Draft laws, which according to the findings of the Committee of the Verkhovna Rada on Ukraine’s Integration into the European Union belong by their subject of legal regulation to the areas the legal relations in which are regulated by the law of the European Union, except for those developed in pursuance of this Programme, shall be submitted to a competent central executive authority within three days for drawing up the findings on the compliance of such draft laws with acquis communautaire. A competent central executive authority shall draw up expert findings on the compliance of the draft laws with acquis communautaire within twenty days and shall refer it to the Committee of the Verkhovna Rada on Ukraine’s Integration into the European Union for preparation of proposals on the expediency of adopting the draft law by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine and its effective date.
