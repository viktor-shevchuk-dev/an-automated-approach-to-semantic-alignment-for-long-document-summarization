The relations between road carrier, performing the transportation of passengers on suburban and intercity public bus routes, which go beyond the territory of the region (interregional routes) and executive authorities, local authorities shall be defined by the permit of executive authorities and local authorities for the service of bus route, in which the following information is set: a list of public routes (travels) to be served by the road carrier, terms of transportation organisation, quality indicators for transport services for the population, term of road carrier work.
