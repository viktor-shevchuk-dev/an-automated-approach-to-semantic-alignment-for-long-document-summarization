Executive authorities and local authorities shall involve on competitive terms under the contract an enterprise (organisation) that has specialists and work experience of at least three years in organisation of passenger transportation to organise the provision and preparation of materials for the meetings of the tender committee. The tender is held with the involvement of representatives of relevant executive authorities, as well as representatives of public organisations. The enterprise, (organisation) involved for the provision of the meetings of the tender committee shall prepare materials on the terms of the tender, passports of bus routes, analysis of the received proposals and their evaluation, contracts with the winners of the competition and other materials.
