2-1. The Register of volunteers of the anti-terrorist operation and/or implementation of measures to ensure national security and defence, repulse and suppression of armed aggression of the Russian Federation in Donetsk and Luhansk regions, carried out by the Joint Forces operation, shall be formed and maintained by the central executive authority implementing the state tax policy and shall be published on its official website. The procedure for forming and maintaining the Register of Volunteers of the anti-terrorist operation and/or implementing measures to ensure national security and defence, repulse and suppression of armed aggression of the Russian Federation in Donetsk and Luhansk regions by the Joint Forces operation shall be determined by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy.
