4. Persons performing public fundraising on behalf of or for the benefit of other beneficiaries (except for charitable organisations), shall act on the basis of a contract (agreement) on chary work with such beneficiary or one of his/her legal representatives. The contract (agreement) should specify, in particular, the purposes, place and term of fundraising or collection of other property, the order of their intended use, accounting and reporting, as well as the responsibility of the person collecting public charitable donations to the beneficiary and philanthropists in case of a breach of contract (agreement) or the procedure for using such donations.
