Parts of fishery water object are provided for use by legal entities or individuals by authorities performing the order of land plots under water (water space) on lease terms in accordance with Land Code of Ukraine, only for placement of floating fish ponds. In such a case, the limits of given for use part of fishery water object are defined by the coordinates of granted water area. Grant of a land plot of water fund under water (water space) and determination of its limits at nature (on the ground) is not carried out.
