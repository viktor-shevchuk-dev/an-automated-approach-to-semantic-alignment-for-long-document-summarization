The qualification of co-generation units is carried out by the central executive authority in the field of energy-saving following the procedure approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. The qualification period is one year. The qualification of a co-generation unit that has been put into operation for the first time is based on the design data and the results of operational tests of the co-generation unit. Qualification of a co-generation unit for each subsequent year after the expiration of its previous qualification is carried out according to the actual indicators of operation of the co-generation unit for the previous 12 months.
