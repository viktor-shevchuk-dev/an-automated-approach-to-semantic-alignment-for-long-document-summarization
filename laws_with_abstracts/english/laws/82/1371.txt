The mortgagor shall be liable for the timeliness and accuracy of the entry of information on the mortgage in the Mortgage Ledger. The mortgagor shall be obliged to indemnify the injured mortgagee or third party in full for the losses caused by the untimely entries in the Mortgage Ledger, incomplete or inaccurate entries as well as the evasion of the obligation to make the Mortgage Ledger available for review.
