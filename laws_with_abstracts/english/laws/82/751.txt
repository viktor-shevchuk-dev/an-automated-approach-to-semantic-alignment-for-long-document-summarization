If the value of the cargo has not been stated and included in the bill of lading, the compensation for the damaged piece or unit of the short cargo, may not exceed 666.67 units of account or 2.0 units of account per kilogramme of the gross weight of the damaged, deteriorated or short cargo, whichever is higher, less the value of the consignor’s (charterer’s) container or another transport unit, the value of which is determined in accordance with Article 182 of this Code.
