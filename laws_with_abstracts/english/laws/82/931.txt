If the object is not ready for towing within the contractual deadline after the arrival of the towboat at the departure point, the proprietor of the towing vessel is entitled to terminate the contract and to charge the proprietor of the object being towed for all costs incurred by the owner of the tugboat for the passage of the towboat to the departure point and the return passage to the home port or another point located no further than the home port and for the lost income (loss of profit).
