In cases of natural disasters, catastrophes and accidents that have caused interruption of traffic, as well as in case of quarantine, the acceptance of cargo may be temporarily suspended or restricted by an order of the harbour manager upon immediate notification of the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of transport, which establishes the term of the prohibition to accept cargo and its restriction and the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport safety.
