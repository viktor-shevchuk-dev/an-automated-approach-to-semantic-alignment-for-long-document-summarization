If the raised property is sold by the seaport administration due to impossibility or inexpediency of its storage, the owner of the property shall be refunded the proceeds from the sale after deduction of all expenses incurred by the seaport administration in connection with the raising, storage and sale of the property. At the same time, if the proceeds from the sale of the raised property do not cover all expenses and losses incurred by the seaport administration, the owner of the property shall reimburse them to the seaport administration.
