4. A decision of the NIPO is appealed against to the Appeals Chamber by filing an objection against the decision by the procedure determined by this Law and under Regulation of the Appeals Chamber, approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of intellectual property. A fee is levied for the submission of such objection. If the fee has not been paid within the period indicated in part 1 of this Article, the objection is deemed not filed, of which a notification is sent to the applicant.
