If the decision regarding registration of a geographical indication was made on an application to which an objection was filed under part 6, Article 11 of this Law, the timeframe for receipt of a document on payment of the state fee for registration and payment of the fee for publication of a registration shall stop until the expiry of the period for appealing of this decision by the person filing the objection, established by paragraph 2, part 1, Article 13 of this Law.
