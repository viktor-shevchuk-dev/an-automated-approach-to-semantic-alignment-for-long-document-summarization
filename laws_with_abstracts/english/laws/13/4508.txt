The person who has violated the customs rules shall reimburse the costs of the customs authority related to the storage of goods and vehicles in respect of which the decision on confiscation has been made, until such time as such decision is made. The central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy, establishes the procedure for reimbursement of these costs. The amount to be reimbursed is calculated in the manner prescribed by the legislation of Ukraine to determine the cost of paid services.
