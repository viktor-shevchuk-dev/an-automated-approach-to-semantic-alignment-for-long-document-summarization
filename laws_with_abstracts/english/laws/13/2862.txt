3. The period of verification by the customs authority the availability of debts to the customs authority under previous financial guarantees shall not exceed three business days after the submission of an application for the status of a guarantor by a legal entity and documents determined in Article 314 of this Code and this Article. The opinion on the results of such verification in an arbitrary form signed by a head of the customs authority shall, within one business day, be sent to the legal entity and its copy shall be attached to other documents.
