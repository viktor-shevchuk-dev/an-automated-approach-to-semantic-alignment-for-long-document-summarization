Customs officials by virtue of the order (directive) of the head of the customs authority or another person authorized by him are obliged to appear on duty and to work over the established daily working hours, as well as on weekends, holidays and non-working days, at night fulfilment of customs formalities outside the working hours established for the customs authorities, as well as for the implementation of urgent or unforeseen measures on customs control, customs clearance, combating smuggling and violations of customs rules and other work tasks.
