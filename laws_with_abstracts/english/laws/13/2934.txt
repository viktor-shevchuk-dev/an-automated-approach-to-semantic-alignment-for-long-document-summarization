10. Release into the relevant customs regime (except for the customs transit regime) of goods which, if imported into the customs territory of Ukraine, are subject to official control measures, shall be carried out subject to the availability of information in the Unified Automatic Information System of Customs Authorities of Ukraine on granting permission for the release of these goods into the declared customs regime in accordance with the purpose of their import into Ukraine based on the results of carrying out such measures by the relevant authorised bodies, except as otherwise provided for in paragraph 2 of this part.
