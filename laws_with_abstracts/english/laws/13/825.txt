9. If the declarant or its authorised representative provides additional documents, the customs authority shall consider the submitted additional documents and within 5 business days from the date of their submission shall make a written decision to accept the declared customs value and cancel the decision to adjust the declared customs value, or provide a substantiated refusal to accept the declared customs value, taking into account additional documents. In this case, the provided financial guarantee shall be returned (released) or sold in the manner and within the time limits specified by this Code.
