Goods (other than goods for sale or use for the purposes not directly related to business activity) imported into the customs territory of Ukraine for at least three years by foreign investors under the Law of Ukraine “On the Regime of Foreign Investments” for the purpose of investment on the basis of registered agreements (contracts) or as a foreign investor’s contribution to the authorised capital of a company with foreign investments, shall be exempt from import duty. Import duty shall be paid on the general grounds upon sale of such goods earlier than three years after the moment of their being put on the balance sheet.
