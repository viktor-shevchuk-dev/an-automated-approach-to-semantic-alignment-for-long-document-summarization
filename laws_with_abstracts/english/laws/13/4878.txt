Customs clearance of a vehicle in compliance with the conditions specified in the first paragraph of this clause shall release the persons responsible for compliance with the terms and conditions (requirements) of the customs regimes of temporary import or transit from the administrative liability provided by Articles 470 , 481 and 485 of this Code, for violation of customs rules in respect of a vehicle in respect of which the terms and conditions (requirements) of the customs regimes of temporary import or transit are not met.
