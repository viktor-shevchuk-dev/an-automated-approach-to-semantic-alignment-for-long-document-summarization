If according to the draft report on the results of the assessment of compliance of the entity with the AEO criterion, the entity does not meet the conditions of compliance with such criterion, the official of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state customs policy responsible for arranging and conducting a compliance assessment shall send to the entity a copy of such draft report in electronic form with a qualified electronic signature within a period not exceeding three working days from the date of its receipt from the customs official conducting the compliance assessment.
