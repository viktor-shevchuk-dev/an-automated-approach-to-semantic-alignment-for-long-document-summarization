1. Decision on adjusting the declared customs value of goods moved across the customs border of Ukraine with the application of import customs regime, shall be taken by the customs authority in writing during validity check of the customs value of these goods both before and after their release, if the customs authority in the cases provided by part 6 of Article 54 of this Code, finds that incomplete and/or inaccurate information about the customs value of goods was declared, including incorrect determination of the customs value of goods.
