3. If under the court decision a person who violated the customs rules is not subject to enforcement in the form of seizure of goods specified in part 2 of this Article, or the proceedings on violation of customs rules are dismissed, the proceeds from the sale of those goods shall be stored in the account of the relevant customs authority after charging the appropriate amounts of customs duties. The costs referred to in part 1 of this Article shall not be reimbursed and the commission shall not be paid to the seller.
