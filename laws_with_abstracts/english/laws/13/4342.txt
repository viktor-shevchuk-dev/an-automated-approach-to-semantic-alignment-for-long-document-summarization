5. In case of detection of violation of customs rules, for which this Code provides the application of administrative penalties by way of the confiscation of goods, vehicles, the period of administrative detention of a person who is in the office of the customs authority or in another room in connection with customs control or customs clearance of such goods, vehicles, is calculated from the moment of issuance of a motivated written decision on its administrative detention. Customs control and customs clearance of goods and vehicles are stopped. In case of detection of other violations of customs rules, the period of administrative detention is calculated from the moment of completion of customs control and customs clearance.
