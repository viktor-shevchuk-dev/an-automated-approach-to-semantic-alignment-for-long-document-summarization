9. If the person who is held to administrative liability for violation of customs rules was not present during drawing up protocol, the corresponding record shall be made in the protocol. It should be signed by the official of customs authority who has made the report and witnesses if they are present, then one copy within three working days should be sent to the person who is held to administrative liability for violation of customs rules, to the address notified by him or available in the customs authorities (place of residence or actual stay).
