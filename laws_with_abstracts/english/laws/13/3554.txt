Goods (except excisable goods) with the total invoice value not exceeding the equivalent of 150 euros, which are moved (shipped) into the customs territory of Ukraine to the address of one consignee in one dispatch from one sender in international shipments, to the address of one consignee in one express cargo carrier's shipment from one consignor in international express shipments, as well as goods (except excisable) with the total invoice value not exceeding the equivalent of 150 euros, moved in unaccompanied baggage, are subject to oral declaration on the basis of shipping documents and are not subject to customs payments.
