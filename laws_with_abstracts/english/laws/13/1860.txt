5. The goods placed in a temporary storage warehouse located outside the checkpoints across the state border of Ukraine at the airports, sea and/or river ports shall if the customs authority decided to refuse the release of such goods into the customs territory Ukraine, be exported from the customs territory of Ukraine by the carrier that has imported them. The release of the goods from the temporary storage warehouse outside the customs territory of Ukraine shall be based on the transport documents and authorisations issued by the customs authority for unloading of the goods from the vehicle.
