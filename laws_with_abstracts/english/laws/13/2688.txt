7. At the request of a taxpayer, the customs authority shall provide him/her with written information on the use of funds paid as advance payments within no later than 30 days after receiving the request. If a taxpayer disagrees with the information of the customs authority, joint reconciliation on the use of such funds shall be carried out. Results of such reconciliation shall be executed as a report in the form approved by a central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy. One copy of such a report signed shall be provided to the taxpayer.
