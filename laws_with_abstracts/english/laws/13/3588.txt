Bodies (including cabs) for the motor vehicles under headings 8701-8705, heading 8707, chassis with mounted engines for goods under headings 8701-8705, heading 8706, in accordance with UKT ZED, are subject to customs clearance as assembled vehicles. They shall be taxed at full rates of import duty in accordance with the Customs Tariff of Ukraine, excise tax and value-added tax at the rates set by the Tax Code of Ukraine.
