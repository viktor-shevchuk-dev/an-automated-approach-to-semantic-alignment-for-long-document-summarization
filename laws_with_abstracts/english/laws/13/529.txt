8.The Automated Customs Clearance System which is a part of the Unified Automated Information System of the Customs Authorities of Ukraine shall be used to perform the customs formalities stipulated by this Code. In case of failure of the Automated Customs Clearance System or the Unified Automated Information System of the Customs Authorities, the customs formalities shall be carried out in accordance with the procedure specified by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy.
