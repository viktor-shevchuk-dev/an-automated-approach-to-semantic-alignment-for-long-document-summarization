1. Deeming it necessary to conduct an examination, the official of the customs authority, in which the case of violation of customs rules is conducted, shall issue a resolution determining the grounds for the examination, the name of the expert or the name of the specialised body for examination and research of the central executive authority implementing the state customs policy, its separate subdivision or other relevant institution in which the examination is to be conducted. The same resolution raises specific issues to be resolved during the examination, as well as defines the materials to be transferred to the expert.
