8. Documents and information for official control measures shall, in addition to documents and information entered in accordance with this Code and other laws of Ukraine to the Single State Information Web Portal “Single Window for International Trade” by state authorities, institutions and organisations authorised to exercise authorisation or control functions in respect of movement of goods, commercial means of transport across the customs border of Ukraine, be entered into the Single State Information Web Portal “Single Window for International Trade” by the declarant (person authorised by him/her) before or simultaneously with the submission of customs declaration for release of goods into the customs regime chosen.
