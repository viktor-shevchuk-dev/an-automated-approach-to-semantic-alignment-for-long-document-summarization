4. Goods unloaded from a foreign-going vessel for temporary storage in case of transit within the same checkpoint, and in case of refusal to release the goods across the customs border of Ukraine or in the event goods are deemed to be imported in error, shall be exported outside the customs territory of Ukraine by the carrier, and no specific authorisation of the customs authority shall be required. Any other operations with such goods shall be carried out without the authorisation of the customs authority.
