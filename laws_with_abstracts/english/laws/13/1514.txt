4. If goods placed under the customs processing regime in the customs territory cannot be re-exported in a timely manner due to their seizure (except for arrest due to lawsuits of private persons), seizure in the case of violation of customs rules, accident or force majeure event, provided confirmation of the fact of such an accident or the action of circumstances in the manner prescribed by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy, the processing period in the customs territory is suspended for the time of such arrest (seizure, accident, force majeure event).
