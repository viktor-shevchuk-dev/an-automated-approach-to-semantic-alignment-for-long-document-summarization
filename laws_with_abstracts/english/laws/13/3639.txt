1. Members of the administrative and technical staff of a diplomatic mission of a foreign state and members of their families living with them, if they are not citizens of Ukraine and do not live in Ukraine permanently, may import into Ukraine goods intended for initial accommodation, subject to the procedure established by this Code with regard to the movement of such goods across the customs border of Ukraine (including the requirements provided by Article 319 of this Code) and shall be exempted from payment of customs duties. The list of goods that can be classified as those intended for initial accommodation is established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
