If the goods are in a state that they cannot be declared in any customs regime other than abolition or destruction, and when the owner or his authorised person disagrees to independently arrange and pay for abolition or destruction operations in respect of such goods and in the absence of other persons, that notified the customs authority in writing of such intention, the cost for loading and unloading, storage, transportation, abolition (destruction), paperwork and other costs associated with the abolition or destruction customs regime, as well as a fine shall be written off (charged) from such owner or the person authorised by him/her without notice, and in the event of impossibility of such write-off (charge) they shall be reimbursed at the expense of the state budget.
