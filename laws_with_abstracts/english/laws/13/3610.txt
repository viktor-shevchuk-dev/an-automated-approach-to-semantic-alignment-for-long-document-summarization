Vehicles classified under headings 8702, 8703, 8704 (with the total weight of up to 3.5 tons), 8711 according to UKT ZED, and trailers thereto, classified under heading 8716 according to UKT ZED, imported by residents in connection with the performance of their duties under employment contracts with non-resident enterprises, as evidenced by the documents of the relevant competent authorities of the country in which the non-resident enterprise is registered, for the purposes of this Code shall be considered vehicles for personal use. Temporary importation by residents into the customs territory of Ukraine of such vehicles is allowed for a period of up to 10 days subject to written declaration in the manner prescribed by the legislation of Ukraine for citizens and with the application of guarantees provided by Section X of this Code.
