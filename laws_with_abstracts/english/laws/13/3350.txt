Tax notice shall be issued by the head or deputy head of the customs authority taking into account the results of the consideration of the auditee’s objections (if any) within ten working days following the date when the audit report is sent or delivered to the auditee in the manner prescribed by the Tax Code of Ukraine for communication (delivery) of tax notices, and, if there are auditee’s objections to the audit report, taking into account the opinions based on the results of the consideration of objections to the audit report within three working days following the date when the hearing of objections takes place and a written response is communicated (sent) to the auditee.
