3. The documents required for customs control and customs clearance of goods and commercial means of transport (except for the documents containing classified information), the submission of which to the customs authorities is provided for by this Code and other laws of Ukraine, shall be submitted by other state authorities, institutions and organisations authorised to exercise authorisation or control functions in respect of movement of goods, commercial means of transport across the customs border of Ukraine, using the “Single Window” mechanism under this Code in the form of electronic documents certified by electronic digital signature.
