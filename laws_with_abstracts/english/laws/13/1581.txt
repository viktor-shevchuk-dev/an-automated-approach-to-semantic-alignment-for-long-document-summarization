Occurrence or detection by the customs authority of circumstances that are not reflected in the documents of the enterprise, but affect the name and volume of processed products, as well as the establishment of Ukrainian legislation prohibitions or restrictions on processing of goods outside the customs territory of Ukraine shall be the ground for refusal of the customs clearance of the following consignments of goods exported outside the customs territory of Ukraine for processing. If such circumstances do not affect the name and volume of output of processed products, information about them shall be included in the previously issued permission.
