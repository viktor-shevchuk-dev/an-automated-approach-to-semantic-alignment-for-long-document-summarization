9-3. Establish that for the period of 270 days from the date of entry into force of the Law of Ukraine“On Amendments to the Customs Code of Ukraine and Certain Other Legislative Acts of Ukraine on Import of Vehicles into the Customs Territory of Ukraine” customs clearance of vehicles classified under heading 8703 according to UKT ZED, in respect of which terms and conditions (requirements) of customs regimes of temporary import or transit were not complied with, is subject to payment of appropriate customs duties and voluntary payment to the state budget by an individual who owns such vehicle or is authorised to dispose of it and declares such vehicle for free circulation in the customs territory of Ukraine, in the amount equal to five hundred non-taxable minimum incomes.
