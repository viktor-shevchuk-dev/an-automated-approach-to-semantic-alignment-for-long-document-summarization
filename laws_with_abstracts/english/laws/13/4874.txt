9-1. In case of drafting a law providing for the introduction of a relevant permit or information on the presence or absence of goods in the relevant register as a basis for the relevant customs formalities by the customs authority, such draft law must contain a provision to ensure the transfer of such documents (information) to customs authorities in the form of an electronic document certified by an electronic digital signature, the relevant authorised state body, other institution or organisation to the Single Window for International Trade unified public information web portal in accordance with this Code on the day of issuance of such documents or inclusion (exclusion) of relevant information to (from) the relevant register.
