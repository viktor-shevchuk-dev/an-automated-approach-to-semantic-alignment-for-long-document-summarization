Supply of goods by the duty-free shop onboard an air (water or rail) commercial means of transport operating an international flight shall be carried out under the control of customs officials in containers under customs security and/or security of the duty-free shop owner. The official of the customs authority carrying out customs control and customs clearance of these goods, shall be provided with the transport documents for these goods and report on goods supplied, sold and not sold on air (water or rail) vehicle, whose form is to be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy.
