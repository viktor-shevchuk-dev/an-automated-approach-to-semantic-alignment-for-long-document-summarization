If this resolution is revoked and the case is closed, or the administrative penalty for violation of customs rules is changed based on the results of verification of legality and validity of the court decision in the case of violation of customs rules, then confiscated goods, vehicles, the amount of the fine or its corresponding part are returned to the person who was held to administrative liability for violating customs regulations, or to his representative. If the confiscated goods or vehicles cannot be returned in kind, their value shall be refunded less the amounts of due customs duties at the rates in force on the day of confiscation. Reimbursement of funds specified in this part shall be carried out by performing treasury services budget from the state budget.
