11. Customs procedures of the special simplified “released at location procedure” use the Automatic Customs Clearance System. In case the Automatic Customs Clearance System or customs official based on risk assessment system results sees no need for the involvement of customs officials in customs procedures using the released at location procedure, these customs procedures shall be automatically performed by the Automatic Customs Clearance System. Customs procedure features at special simplified “released at location procedure” are identified by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy.
