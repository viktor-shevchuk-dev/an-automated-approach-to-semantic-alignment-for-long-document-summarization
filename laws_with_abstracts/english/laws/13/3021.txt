Documents and information, including in electronic form, submitted to the customs authorities by the declarants, their representatives, other persons concerned, as well as by state authorities, institutions and organisations authorised to exercise the authorisation or control functions in respect of the movement of goods, commercial means of transport across the customs border of Ukraine, including via the Single State Information Web Portal “Single Window for International Trade”, for the implementation of customs formalities, shall be stored by the customs authorities for no less than 1095 days from the day of completion of customs procedures.
