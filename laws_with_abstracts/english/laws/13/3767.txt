7. If within 10 working days after receiving the notice of suspension of customs clearance of goods the declarant submits the objection of the goods owner to their destruction in writing to the customs authority, the customs authority the same day shall notify the right holder of the consignee, the declarant, the goods owner, their addresses, name, quantity, direction of movement, country of origin, country of departure of the goods, whose customs clearance has been suspended on suspicion of infringement of intellectual property rights, and shall provide images of such goods.
