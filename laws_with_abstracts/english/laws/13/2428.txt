1.The rules of charging duties on goods moving across the customs border of Ukraine, except for the specific types of duties, shall be determined by this Code and international treaties ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine. The rules of charging specific types of duties shall be determined by the Laws of Ukraine “On Protection of the National Producer against Dumped Imports”, “On Protection of the National Producer against Subsidised Imports”, “On Application of Special Measures to Ukrainian Imports”, and a separate law on establishing additional import duty.
