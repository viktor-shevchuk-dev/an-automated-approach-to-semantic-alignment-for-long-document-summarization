2. In the cases prescribed by this Code, the customs authorities shall, on their own initiative or the initiative of law enforcement agencies, have the right to require in writing the persons moving the goods, commercial means of transport across the customs border of Ukraine to carry out the operations provided for in part 1 of this Article. If this is the case, the cost of operations shall be reimbursed by the initiating authority. If the legislation of Ukraine is violated as a result of those operations, their cost shall be reimbursed by the holder of the goods, commercial means of transport or persons authorised by him/her.
