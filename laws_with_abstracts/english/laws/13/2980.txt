For the purposes of identification of goods placed under the customs regime, the list of means specified in part 2 of this Article may be extended with marking, including micro- or other electronic devices, or serial numbers affixed to the goods or their parts by the producer, other commercial means of identification; export (import) certificates; copies of documents that are used by the customs authority to release the goods under the previous customs regime and allow for clear identification of goods; and other similar documents.
