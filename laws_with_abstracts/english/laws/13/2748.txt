5. In case of failure to perform the obligation to pay customs payments secured with a financial guarantee provided in the form of a document, the customs authority engaging the representatives of the guarantor who issued the guarantee shall establish the circumstances of such failure, and then send to such guarantor a demand for payment of customs payments due. The copies of documents confirming the failure to perform the obligation certified by the customs authority shall be attached to the demand. The procedure for sending a demand and the list of required documents attached thereto shall be determined in the agreement provided for by part 3, Article 314 of this Code.
