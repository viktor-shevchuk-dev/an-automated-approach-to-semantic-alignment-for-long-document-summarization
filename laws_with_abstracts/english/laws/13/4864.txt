6-3. 2. Temporarily, until the entry into force for Ukraine of Common Transit Convention, the basic principles of the organisation and implementation of the goods common transit regime through the customs territory of Ukraine, the procedure and conditions for such goods transfer by enterprises through the customs territory of Ukraine under the common transit regime, the implementation of customs formalities, the application of the mechanism for guaranteeing payment of customs debt, the application of special transit simplifications and other features of the implementation of operations of the common transit regime shall be governed by a separate law.
