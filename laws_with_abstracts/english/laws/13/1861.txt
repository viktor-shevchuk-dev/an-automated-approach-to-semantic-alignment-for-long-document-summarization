6. With the authorisation of the customs authority, the goods placed in the temporary storage warehouses located outside the checkpoints across the state border of Ukraine at the airports, sea and/or river ports shall be subject only to simple warehousing operations required to ensure their preservation: movement of the goods within the warehouse for efficient allocation, cleaning, airing, maintenance of optimal temperature conditions for storage, drying (including creation of a heat flow), rust control, pest control, stock control.
