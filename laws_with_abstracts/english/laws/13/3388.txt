Probes (samples) with customs instrumentality attached and the report on their taking within a period not exceeding three calendar days after their taking shall be delivered by mail or by the customs authority official to a specialised expert examination and testing agency of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state customs policy or to its separate structural unit or other expert institution (organisation) for testing (analysis, expert examination). Probes (samples) of perishable goods or goods with a limited storage period shall be delivered for testing (analysis, expert examination) no later than the next day after their taking.
