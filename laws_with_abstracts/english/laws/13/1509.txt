Some goods processing operations or a full processing cycle on behalf of an enterprise that has been issued a permission for goods processing in the customs territory of Ukraine, and with the permission of the customs authority may be carried out by other enterprises. In this case, the enterprise shall be held liable for violation of the specified procedure for processing goods to the customs authorities, which has been issued a permission to perform operations for goods processing in the customs territory of Ukraine. Permits to perform certain processing operations or full processing cycle shall be issued simultaneously with the permission for goods processing in the customs territory of Ukraine or, if necessary, based on the application of the relevant person after granting this permission and reflected in it.
