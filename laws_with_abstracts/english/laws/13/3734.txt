3. In case of import into the customs territory of Ukraine or export outside its borders of goods specified in part 1 of this article, the customs authority carrying out customs clearance of such goods shall send to the right holder and declarant the notification specified in part 3, Article 399 of this Code on the same day by fax and/or e-mail. The date of receipt of the notification by the right holder and by the declarant shall be the date when such notification is sent by the customs authority.
