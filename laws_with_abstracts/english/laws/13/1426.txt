The customs official shall check the compliance of the duty-free shop owner's put on container and goods in the container to the data contained in the report of goods, and, in the absence of findings, shall approve the supply of goods to the air (water or rail) means of transport by affixing a personal stamp on the report. An official of the customs authority may check the contents of the container at any stage of delivery of goods by the duty-free shop to the air (water or rail) commercial means of transport and return these goods from the vehicle to the specified store.
