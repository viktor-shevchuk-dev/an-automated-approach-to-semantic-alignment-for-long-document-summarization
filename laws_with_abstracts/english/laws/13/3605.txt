1. Temporary importation by non-residents into the customs territory of Ukraine of personal vehicles is allowed for a period of up to one year. This period may be extended by the customs authorities taking into account the force majeure circumstances and personal circumstances of citizens who imported such vehicles, provided that these circumstances are documented, but only for a period not to exceed 60 days. The obligatory condition for the specified vehicles to be allowed for temporary importation into the customs territory of Ukraine is registration of these vehicles in the authorised bodies of the foreign states that is confirmed by the corresponding document.
