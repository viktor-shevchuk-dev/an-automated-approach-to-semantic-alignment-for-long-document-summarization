At the place of delivery the goods and vehicles shall be presented, and the accompanying documents transferred to the customs authority at the earliest opportunity after their arrival, or after the opening of the customs authority if they arrive in non-working hours established for such authority. If the delivery of goods, commercial means of transport within the limits of the special simplified procedures granted to an AEO-C-authorised entity is carried out to the relevant AEO facilities (buildings, structures, open or enclosed sites, etc.) defined for the application of a special simplification, namely “release at a location procedure,” further customs formalities shall be carried out in the manner defined for this special simplification.
