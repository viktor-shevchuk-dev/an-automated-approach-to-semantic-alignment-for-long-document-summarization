The temporary participant shall be deemed included in the temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund unless within three (3) years upon the grain storage facility acceptance to the temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund such grain storage facility has breached the obligations under part 8 hereof. The Guarantee Fund shall make the relevant entry on the temporary participant’s inclusion into the Register of Grain Storage Facilities Being Members (Temporary Members) of the Guarantee Fund for the Fulfilment of Obligations Under Warehouse Documents for Grain and shall issue a certificate of a Guarantee Fund participant. The form and procedure for the certificate issuance shall be established by the Guarantee Fund’s Administration Board.
