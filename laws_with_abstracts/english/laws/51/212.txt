The holder of the pledge certificate only shall be entitled to demand from the loan recipient (the first endorser) to repay the entire debt and interest thereon, as well as the pledge for the grain in the amount complying with the loan and interest thereon. In the case of the grain pledge, this is to be indicated in the warehouse certificate.
