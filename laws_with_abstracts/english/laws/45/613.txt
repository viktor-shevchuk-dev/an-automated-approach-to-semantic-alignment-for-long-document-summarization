An application for state registration of entering the information on a legal entity in the United State Register, as well as a copy of a statement from the United State Register of Enterprises and Organisations of Ukraine and a copy of a decision by an authorised body of a religious organisation to appoint the head of such organisation shall be submitted for the state registration of the information on a legal entity - religious organisation with a charter registered before 1 January 2013, the information on which is not contained in the United State Register.
