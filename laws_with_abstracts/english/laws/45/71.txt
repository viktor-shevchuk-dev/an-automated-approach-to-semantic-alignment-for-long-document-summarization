4. In case of spin-off of legal entities, the state registration of the legal entities established due to the spin-off and the state registration of changes in the information contained in the United State Register on the legal entity, from which the spin-off took place, in respect of the legal entity - successor shall be carried out. The spin-off shall be deemed completed from the date of state registration of changes in the information contained in the United State Register on the legal entity, from which the spin-off took place, in respect of the legal entity - successor.
