When carrying out registration actions in respect of a legal entity (except for state registration of dissolution) and in case of submission by such legal entity of information on the person who is the ultimate beneficial owner of the legal entity, the state registrar shall necessarily check (verify) the information specified in respect of this person, using the information from the State Civil Register, the Unified State Demographic Register, the State Register of Individual Taxpayers, the Unified Information System of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine on the search for missing persons and stolen (lost) documents upon applications of citizens.
