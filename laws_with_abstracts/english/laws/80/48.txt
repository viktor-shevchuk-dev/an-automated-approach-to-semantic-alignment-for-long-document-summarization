The Fund's money is allocated for the implementation of budget programmes, which involve works and measures to design, build, commission, operate, decommission or shut down the facilities intended for radioactive waste management, which have been transferred by licensees to state property. They include sites for long-term storage or disposal of radioactive waste generated after the processing of spent nuclear fuel of Ukrainian nuclear power plants. Other measures are also carried out related to the management of radioactive waste that has been transferred by licensees to state property. The Fund's money is allocated for the implementation of such budget programmes upon the submission of the Committee of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine on Environmental Policy, Use of Natural Resources and Elimination of Consequences of Chornobyl Disaster and approved by the Committee of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine on Budget.
