Seasonal duties shall be established on goods falling under the definition of the following codes of the Ukrainian Classification of Goods of Foreign Economic Activity (UKT ZED): 07.01–07.08, 08.06.10, 08.07.10, 08.08.10, 08.08.20, 08.09.10000, 08.09.20, 10.01–10.05, 10.08, 12.06–12.08, 12.10, 12.12.91, 12.12.92, 12.13, 12.14 and shall be taxable at a preferential rate equal to or exceeding 30 per cent under Article 6 of this Law.
