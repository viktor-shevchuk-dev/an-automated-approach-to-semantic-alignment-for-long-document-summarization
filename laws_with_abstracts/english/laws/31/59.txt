For the production of wines and cognacs of Ukraine technical grape varieties that meet the requirements of regulations shall be used. Mixing European grape varieties with Labrusca species varieties and hybrid direct producers during processing shall be prohibited. Table grapes can be used to make ordinary wines. For the production of ordinary wines, brands and cognacs of Ukraine, the use of table grapes shall be allowed, if by the content of sugars and other quality indicators they meet the requirements for wine grapes.
