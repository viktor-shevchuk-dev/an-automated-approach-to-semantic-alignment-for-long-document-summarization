Cognacs of Ukraine are strong alcoholic beverages with a specific raciness and taste, made by blending cognac alcohols obtained by distillation of cognac wine materials on special copper apparatus with fractionation, aged for at least 3 years in oak containers or stainless or enamelled containers with an oak lag. The duration of recovery of cognacs of Ukraine after blending shall be set by the decision of the subject of economic activity. Cognacs of Ukraine may be produced under their own name.
