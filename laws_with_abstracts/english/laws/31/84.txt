Producers of wine products shall be entitled to set their own guaranteed storage life of certain names of champagne, sparkling and still wines, cognacs of Ukraine, which exceed the minimum guaranteed terms set by technological documentation or other regulations, with mandatory responsibility for compliance with regulatory requirements. Own guaranteed terms shall be included in the technological instructions developed by the manufacturer for individual product names approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping state farm policy, policy in the field of agriculture, in the prescribed manner.
