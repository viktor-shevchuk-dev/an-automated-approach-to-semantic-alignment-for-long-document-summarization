The bodies exercising state supervision (oversight) as prescribed by this Law, taking into account the peculiarities defined by laws in respective fields and international treaties, must ensure the observance of the requirements of Article 1, Article 3, part 1, part 4, parts 6–8, paragraph 2, part 10, parts 12–14 of Article 4, part 11 of Article 4-1, part 3 of Article 6, parts 1–4, part 6 and part 10 of Article 7, Articles 9, 10, 12, 19, 20, 21 of this Law.
