The central executive authority implementing state policy in the field of food safety, food quality and veterinary medicine, its regional branches must ensure observance of the specifics defined by the Law of Ukraine “On State Oversight of Compliance with the Legislation on Foodstuff, Fodder, Animal By-Products, Animal Health and Welfare”, solely the requirements of parts 1, 4, 6 and 7, and paragraph 2 of part 10, Article 4, parts 1 and 2, parts 1 and 2 (except for the requirement to obtain the approval of a central executive authority implementing state policy in the relevant area of state supervision (oversight) or the relevant state collegial authority for the holding of an ad hoc event) and part 3 of Article 6; parts 1–4, 6, 8–10 of Article 7, parts 1 and 2 of Article 12, Articles 13 to 18, Article 20 and Article 21 of this Law.
