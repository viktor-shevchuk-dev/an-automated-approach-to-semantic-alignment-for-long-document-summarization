4-1. Validity of accreditation certificates of temporarily relocated institutions of higher education (scientific institutions) in the specialities and training areas that were valid on the day of relocation from the temporarily occupied territory or from settlements where government authorities temporarily do not exercise their powers shall be extended for the period of active anti-terrorist operation or any mission aimed at national security and defence, repelling and suppression of armed aggression from the Russian Federation in Donetsk and Luhansk regions, but not more than five years from the date of expiration of the relevant accreditation certificates.
