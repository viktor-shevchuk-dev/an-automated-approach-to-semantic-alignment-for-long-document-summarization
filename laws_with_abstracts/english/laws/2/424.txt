If the license is cancelled in accordance with the Clauses 1, 3–5, 7, 8 of this Part, the institution of higher education shall forfeit the right to conduct higher educational activities at a certain higher education level or under a certain educational programme presupposing assignment of professional qualifications in additionally regulated professions. If the license is cancelled in accordance with Clauses 2 and 6 of this Part, the institution of higher education shall forfeit the right to conduct all types of higher educational activities.
