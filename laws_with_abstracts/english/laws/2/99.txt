3. Junior bachelor is an educational or educational professional degree obtained at the primary level (short-term course) of higher education and granted by an institution of higher education after a higher education applicant successfully completes the educational programme of 120 ECTS credits. To grant a junior bachelor degree on the basis of professional pre-higher education, the institution of higher education shall be entitled to recognise and re-account the ECTS credits, the maximum amount of which shall be determined by higher education standard.
