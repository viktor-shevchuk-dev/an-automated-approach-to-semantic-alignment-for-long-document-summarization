The head of the institution of higher education may be dismissed by the founder (founders) or their authorised body (person), as well as by decision of higher collegial public self-government body that elected him/her, on the grounds defined by labour legislation or for violation of the institution's statute and contract terms. A proposal on recalling the head may be submitted to the higher collegial public self-government body at the institution of higher education when it is supported by at least half of the statutory members of the institution's supervisory or academic council. The decision on recalling the head of the institution of higher education shall be made by a majority of votes with the presence of at least two-thirds of the statutory members of the institution's higher collegial public self-government body.
