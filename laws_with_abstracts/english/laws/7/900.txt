The redemption of the investment certificates of the interval unit fund shall be carried out from the day following the day of receipt of the notification of the Commission of compliance of the fund with the minimum volume of assets of the collective investment institution, in accordance with the terms set by the prospectus for issue of securities of such a collective investment institution. The redemption of shares of a interval corporate fund shall be carried out in accordance with the terms set by the prospectus for issue of securities of such a collective investment institution, starting from the day of registration by the Commission of the rules of the mentioned fund and its entry in the Register.
