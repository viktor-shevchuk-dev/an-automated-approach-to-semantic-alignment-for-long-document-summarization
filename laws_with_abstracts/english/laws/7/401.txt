The request to convene an extraordinary general meeting shall be submitted in writing to the place where the corporate fund is located, indicating the surname, first name and patronymic (name) of the participants of the corporate fund, requiring the convening of an extraordinary general meeting, the reasons for convening and the agenda, except for the convening of an extraordinary general meeting on the initiative of the supervisory board. If an extraordinary general meeting is convened at the initiative of the participants of the corporate fund, the request should also contain information on the number of shares held by the participants of the corporate fund and be signed by all participants of the corporate fund, submitting it.
