The asset management company shall be obliged to transfer funds for the securities of the collective investment institution, which are withdrawn from circulation from the account of the collective investment institution, as well as the amount of additional payments (if any) to the account of the collective investment institution, the securities of which are acquired by the investor as result of conversion, not later than three working days from the date of depositing the securities of the collective investment institution, which are withdrawn from the circulation, to the account of the issuer.
