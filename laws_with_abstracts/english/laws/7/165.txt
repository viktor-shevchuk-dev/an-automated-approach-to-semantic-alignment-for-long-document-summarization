5. Failure of the founding meeting to approve the charter and the results of the shares placement of the corporate fund shall be deemed a refusal to establish a corporate fund and shall be a basis for the return of the funds contributed as the payment for the shares of the corporate fund by the founders. The funds shall be returned within 30 working days from the day of the founding meeting, whereat the resolution on the approval of the charter and results of the shares placement of the corporate fund was not taken.
