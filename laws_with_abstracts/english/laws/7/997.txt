The custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution shall carry out activities in accordance with the law, the charter and the service agreement of the custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution, entered into with the corporate fund or the asset management company of the unit fund. When such an agreement is entered into, the custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution shall be provided with a copy of the rules. The requirements for the service agreement of the collective investment institution shall be approved by the Commission.
