The participants of the corporate fund, as of the date of preparation of the list of participants, authorised to participate in the general meeting, collectively owning 10 per cent or more of the shares of the corporate fund, and the Commission may appoint its representatives to supervise the registration of participants of the corporate fund, holding of the general meeting, process of voting and declaring its results.
