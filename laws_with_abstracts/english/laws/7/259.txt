1. The general meeting may be attended by persons included in the list of participants of the corporate fund or their representatives. The general meeting at the invitation of the person convening the general meeting, may also be attended by representatives of the asset management company, the custodian of the assets of the corporate fund, the appraiser of the assets of the corporate fund, the auditor (the audit firm) of the corporate fund and officials of the corporate fund, regardless of whether they hold shares in such a fund.
