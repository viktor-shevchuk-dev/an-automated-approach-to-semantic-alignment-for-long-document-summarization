In the event of an extension of the operating term of the fixed-term collective investment institution, the participants must redeem the securities of such institution, who, within three months of the date of the resolution, submitted a written statement of redemption of their securities and of a participant in a corporate investment fund, also provided that the participant did not vote in favour of the resolution. Such redemption shall be made at the estimated value on the date of the resolution to extend the term of the fixed-term collective investment institution, and the number of securities to be redeemed from a participant may not exceed the number of securities he held on the date of the mentioned resolution. The procedure for compulsory redemption of securities of the collective investment institution shall be established by the Commission.
