During the term of this Law, in relation to mortgaged immovable property located within the territory of the anti-terrorist operation, owned by citizens of Ukraine (including individual entrepreneurs) or legal entities – small and medium-sized businesses, the effect of Article 37 (regarding the implementation of the mortgagee's right to acquire ownership of the mortgage subject), Article 38 (regarding the implementation of the mortgagee's right to sell the object), Article 40 (regarding the eviction of residents from residential buildings and premises transferred to the mortgage, for which there is a court decision on foreclosure on such objects), Articles 41, 43–47 (regarding the sale of the mortgaged object at public auction) of the Law of Ukraine “On mortgage” shall be suspended.
