Employees of temporarily displaced higher education institutions and temporarily displaced scientific institutions who did not start performing their duties at the new location of the higher education institution or scientific institution within two months from the date of publication of the order on the temporary relocation of a higher education institution and scientific institution of the central executive authority in the field of education and science, are considered to have refused to transfer to a job in another area, with the application of the consequences provided for in clause 6, part 1, Article 36 of the Labour Code of Ukraine.
