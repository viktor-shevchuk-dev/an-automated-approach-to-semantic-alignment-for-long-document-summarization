2. Legal entity lose the status of the management company from the day of termination of contract on creation and operation of an industrial park. The initiator of the creation is obliged to notify state authority during the same day about loss of the status of the management company by the legal entity. If the industrial park is included in the Register of industrial parks, the state authority is obliged to notify during the same day the central executive authority implementing the state tax policy, the central executive authority implementing the state customs policy and the relevant local state administrations about the loss of the status of the management company by the legal entity.
