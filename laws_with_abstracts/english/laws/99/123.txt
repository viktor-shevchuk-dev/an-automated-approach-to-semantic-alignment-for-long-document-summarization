If a religious centre (directorate) is a part of or recognises (declares) in any form the subordination in canonical, organisational and other issues to any other religious organisations operating in or outside Ukraine, the registration of the charter (regulations) of such religious centre (directorate) shall require a document confirming that the religious organisation recognises the subordination of a newly formed religious centre (directorate) to it or a document confirming that the newly formed religious centre (directorate) is a part of such religious organisation.
