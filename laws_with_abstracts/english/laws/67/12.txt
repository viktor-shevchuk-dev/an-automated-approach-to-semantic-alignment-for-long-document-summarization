In the case when instead of supplies of goods (works, services) imported under a barter agreement, a foreign counterparty fulfils its obligations by means of transferring funds to the account of a subject of foreign economic activity of Ukraine - a party to the agreement, the basis for removing from the control of the customs body the issue of the import part under the barter agreement shall be the submission by the specified subject to the customs body of a certificate of the authorized bank on the receipt of funds into the account of this agreement within the time limits stipulated by the legislation of Ukraine for an amount equivalent to the value of goods (works, services) specified in the agreement as well as copies of additional agreements that determine the change of the nature of the agreement.
