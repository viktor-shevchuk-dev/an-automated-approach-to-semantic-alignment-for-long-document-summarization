The central executive authority implementing state policy in the field of State Export Control directly or jointly with other central executive bodies and intelligence bodies of Ukraine promote the implementation of activities related to the international transfer of goods, when it is in the national interest, in particular by creating new and maintaining existing high-tech jobs, or restricts or prohibits such activities where it is contrary to the national interests of Ukraine, its international obligations, the objectives of the fight against terrorism, as well as when there is reason to believe that these goods belong to weapons of mass destruction or are intended for the creation of such weapons, their means of delivery, or in the absence of appropriate warranties (obligations) regarding the end use of goods.
