Open permit or conclusion can be issued to the entity of international transfers of goods, respectively, in the case when it is provided for repeated negotiations related to the conclusion of foreign economic agreements (contracts), or repeated implementation of such transfers to the different ultimate customers of a particular state of destination under such agreements (contracts), concluded during the term of such permit or conclusion within the framework of relevant international agreements, or in the case of such transfers with states which are parties to international export control regimes or which are subject to appropriate state policies, and are valid for a specified period, but not more than three years.
