The entity of international transfers of goods is obliged to provide, at the request of the central government body, implementing the public policy in the field of State Export Control, the documents and information necessary for its implementation of measures in the field of export control, including documents on warranties, technical certificates and other documents related to the conclusion and fulfilment of foreign economic agreements (contracts) on the implementation of international transfers of goods, as well as to ensure the storage of documents related to the conclusion and fulfilment of these agreements (contracts), based on which permits, conclusions or International Import Certificates were obtained, within five years from the date of completion of the international transfer of goods.
