At the request of the importer of goods, which can be submitted, including using the “single window” mechanism, the customs authorities issue a Certificate of Delivery Confirmation, which certifies the fact of receipt in Ukraine of goods specified in the specified certificate. Certificate of Delivery Confirmation of the applicant's choice shall be issued in paper form or in the form of an electronic (scanned) copy of a paper document certified by an electronic digital signature, using the “single window” mechanism.
