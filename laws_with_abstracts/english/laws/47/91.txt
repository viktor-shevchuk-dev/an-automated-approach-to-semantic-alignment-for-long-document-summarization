Upon receipt by the central government bodies exercising State Export Control and the intelligence agencies of Ukraine participating in export control measures, of information on the intentions or capability of using any goods not included in the lists in the states that are their ultimate customers, for the development, manufacture, assembly, testing, repair, maintenance, modification, modernisation, operation, management, storage, detection, identification or distribution of weapons of mass destruction or their means of delivery, these authorities shall inform the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of State Export Control, which has the right in this regard to apply to such goods the procedures of State Export Control.
