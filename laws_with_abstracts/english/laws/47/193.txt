State Export Control over the fulfilment by ultimate customers in Ukraine of obligations to use imported goods for the stated purposes shall be carried out based on analysis of reports of business entities - ultimate customers on the actual use of goods, as well as by conducting routine random inspections of actual use as intended by the ultimate customers. Such inspections can be carried out by authorised officials of the central executive authority, implementing state policy in the field of State Export Control, or by interdepartmental control commissions formed by it for this purpose.
