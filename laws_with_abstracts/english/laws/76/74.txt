Equality of the rights of women and men shall be ensured by providing women with opportunities equal to those of men in public, political and cultural activity, in obtaining education and in professional training, in work and remuneration for it; by special measures for the protection of work and health of women; by establishing pension privileges; by creating conditions that allow women to combine work and motherhood; by legal protection, material and moral support of motherhood and childhood, including the provision of paid leaves and other privileges to pregnant women and mothers.
