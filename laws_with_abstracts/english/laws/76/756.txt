9. The prosecutor's office shall, in accordance with the laws in force, continue to perform the function of pre-trial investigation until the bodies to which the relevant functions will be transferred by law begin functioning, as well as the function of control over compliance with laws in the execution of court decisions in criminal cases and in the application of other coercive measures related to the restriction of personal liberty of citizens until the effective date of the law on the establishment of a dual system of regular penitentiary inspections.
