Article 156. A draft law on introducing amendments to Section I “General Principles”, Section III “Elections. Referendum”, and Section XIII “Introducing Amendments to the Constitution of Ukraine” shall be submitted to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine by the President of Ukraine or by at least two-thirds of the constitutional composition of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine and, subject to its adoption by at least two-thirds of the constitutional composition of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, shall be approved by an all-Ukrainian referendum designated by the President of Ukraine.
