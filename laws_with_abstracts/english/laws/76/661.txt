Article 141. Village, settlement, city, raion or oblast councils shall comprise deputies elected by residents of a village, settlement, city, raion, or oblast by secret ballot on the basis of universal, equal, and direct suffrage. The term of office of village, settlement, city, raion or oblast councils, deputies of which are elected on regular elections, shall be five years. The termination of office of village, settlement, city, raion or oblast councils shall entail the termination of office of deputies of the respective council.
