The licence conditions to perform activities at a particular stage of the life cycle which the operating organisation obtains shall determine the types of works or operations, the implementation of which at the stages of construction, commissioning, operation and decommissioning of a nuclear facility and at the stages of operation and closure of radioactive waste storage facility is possible only if separate written permission is available issued by the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety. The conditions and a procedure for issuing such permits shall be determined by the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety.
