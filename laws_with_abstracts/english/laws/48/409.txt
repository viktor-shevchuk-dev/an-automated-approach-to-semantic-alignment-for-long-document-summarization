2. A seal shall not be a mandatory attribute of any document submitted by an economic entity to a government authority or local government. A copy of a document submitted by an economic entity to a government authority or local government shall be considered certified under the established procedure provided such copy is signed by an authorised person of such economic entity or it has a personal signature of an individual entrepreneur. Government authority or local government shall not require notarisation of a copy of a document unless such a requirement is established by law.
