2. Economic strategy shall be understood as the state-determined line of the economic policy, long-term and targeted upon resolving of large-scale economic, social and cultural issues, securing economic safety of the state, preserving and augmentation of its economic capacity and national wealth, increase of the public well-being. The economic strategy shall determine priority goals of the economy, means and ways of their implementation, according to the objective processes and trends of the national and global economy, with due regard to legitimate interests of economic entities.
