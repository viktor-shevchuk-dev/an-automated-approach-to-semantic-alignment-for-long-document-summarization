5. Co-operative payments shall be deemed a part of the income of a production co-operative distributed among the members of the co-operative, with due account of their labour and other participation in the co-operative’s activities. Accrual and payment of shares of equity income shall be based on the outcome of the financial year and effected from the income remaining at the disposal of a co-operative, taking into account the need to generate its funds. By decision of the general meeting of co-operative’s members, the payment of shares of equity income can be made in cash, goods, securities, etc..
