Liquidation of an economic association shall be conducted upon the decision of stakeholders, and liquidation of a state (communal) association shall be conducted upon the decision of the authority that has decided to establish the association. Liquidation of an enterprise association shall be conducted in accordance with the procedure for the liquidation of an enterprise established by this Code.
