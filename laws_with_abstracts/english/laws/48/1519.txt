A party to economic relations shall be liable for non-performance or improper performance of an economic obligation or violation of the regulations of economic activity, unless it proves that it has taken all measures to prevent an economic offence. Unless otherwise provided by law or agreement, for breach of economic obligation an economic entity shall bear economic and legal liability, unless it proves that proper performance of the obligation was impossible due to force majeure, that is extraordinary circumstances beyond control under these conditions of economic activity.
