3. The founder of an enterprise of a citizens’ association shall be a respective citizens’ association, which has the status of a legal entity, and an association (union) of public organisations if its charter provides for the right to establish enterprises. Political parties and legal entities created by them shall be prohibited from founding enterprises, except for the mass media, enterprises selling socio-political literature, other propaganda materials, products with their own symbols, holding exhibitions, lectures, festivals and other social and political events.
