7. Subornation of a supplier’s employee shall be deemed providing or offering to such an employee material values, property or non-property benefits directly by the buyer’s (customer’s) competitor or through the mediator for improper fulfillment or non-fulfillment by a supplier’s employee of his/her duties, arising from or related to the concluded agreement between a supplier and a buyer on supply of goods, execution of works, or rendering services, which have resulted or might have resulted in gaining by the buyer’s (customer’s) competitor of certain advantages before the buyer (customer).
