Economic activity under a state of emergency – a special legal regime for the activities of government authorities and local governments, enterprises, institutions, organisations provided for by the Constitution of Ukraine, which shall temporarily allow restrictions in the exercise of constitutional rights and freedoms of citizens, as well as the rights of legal entities and impose additional duties on them – may be conducted with due account of the restrictions and obligations established by the Decree of the President of Ukraine issued in accordance with the Constitution of Ukraine on the introduction of a state of emergency in Ukraine or in certain localities.
