Competent representative of entities of cross-border co-operation planning to form the Euroregion co-operation association shall send draft agreement and charter of the association for approval to a central executive authority enforcing the state regional policy, which shall, jointly with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, inspect and approve the agreement within one month after receiving it, provided that it complies with Article 157 of the Constitution of Ukraine and international obligations of Ukraine in the field of cross-border co-operation.
