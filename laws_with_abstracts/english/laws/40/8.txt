Euroregion co-operation association shall mean a form of cross-border co-operation with the status of a legal entity under the national legislation of a state on the territory of which it is located, the purpose of which is to encourage, support and develop for the benefit of the population of cross-border and interterritorial co-operation between entities and participants in such relations in the fields of shared competence and according to the powers determined under the national legislation of a relevant state;
