The regulations stipulated in Article 126-1 of the Land Code of Ukraine on renewal of land lease contracts shall apply to land lease contracts concluded or amended after the entry into force of the Law of Ukraine “On Amendments to Certain Legislative Acts of Ukraine on Countering Raiding”, and renewal of land lease contracts concluded before the enactment of this Law shall be subject to the conditions specified in such contracts, according to the rules in force at the time of their conclusion.
