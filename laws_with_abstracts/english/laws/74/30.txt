Main-line railroads of public use and placed on them technological structures, transmitting devices that are directly used to ensure the transportation process, namely: railway stations and public tracks, traction substations, contact network and other devices of technological power supply, alarm and centralisation systems, blocking and traffic control, facilities and property intended directly for emergency recovery work, are state property, assigned to the JSC Ukrzaliznytsia according to the right of economic management and are not subject to privatisation.
