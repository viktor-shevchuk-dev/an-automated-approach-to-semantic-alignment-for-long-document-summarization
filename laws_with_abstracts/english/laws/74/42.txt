Local executive authorities and local governments are responsible for improving station forecourts, constructing and streamlining entrances to railway stations, providing them with telephone communication and transport links with the settlements at the location of stations, and preventing offences in railway transport. Local executive authorities and local governments interact with railway transport enterprises to enhance the use of vehicles by cargo owners, work seamlessly with other means of transport, and develop (including on a shared basis) transport and social infrastructure for passenger service and regional economy.
