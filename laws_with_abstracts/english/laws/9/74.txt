The provisions of this Law concerning the enforcement of judgments and writs of execution referred to in the Law of Ukraine “On Enforcement Proceedings”, as well as the terms for the enforcement thereof for those enterprises of the fuel and energy sector that are parties to the debt repayment procedure on the terms set out by the Law of Ukraine “On Measures to Ensure the Sustainable Functioning of Enterprises of the Fuel and Energy Sector”, shall apply until 1 January 2016, subject to the features set out by the Law of Ukraine “On Measures to Ensure the Sustainable Functioning of Enterprises of the Fuel and Energy Sector”.
