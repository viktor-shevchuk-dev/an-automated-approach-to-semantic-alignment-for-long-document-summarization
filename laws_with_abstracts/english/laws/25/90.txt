If the public-private partnership implementation requires a private partner to obtain land parcels and/or the right to build the relevant land parcels, the lease of the relevant land parcels to a private partner for the term of the public-private partnership agreement shall be carried out in compliance with the procedure determined by the Land Code of Ukraine, and transfer of the building right shall be carried out taking into account the norms established by the Law of Ukraine On Regulation of Town-Planning Activities.
