If after announcement of the tender for selection of the private partner for the public-private partnership implementation, there is only one applicant for the said tender, the authorised executive authority may conclude the mentioned agreement with this applicant by negotiating with him/her essential conditions of the agreement, provided that such applicant meets the basic qualification requirements for tender participants, unless otherwise provided in the laws that regulate relations arising under conclusion and implementation of the agreements specified by Paragraph 1, Article 5 of this Law.
