Transfer of an object of public-private partnership to a private partner or construction of a public-private partnership object by a private partner shall be the basis for registration of private partner's proprietary rights of ownership and the use of a public-private partnership object in the State Register of Property Rights to Immovable Property. In the case of construction by a private partner of a public-private partnership object, the registration of the proprietary rights of the private partner is preceded by the registration of the property right of the state or local community, except for the cases provided for in Paragraph 10 of this Part.
