This article shall apply exclusively to those participants of joint operation agreements, according to which the value of the contribution of enterprises, 50 per cent or more in the authorised capital of which is the share of state, companies, 50 per cent or more of shares (stakes, units) of which are in the authorised capital of other companies, ta shareholder of which is the state and owns a controlling interest, as well as subsidiaries, representative offices and branches of such enterprises and companies, shall be 50 per cent and more of the total value of contributions of participants in joint activity agreements.
