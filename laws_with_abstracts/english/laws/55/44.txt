Enterprises, 50 per cent or more in the authorised capital of which is the share of the state, companies, 50 per cent or more of shares (stakes, units) of which are in the authorised capital of other companies, a shareholder of which is the state and owns a controlling interest, as well as subsidiaries, representative offices and branches of such enterprises and companies, participants in joint operation agreements, and/or persons authorised by agreements on joint activities concluded with the participation of these enterprises shall sell crude oil and natural-gas condensate of equity production on a monthly basis, extracted by virtue of a special permit to use oil and gas potential subsoil (except for volumes used for own technological needs), as well as liquefied gas - at stock auctions exclusively.
