Opening price (excluding value-added tax and costs of transportation, supply and conservation) at stock auctions for oil and natural-gas condensate shall be determined by virtue of the customs value of oil according to the central government body, implementing the state customs policy, 15 days before the date of registration of bids for the stock auction, and taking into account the grade price differential for crude oil and natural-gas condensate. Reducing the price of oil or natural-gas condensate at stock auctions below the opening price shall not be allowed.
