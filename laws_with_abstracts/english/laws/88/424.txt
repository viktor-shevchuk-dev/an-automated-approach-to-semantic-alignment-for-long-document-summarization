Athletes of high category, who serve in Armed Forces of Ukraine and in other military formations established according to the Laws of Ukraine, in law enforcement units, in rescue units and in other specialised services can be admitted to the physical culture and sports institutions or can be assigned to the positions in departments that are in charge of sports activities and development of physical culture and sport, where they are provided with necessary conditions for increasing their sports mastership and also are provided with opportunities to participate in international, all-Ukrainian and other sports competitions.
