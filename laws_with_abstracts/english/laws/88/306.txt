State authorities and local self-government authorities promote development of physical culture in educational institutions. Physical culture in the sphere of education is aimed at ensuring physical health of pupils and students; at comprehensive approach in shaping mental and physical capabilities of an individual; at improvement of physical and psychological readiness to an active lifestyle, professional activities according to the principles of individual approach; at prioritizing recreational activities; at using various ways and forms of physical education and mass sport; at life-long continuation of this process.
