International sports competitions are conducted by sports event organisers at the territory of Ukraine upon the decision of the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the sphere of physical culture and sport, while considering suggestions from relevant sports federation that has the status of a national sports federation, relevant local state administrations and local self-government authorities, at the territory of which such competitions will be conducted. These international sports competitions are included by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the sphere of physical culture and sport to the Unified calendar plan of physical culture and recreation and sports events of Ukraine. Sports competitions are not recognised and can not be recognised as international, including in advertisements and in information announcements without being included in the Unified calendar plan of physical culture and recreation and sports events of Ukraine.
