The archival set of film source materials, source materials of the film made completely or partially at the expense of the State Budget of Ukraine, as well as film copies created at the expense of private legal entities and individuals shall be stored in the State Film Fund of Ukraine. The procedure for transferring films to the State Film Fund of Ukraine, conditions for storing of film source materials and film copies and the Provision on the State Film Fund of Ukraine shall be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the sphere of cinematography.
