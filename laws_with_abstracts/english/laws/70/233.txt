The prohibition to distribute and screen films containing the popularisation and propaganda of the aggressor state's authorities and their separate actions, provided for in part 1 of this Article, extends to the distribution and screening of any films irrespective of the country of their origin made after 1 August 1991. The prohibition to broadcast films made by individuals and legal entities of the aggressor state but not containing the popularisation or propaganda of the aggressor state's authorities and their separate actions extends to films made and/or first published (screened) after 1 January 2014.
