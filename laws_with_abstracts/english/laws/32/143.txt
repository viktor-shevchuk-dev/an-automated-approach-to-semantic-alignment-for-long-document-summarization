The method of state supervision (oversight) in the field of heat supply is the implementation by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of supervision (oversight) in the field of heat supply, of relevant studies, checks, examinations, inspections of the heating and heat-using plants and networks, energy equipment of the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply in the order specified by the Law of Ukraine “On the Main Principles of State Supervision (Oversight) in the Area of Commercial Activity”.
