If the competent authority, within 30 calendar days from the date of receipt of the application, does not set the amount of the tariff calculated in accordance with parts four and/or five of this Article or does not provide a reasoned refusal to set it, it shall be considered that for the business entity generating thermal energy in installations using alternative energy sources, including heat and power plants, thermal power plants and co-generation units, the amount of the tariff is established as equal to the tariff calculated by such business entity in accordance with parts four and/or five of this Article and submitted in the application. A copy of the application (description of documents) with a note on the date of their receipt shall be a confirmation of submission of the application and tariff calculations to the authorised authority.
