Gas supply of the heat supply facilities shall be terminated in case of the heat-generating and heat supply entities' failure of timely payment for actually consumed natural gas and/or in case of natural gas withdrawal when such entities do not have a planned volume of natural gas supply for the current month which is provided by the supplier under the contract for natural gas supply, and/or in case of a failure to comply with the requirements of Article 19-1 of this Law. Such termination shall be carried out in accordance with the procedure approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
