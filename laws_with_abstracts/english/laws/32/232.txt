The National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission and local authorities shall provide the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of efficient use of fuel and energy resources, energy saving, renewable energy sources and alternative fuels with the information necessary for the calculation of the weighted average tariff for thermal energy produced using natural gas for the needs of the population, institutions and organisations financed from the state or local budget, as well as tariffs for transportation and supply of thermal energy quarterly, by the 10th day of the last month of each quarter.
