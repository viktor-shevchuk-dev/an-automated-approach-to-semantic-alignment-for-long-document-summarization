Crediting of funds of international financial organisations, foreign government agencies or funds raised under intergovernmental agreements, or funds in the form of loans (borrowings, grants), or funds raised by the state or business entities operating in the field of heat supply under state or local guarantees, to special accounts for credit funds of heat supply entities, opened by banks in national or foreign currency, and the use of such funds for the preparation and implementation of investment programmes (measures) in the relevant field shall be carried out in accordance with agreements concluded with international financial organisations, foreign government agencies, or intergovernmental agreements.
