16. A central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall send a person in whose respect a decision on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection was made, or a decision on recognising a person as a refugee or a person in need of subsidiary protection was cancelled, shall or hand to such a person a notification specifying the reasons for such a decision and explaining the appeal procedure within seven business days. A refugee certificate or certificate of a person in need of subsidiary protection, as well as their travel documents for travelling abroad, shall be seized or invalidated.
