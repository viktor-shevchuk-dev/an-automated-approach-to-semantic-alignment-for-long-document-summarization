1. Foreigners and stateless persons recognised as refugees by other countries, which are parties to the 1951 Convention relating to the Status of Refugees and the 1967 Protocol relating to the Status of Refugees, and who stay in Ukraine on legal grounds shall enjoy the same rights and freedoms, and incur the same obligations as the nationals of Ukraine, save for the exceptions determined by the Constitution and the laws of Ukraine, as well as by international treaties ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.
