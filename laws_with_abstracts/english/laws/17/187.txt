In case of doubts as to the authenticity of the information provided in the submission, and the need to prove the authenticity and validity of documents, a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall have the right to submit relevant inquiries to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Security Service of Ukraine, other government authorities, local governments and public associations, which could assist in establishing real facts about a person in whose respect the issue on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or a person in need of subsidiary protection is being considered.
