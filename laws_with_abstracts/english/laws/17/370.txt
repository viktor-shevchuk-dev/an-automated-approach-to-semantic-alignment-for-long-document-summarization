A central executive authority implementing the state policy on employment and social protection of population shall, if possible, assist persons in whose respect a decision on issuing documents for resolving the issue of recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection is made, who have been recognised as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, in employment, ensure that the persons recognised as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection receive financial aid, pensions, and other types of social security, and persons granted temporary protection receive financial aid.
