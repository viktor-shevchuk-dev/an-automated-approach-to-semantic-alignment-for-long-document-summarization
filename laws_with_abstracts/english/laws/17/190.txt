In case of detecting any mistakes or discrepancies in the information about a person entered into decisions on cessation, deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection and on cancelling the decision on recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall make a decision on correcting them within one month after receiving the personal file of a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection with a written conclusion.
