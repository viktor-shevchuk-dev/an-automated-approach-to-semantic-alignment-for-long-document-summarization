14. Based on fully-fledged study and assessment of all the documents and materials, a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall decide to cease or deprive a person of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, as well as to cancel the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, or on the absence of grounds for cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection or cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection.
