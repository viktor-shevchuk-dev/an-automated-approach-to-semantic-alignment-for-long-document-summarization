10. A decision on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or on cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary shall be made by a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection following the submission of authorised officers of the central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection within one month after the submission and his/her personal file are received. The deadline for making a decision may be extended by the head of the central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection, but no longer than up to three months.
