8. A central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall issue and provide to each person who has reached sixteen years old a refugee certificate or certificate of a person in need of subsidiary protection within 15 business days after the date the decision on recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection is made. As an exceptional case, following a relevant request of parents or legal representatives approved by the education or healthcare institution, etc., a relevant certificate can be issued and provided to a minor specified in part 12 of this Article, as soon as he/she turns fourteen years old, if the issue of the certificate is needed for exercising his/her right to education, medical treatment, etc.
