10. Information about family members of a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection under sixteen years old shall be specified in a refugee certificate or certificate of a person in need of subsidiary protection of one of the parents, and if there are no parents, a grandfather or grandmother, adult brother or sister, custodian or guardians or another person who prior to arrival in Ukraine, voluntary or owing to the practice in the country of origin has undertaken the responsibility for bringing up and supporting the children.
