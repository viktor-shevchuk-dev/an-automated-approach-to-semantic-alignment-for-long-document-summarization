Transcarpathian alunites are also a potential raw material for aluminium extraction, provided that they are used in a complex way. There are two large deposits, namely Bihan and Berehove, with proven alunite ores of 290.3 and 51.4 million tons, respectively, on the state balance of mineral reserves. In addition, there are almost 10 more ore occurrences associated with secondary quartzites within Berehove orefield.
