The implementation of the Programme requires improvement of regulatory legal acts, in particular the preparation of a new version of the Code of Ukraine On Subsurface Resources, improvement of the methodology for calculating the initial sale price of special permits for the use of subsurface resources, criteria for determining the winners of tenders (auctions) for the use of subsurface resources and the right to conclude production sharing agreements, the responsibility of subsurface users for the implementation of investment agreements, project documentation for the development of deposits, elimination of the consequences of subsurface use, the implementation of secondary operations for the purchase and sale of rights to use subsurface resources under the practice of developed countries.
