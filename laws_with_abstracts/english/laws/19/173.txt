This type of raw material belongs to Category A. In Ukraine, the titanium metallogenic province covers the north-eastern part of the Ukrainian Crystal Shield, the Dnipro-Donets Rift, and part of the Voronezh Crystal Massif's south-western slope, a total area of more than 200 thousand square kilometres. There are 78 deposits of various levels of study. Of these, 19 are accounted for by the state balance of mineral reserves. Seven deposits are at different stages of industrial development. Deposits in operation or prepared for operation have total reserves exceeding all industrial deposits in foreign countries. In general, Ukraine has created a powerful mineral resource base of titanium, which includes more than 40 deposits, including one unique, 13 large and 10 medium-sized.
