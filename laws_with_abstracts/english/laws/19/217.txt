This type of raw material belongs to Category C. Ukraine has significant and promising lithium resources. According to the proven lithium reserves and projected resources data, Ukraine can be considered the most affluent country in Europe (including the European part of Russia). Ukraine can fully meet its own needs and meet the needs of the Western European market in lithium raw materials. There are pegmatite deposits studied at various levels: Shevchenkove (spodumene ores), Polokhivka (petalite ores), Stankovate (spodumene-pelite ores), Kruta Balka (complex rare metal ores), as well as numerous ore occurrences of this type.
