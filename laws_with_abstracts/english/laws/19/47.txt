The Programme's main objective is to ensure a sustainable priority of meeting the growing needs of the national economy in mineral raw materials. Ukraine has a well-developed mineral resource base of many types and significant prospects for its expansion, a powerful geological industry, mining and processing industry, and significant potential of experienced, highly qualified scientific and technical workers. Ukraine occupies a prominent place among the CIS countries, Europe, and the world in terms of proven reserves and the extraction of mineral raw materials. In the context of the growth of global integration processes and globalisation, it is necessary to develop the national geological industry.
