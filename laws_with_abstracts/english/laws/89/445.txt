Unless the patent holder proves that the failure to use the invention (utility model) was caused by justifiable reasons for non-use, the court of law shall pass a decision to grant the permit to the interested person to use the invention (utility model) stating the scope of the application, the term of validity of the permit, the amount and the procedure of the payment of emoluments to the patent holder. The right of the patent holder to grant permission to use the invention (utility model) is not limited in this case.
