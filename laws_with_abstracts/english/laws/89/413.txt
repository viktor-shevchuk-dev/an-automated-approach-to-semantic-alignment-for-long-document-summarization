The relations in the course of the use of an invention (utility model), for which the patent is held by several persons, shall be determined by an agreement among such person Lacking such an agreement, each patent holder may use the invention (utility model) at his/her discretion, however, none of them shall have the right to grant a permit (a licence) for using the invention (utility model) and transfer the title to the invention (utility model) to another person without a consent of other patent holders.
