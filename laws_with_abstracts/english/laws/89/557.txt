1. Applications for patents of Ukraine for inventions with a validity period of five years without expert appraisal of the content (hereinafter referred to as ‘five-year patents’) submitted in accordance with Resolution of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine of 23 December 1993 "On Enforcing the Law of Ukraine On Protection of Rights to Inventions and Utility models", whose processing was not completed by the enforcement of this Law, shall be treated by the NIPO as applications for declarative patents for inventions without the expert appraisal for local novelty.
