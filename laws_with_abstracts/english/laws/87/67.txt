Failing such agreement, a party which intends to challenge an arbitrator shall, within 15 days after becoming aware of the constitution of the arbitration tribunal or after becoming aware of any circumstance referred to in clause 2 of Article 12, send a written statement of the reasons for challenging to the arbitration tribunal. Unless the challenged arbitrator withdraws from his office or the other party agrees to the challenge, the arbitration tribunal shall decide on the challenge.
