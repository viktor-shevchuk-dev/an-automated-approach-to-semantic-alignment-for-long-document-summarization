- the term “commercial” shall be interpreted broadly and encompass matters arising from any commercial relations, both contractual and non-contractual. Relationships of commercial nature include, but are not limited to, the following agreements: any trade agreements on the delivery of goods or output of services, or the exchange of goods and services; distribution contracts, trade representative; factoring; leasing; engineering; construction of industrial works; consulting; licensing agreements; investment; provision of finance; banking services; insurance; operating or concession agreements; joint ventures and other forms of industrial or business cooperation; and transportation of goods and conveyance of passengers by air, sea, rail and road;
