3. The arbitration tribunal may rule on a plea referred to in clause 2 of this Article either as a preliminary issue or in an award on the merits. If the arbitration tribunal rules as a preliminary issue that it has jurisdiction, any party may request, within 30 days after having received notification of that ruling, the authority specified in Article 6 to decide the matter; such a decision shall be subject to no appeal; while such a request is pending, the arbitration tribunal may continue the arbitration proceedings and make an arbitration award.
