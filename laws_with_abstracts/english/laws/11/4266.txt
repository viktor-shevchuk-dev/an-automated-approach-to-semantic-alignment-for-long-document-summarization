2. When transferring physical evidence and documents to the competent authority of the requesting party pursuant to the request (letters rogatory, motions) within the procedure of international co-operation, the competent authority of Ukraine may waive the requirement in respect of their returning back after the completion of the criminal proceedings in the requesting party where there is no need in the territory of Ukraine to use them for pre-trial investigation and trial in another criminal proceedings, or there are no lawful claims from third parties with regard to the title to the property concerned, or where the litigation with regard thereto is pending in court.
