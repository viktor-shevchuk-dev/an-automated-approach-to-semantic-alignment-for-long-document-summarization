3. The issue of referral of a criminal proceeding from one court to another within jurisdictions of different courts of appeals, and also of referral of a proceeding from one court of appeals to another shall be considered by a panel of judges of the Criminal Court of Cassation of the Supreme Court at the request of the court of appeals or upon a motion of the parties or the victim no later than five days from the date of such request or motion, on which a motivating ruling is issued.
