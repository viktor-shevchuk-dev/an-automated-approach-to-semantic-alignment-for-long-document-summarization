Specific time of notification shall be chosen taking into account the presence or absence of possible risks for the attainment of the objective of pre-trial investigation, public security, life or health of individuals who are involved in the conduct of covert investigative (detective) actions. Appropriate notification of the fact and findings of covert investigative (detective) actions shall be made within twelve months since the date of termination of such actions, but not later than an indictment has been produced to court.
