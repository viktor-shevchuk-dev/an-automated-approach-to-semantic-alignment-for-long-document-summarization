After the appeal proceedings have ended, the records of the criminal proceedings shall be forwarded to the court of first instance not later than within seven days, but in the proceedings on the appellate complaint against the decision of the investigating judge or the court decision on choosing a measure of restraint precautionary measure in the form of detention, as well as the extension of the period of detention imposed during the proceedings in the court of first instance until the adoption of a court decision on the merits no later than three days.
