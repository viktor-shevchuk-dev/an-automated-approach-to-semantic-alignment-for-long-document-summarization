1. Investigator, inquiring officer or public prosecutor shall immediately but in any case no later than within 24 hours after submission of a report, notification on a criminal offence that has been committed or after he has learned on his own from any source about circumstances which are likely to indicate that a criminal offence has been committed, to enter the information concerned in the Unified Register of Pre-Trial Investigations, and to initiate investigation. The investigator who will conduct the pre-trial investigation shall be appointed by the chief officer of the pre-trial investigation agency, and the inquiring officer shall be appointed by the chief officer of the inquiry agency, and in the absence of the inquiry agency he shall be appointed by the chief officer of the pre-trial investigation agency.
