4. A decision to grant a request for extradition of a person shall not be passed if such person has filed an application for the status of a refugee or person requiring subsidiary protection, or has exercised the right under the effective legislation to appeal decision as to the said statuses, until final determination of the application under the rules established by the legislation of Ukraine. Information as to whether a person has filed said applications or appealed said decision shall not be divulged to the requesting foreign state.
