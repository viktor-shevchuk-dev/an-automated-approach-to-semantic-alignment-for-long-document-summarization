Having studied and examined materials, the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine, where such materials are duly drawn up and if grounds specified by this Code or by the respective international treaty exist, shall take a decision to transfer the person sentenced by the Ukrainian court to imprisonment, for him/her to continue serving the sentence in the state of his/her nationality, and shall send information thereon to the appropriate foreign authority and to the person upon whose initiative the issue of such transfer of the sentenced person was considered.
