In case of joining in one proceeding records of pre-trial investigations against several persons suspected of committing one criminal offence, or with respect to one person suspected of committing several criminal offences, including criminal proceedings under the jurisdiction of the High Anti-Corruption Court, judicial proceedings shall be conducted by the High Anti-Corruption Court if the disjoinment of the records of criminal proceedings, which are not within the jurisdiction of this court, may adversely affect the completeness of the court proceedings.
