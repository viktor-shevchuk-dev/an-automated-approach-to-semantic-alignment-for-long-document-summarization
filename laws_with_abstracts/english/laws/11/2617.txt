Special pre-trial investigation shall be conducted following the investigative judge's resolution in criminal proceedings relating to crimes specified in Articles 109, 110, 110-2, 111, 112, 113, 114, 114-1, 115, 116, 118, parts 2–5 of Article 191 (in case of abuse of power by an official), Articles 209, 255–258, 258-1, 258-2, 258-3, 258-4, 258-5, 348, 364, 364-1, 365, 365-2, 368, 368-2, 368-3, 368-4, 369, 369-2, 370, 379, 400, 436, 436-1, 437, 438, 439, 440, 441, 442, 443, 444, 446, 447 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine, with regard to a suspect, except for minors, who hides from the investigation and judicial bodies with the view of avoiding criminal liability, and if he/she is announced in interstate or international wanted list. Special pre-trial investigation of other crimes shall not be allowed, except in cases where crimes are committed by persons who are hiding from the investigation and judicial bodies with the view of avoiding criminal liability, and if he/she is announced in interstate or international wanted list, and they are investigated in the same criminal proceedings with crimes specified in this part, and the with the view of avoiding criminal liability, and if he/she is announced in interstate or international wanted list, and they are investigated in the same criminal proceedings with the crimes specified in this part, and disjoining of records on them may adversely affect the completeness of the pre-trial investigation and court proceedings.
