20-3. Procedural actions within the framework of international co-operation in accordance with the Agreement between Ukraine and the Kingdom of the Netherlands on international legal co-operation in connection with the crimes related to the downing of Malaysia Airlines flight MH17 on 17 July 2014, concluded on 7 July 2017 in the city of Tallinn, shall be held in the manner prescribed by this Code, with due account of specific features established by the Law of Ukraine “On the implementation of the Agreement between Ukraine and the Kingdom of the Netherlands on international legal co-operation in connection with the crimes related to the downing of flight MH17 of Malaysia Airlines on 17 July 2014.”
