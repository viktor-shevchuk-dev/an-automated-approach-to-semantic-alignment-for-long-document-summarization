1. Having established that the appellate complaint against judgment or ruling of the court of first instance has been filed in violation of requirements of Article 396 hereof, the judge-rapporteur shall pass the ruling to take no action on the appellate complaint such ruling stating shortcomings of the complaint and fixing a time limit which is sufficient to eliminate shortcomings and may not exceed fifteen days after the day of receipt of the ruling by the appellant. A copy of the ruling to take no action on the appellate complaint shall be immediately sent to the appellant.
