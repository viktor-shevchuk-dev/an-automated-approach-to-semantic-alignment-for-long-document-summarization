Having got the evidence that the given person violated the terms and conditions of his/her release on probation, the court, by its ruling, shall revoke the ruling to close criminal proceedings and relieve the person from criminal liability, and shall submit the records of the proceedings for conducting the pre-trial investigation in accordance with the general procedure, or conduct judicial proceedings in accordance with the general procedure where the decision on the relief from criminal liability was passed after the indictment had been submitted to the court.
