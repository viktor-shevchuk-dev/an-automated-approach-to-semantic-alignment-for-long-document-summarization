The issue of transfer on the basis of paragraph 5 of this clause of the motion for selection or extension of a measure of restraint in the form of detention to another court shall be decided by the presiding judge of the respective appellate court (presiding judge of the Criminal Court of Cassation in the case of transfer between courts within the jurisdiction of different courts of appeals) upon a reasoned motion of the local court (court of appeals) immediately, but not later than 24 hours from the date of receipt of such motion, which shall be supported by the respective ruling. Jurisdictional disputes between courts shall not be allowed in this case.
