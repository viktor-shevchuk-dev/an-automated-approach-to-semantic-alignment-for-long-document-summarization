The court, by its ruling, shall revoke the judgment by which the agreement was approved, provided the person who has filed the corresponding motion proves that the convict failed to fulfil the terms and conditions of the agreement. The revocation of the judgment shall entail scheduling the court proceedings in accordance with the regular procedure, or returning the records of proceedings for the completion of the pre-trial investigation in accordance with the regular procedure where the agreement was initiated at the stage of the pre-trial investigation.
