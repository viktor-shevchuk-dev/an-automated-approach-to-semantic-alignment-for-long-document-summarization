4. Failure to appear of the parties or other participants to the criminal proceedings who have been duly notified of the date, time and place of the appeal review and have not informed of any good cause for their non-appearance, shall not preclude holding of the appeal proceedings. Where participation in the court session of those participants to the criminal proceedings who have failed to appear shall be mandatory by the decision of the appellate court or by law, appeal review shall be adjourned.
