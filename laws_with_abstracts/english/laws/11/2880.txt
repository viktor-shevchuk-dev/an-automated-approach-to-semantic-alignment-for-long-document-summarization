In case of special judicial proceedings the summons shall be sent to the last-known address of residence of staying of the accused and the procedural documents subject to the delivery to the accused shall be sent to the defence counsel. Information about the documents and the summons shall be published in national mass media according to provisions of Article 297-5of this Code. The accused shall be deemed to have been properly informed about the summons contents from the moment of its publishing in the national mass media.
