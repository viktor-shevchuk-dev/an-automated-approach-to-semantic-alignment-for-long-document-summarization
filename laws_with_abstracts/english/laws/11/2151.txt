2. Examination shall be made upon ruling of public prosecutor and, where necessary, with participation of a forensic medical examiner or a doctor. Examination which is accompanied by denudation of the individual examined shall be conducted by an individual of the same sex, with exception for a doctor, and upon consent of the individual examined. Investigator or public prosecutor shall have no right to be present when an individual of other sex is being examined, where the examination involves the necessity to denude the individual examined.
