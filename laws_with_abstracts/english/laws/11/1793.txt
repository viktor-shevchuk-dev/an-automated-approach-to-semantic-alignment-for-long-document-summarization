1. A suspect or accused, to whom a measure of restraint has been applied, his/her defence counsel shall be entitled to file with a local court, within the territorial jurisdiction of which a pre-trial investigation is conducted, and in criminal proceedings on criminal offences under the jurisdiction of the High Anti-Corruption Court file to the High Anti-Corruption Court a motion to change the measure of restraint, including the revocation or change of additional duties provided for by part 5 of Article 194 hereof and imposed on him/her by the investigating judge or court, or for modifying the manner of their performance.
