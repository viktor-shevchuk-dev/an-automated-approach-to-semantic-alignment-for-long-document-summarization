Attachment of property shall aim to prevent the possibility of its concealment, damage, deterioration, destruction, transformation or alienation. The investigator or public prosecutor shall take all necessary measures to identify and search for property that may be attached in criminal proceedings, in particular by requesting the necessary information from the National Agency of Ukraine for Detection, Search and Management of Assets Obtained from Corruption and Other Crimes, other government authorities and local governments, individuals and legal entities.
