14. Criminal proceedings concerning the consideration of an indictment against a minor, motions for release from criminal liability, application of coercive measures of a medical or reformatory nature, their continuation, change or discontinuation, and also criminal proceedings under appellate or cassation procedure as for review of court decisions adopted in respect of these issues shall be conducted by a judge authorised in accordance with the Law of Ukraine “On the Judiciary and the Status of Judges” for the conduct of criminal proceedings against minors.
