Public prosecutor supervising pre-trial investigations conducted by detectives of the National Anti-Corruption Bureau of Ukraine may, by his ruling, attribute criminal proceedings in criminal offences provided for by paragraph 1 of this part to the jurisdiction of detectives of the National Anti-Corruption Bureau of Ukraine where the respective criminal offence has caused or could have caused grave consequences to legally protected freedoms and interests of an individual or legal entity, as well as to state or public interests. Grave consequences shall be deemed as harm to the vital interests of society and the state, including state sovereignty, territorial integrity of Ukraine, exercise of constitutional rights, freedoms and responsibilities of three or more persons.
