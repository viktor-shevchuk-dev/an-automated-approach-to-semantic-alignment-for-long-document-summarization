3. When bail is applied as a measure of restraint, the suspect, accused shall be informed of his/her duties and implications of non-fulfilment, and the bail bondsman shall be informed of the criminal offence that the person concerned is suspected or accused of, the statutory punishment for the commission thereof, the duties of ensuring proper behaviour of the suspect or accused and his/her appearance upon summons, as well as implications of non-fulfilment of such duties.
