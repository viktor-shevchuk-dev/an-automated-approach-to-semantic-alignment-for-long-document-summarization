2. A summoned person shall not be convicted of a criminal offence; arrested; be subjected to an imposed restraint measure in the form of detention; be subjected to other measures in support of criminal proceedings or restriction of personal freedom either for any criminal offence that is the subject of criminal proceedings or any other criminal offence committed before crossing the state border of Ukraine when entering Ukraine. A sentence passed on the person before that person has crossed the state border of Ukraine may not be executed. A suspect, defendant or convict may be arrested or subjected to a restraint measure or execution of a sentence only for an offence specified in the summons.
