10. Where during the pre-trial investigation other criminal offences committed by the person under pre-trial investigation or another person are established, where they are related to criminal offences committed by the person under pre-trial investigation and which are not subject to that body which conducts pre-trial investigation in criminal proceedings, the prosecutor who supervises the pre-trial investigation, in case of impossibility to allocate these records in a separate proceeding, shall determine by his ruling the jurisdiction of all these criminal offences.
