Duration of custody shall be calculated from the date of having been committed to custody, and where commission to custody was preceded by apprehension of the suspect or accused, from the date of apprehension. Time of custody shall include the time spent by the person concerned in a healthcare facility while undergoing in-patient psychiatric examination. In case of repeated commission to custody of a person in the course of the same criminal proceedings, time of custody shall be calculated with account of the time of the previous term under custody.
