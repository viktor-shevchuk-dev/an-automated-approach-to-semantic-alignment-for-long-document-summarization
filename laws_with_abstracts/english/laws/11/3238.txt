2. Where issuance of court decision in the form of ruling (resolution) requires significant time, the court shall have the right to confine itself to the issuance and pronouncement of a part of the operative part of decision to be signed by all judges. The full text of the ruling (resolution) shall be issued no later than within five days upon pronouncement of the operative part of decision and pronounced to the parties to judicial proceedings. The time of pronouncement of full text of the ruling shall be indicated in a previously issued operative part of decision.
