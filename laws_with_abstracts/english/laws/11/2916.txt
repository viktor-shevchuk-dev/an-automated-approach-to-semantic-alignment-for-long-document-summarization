3. Irrespective of the presence of motions, the court shall dispose the issue of expedience to extend the period of keeping the accused in custody until the expiry of the two-month period after the receipt by the court of the indictment, a motion to enforce compulsory medical or reformatory measures, or after the day of enforcing in respect of the accused of the measure of restraint in the form of keeping in custody. Upon the results of consideration of the issue, the court shall by its motivated ruling, repeal or change the measure of restraint in the form of keeping in custody, or extent its validity for a period that may not exceed two months. A copy of the ruling shall be handed over to the accused and the public prosecutor and shall be forwarded to the authorised person of the place of custody.
