8. A person to whom temporary arrest has been applied shall have the right to submit a written request for consent to his/her surrender (extradition) with the investigating judge of the court within whose territorial jurisdiction the person is taken to custody, which may indicate the refusal to apply a special rule on the limits of criminal liability. Such a statement shall be subject to immediate consideration by the investigating judge in the manner prescribed by part 6 of this Article, with the participation of the public prosecutor, the person who has filed the request, his/her defence counsel and, where necessary, an interpreter.
