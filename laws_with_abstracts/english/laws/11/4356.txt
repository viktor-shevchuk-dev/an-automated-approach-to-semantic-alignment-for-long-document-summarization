1. Where for the purpose of conducting criminal proceedings it is required that a person who is detained or serves a punishment in the form of imprisonment in the territory of a foreign state and is not convicted of a criminal offence in those particular criminal proceedings is present to give testimony or participate in other procedural activities, a pre-trial investigation agency, a public prosecutor, a judge, or a court of Ukraine carrying out the criminal proceedings shall draw up a request for temporary surrender of that person to Ukraine.
