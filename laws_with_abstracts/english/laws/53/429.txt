Water supply and wastewater disposal enterprises, violated the law in the field of drinking water, drinking water supply and wastewater disposal, which led to diseases, poisoning, long-term or temporary disability, shall be obliged to pay the damages to consumers, to pay the additional costs to the executive authority, implementing the state policy in the field of sanitary legislation, for the realisation of sanitary measures, to pay the costs to the health care institutions for the medical assistance provision to the victims.
