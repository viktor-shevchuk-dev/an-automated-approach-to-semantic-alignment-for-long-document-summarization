A change in the form of ownership or the transfer of ownership of drinking water supply and/or wastewater disposal systems (except for centralised drinking water supply and wastewater disposal facilities) shall be carried out in the manner prescribed by law, provided that this does not lead to a violation of the mode of operation of such systems or their parts, deterioration the quality of drinking water and the provision of services to consumers, including an increase in tariffs for services of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal.
