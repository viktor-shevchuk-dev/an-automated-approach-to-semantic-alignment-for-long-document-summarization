The central executive authority shaping the state policy in the fields of culture and arts shall, no later than 1 October, develop and submit to the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine a draft Action Programme for the Development of Culture for the next calendar year. Other central executive authorities, the competence of which includes the implementation of tasks in the field of culture, in particular, central executive authorities shaping the state policy in the field of education and science, the state policy of economic and social development and others, shall be involved in the development of the Action Programme for the Development of Culture.
