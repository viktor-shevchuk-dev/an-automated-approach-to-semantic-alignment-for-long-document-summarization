3. Ukraine shall guarantee the possibility of establishing cultural institutions of various forms of ownership and types of activities in the field of culture, for example, theatres, concert organisations, philharmonic societies, cultural centres, production agencies, professional art groups, film studios, film and video rental establishments, publishing houses, museums, archives, reserves, art galleries (exhibitions), libraries, club establishments, educational institutions in the field of culture and art, art schools, studios, cinemas, circuses, parks of culture and recreation, architectural, design, advertising, restoration centres and workshops, etc.
