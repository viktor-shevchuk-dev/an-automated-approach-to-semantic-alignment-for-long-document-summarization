The Ukrainian Cultural Foundation shall independently determine the projects to be supported in the manner established by the Law of Ukraine “On the Ukrainian Cultural Foundation”. Such projects may include, in particular, projects related to the organisation and holding of exhibitions, fairs, symposiums, congresses, etc., aimed at popularisation of Ukrainian culture or related to the participation of domestic producers in similar events held in foreign countries if such participation is expedient from the perspective of popularisation of Ukrainian culture abroad and the relevant project has been selected by the Ukrainian Cultural Foundation in the manner prescribed by the Law of Ukraine “On the Ukrainian Cultural Foundation”.
