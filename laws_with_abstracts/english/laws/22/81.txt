The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall annually approve the action programme of the central executive authority shaping the state policy in the fields of culture and arts for the development of culture for the corresponding year (hereinafter referred to as “the Action Programme for the Development of Culture”) that determines the strategic goals and priority tasks of the state cultural policy for a certain calendar year, as well as measures that are planned and implemented by the central executive authority shaping the state policy in the fields of culture and arts to achieve these goals and implement tasks.
