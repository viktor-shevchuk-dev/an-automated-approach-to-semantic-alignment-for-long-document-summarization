The works related to the construction of hydraulic, linear and hydrometric structures, engineering and fortification structures, fences, border signs, border clearings, communications, deepening of the bottom for navigation, mining (except sand, pebbles and gravel in riverbeds and small mountain rivers), clearing riverbeds, canals and the bottom of reservoirs, laying cables, pipelines, other communications, as well as drilling and exploration works may be performed on water fund lands.
