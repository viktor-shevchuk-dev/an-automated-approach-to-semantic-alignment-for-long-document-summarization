The list of water bodies classified as medical, indicating water reserves and their medicinal properties, as well as other favourable conditions for treatment and prevention, shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine at the request of the central executive authority in charge of shaping state health policy, the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of the geological study and the rational use of subsoil, the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of water management.
