Coastal protective belt along the seas, bays and estuaries is part of the sea sanitary protection zone and can be used only for the construction of military and other defence facilities, facilities that produce energy through the use of wind, sun and wave energy, facilities for supply, distribution, transfer (transportation) of energy, as well as sanatoriums, children's health camps and other medical and health facilities with mandatory centralised water supply and sewerage, hydraulic, hydrometric and linear structures.
