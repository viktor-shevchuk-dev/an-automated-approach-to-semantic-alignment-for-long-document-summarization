The rational use of water must be ensured during the placement, design, construction, reconstruction and commissioning of enterprises, buildings and other facilities, as well as during the introduction of new technological processes. With regard to the above-mentioned, the technologies are provided, protecting water from pollution, clogging and depletion, preventing their harmful effects, protecting land from salinisation, flooding or dehydration, as well as contributing to the preservation of natural conditions and landscapes both directly in the area of their location and in the catchment area of water bodies.
