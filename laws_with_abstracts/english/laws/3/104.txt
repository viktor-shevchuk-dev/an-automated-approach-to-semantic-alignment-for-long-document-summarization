Military personnel of military formations established under the laws of Ukraine and law enforcement agencies of Ukraine who served in the territory of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the city of Sevastopol, and their family members shall have the right to privatise duly received residential premises for permanent residence, departmental residential premises, official residential premises and residential premises in dormitories located in the territory of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the city of Sevastopol, where they lived on 1 January 2014.
