Criminal law measures, in the cases provided for in paragraph 1, part one of Article 96-3 hereof, may be applied by the court to an enterprise, institution or organisation, except of state bodies, authorities of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local self-government bodies, organisations duly established by them, which are fully maintained at the expense of state or local budgets, mandatory state social insurance funds, the deposit guarantee fund for individuals, as well as international organisations.
