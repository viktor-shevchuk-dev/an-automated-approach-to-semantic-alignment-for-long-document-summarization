The relevant bank shall issue the certificate of the specified form that is the reason for passing the goods across the customs border of Ukraine to foreign economic activity entity that has opened the previous import deposit. In case of goods transfer by the foreign business entities through the banking and credit institutions of Ukraine and the absence of crossing the customs border of Ukraine by the goods, the certificate specified in this clause shall be the reason to make the settlements with the foreign business entity through the banking and credit institutions of Ukraine or to clear the relevant amount in cash through the customs.
