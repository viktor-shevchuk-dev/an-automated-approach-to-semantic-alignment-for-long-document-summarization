The common customs tariff of Ukraine shall be determined under the relevant law of Ukraine and inter-republican agreements of Ukraine. The common customs tariff of Ukraine shall establish the imposition of duty on the items imported into or exported from the territory of Ukraine on the common customs territory of Ukraine. The common customs tariff rates of Ukraine shall be unified for all foreign economic activity entities irrespective of ownership forms, organisation of economic activity and territorial location, except for the cases provided for by the laws of Ukraine and its international agreements.
