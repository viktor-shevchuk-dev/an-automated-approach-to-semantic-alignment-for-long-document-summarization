Individuals shall be entitled to conduct foreign economic activity from the moment they acquire civil capacity under the laws of Ukraine. Individuals with permanent residence in the territory of Ukraine shall have the specified right if they are registered as entrepreneurs under the Law of Ukraine “On Entrepreneurship”. Individuals without permanent residence in the territory of Ukraine shall have the specified right if they are business entities under the law of the state in which they have permanent residence or nationals of which they are. Legal entities shall be entitled to conduct foreign economic activity under their statutory documents from the moment they acquire the status of a legal entity.
