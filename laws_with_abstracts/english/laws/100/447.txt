As a state, Ukraine shall be fully financially liable before the foreign economic activity entities and foreign business entities for all its actions that contradict the current laws of Ukraine and cause losses (direct, consequential), moral damage to these entities and lead to the loss of profit, as well as for its other actions, including those that regulate foreign economic activity and not directly provided for in this Law, which cause the mentioned losses (damage) and lead to loss of profit, except for cases when such actions are caused by illegal actions of the specified foreign economic activity entities and foreign business entities.
