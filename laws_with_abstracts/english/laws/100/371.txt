Every foreign economic activity entity shall be entitled to get acquainted timely with official texts of laws and other regulatory acts with subsequent information about amendments to them that regulate relations directly or indirectly referred to foreign economic activity. State bodies, agencies and institutions that issue regulatory acts shall be obliged to officially publish them in publicly available mass media of Ukraine, and the specified acts shall not enter into force before their official publication. Every foreign economic activity entity shall be entitled to obtain information directly from the public authorities under the Law of Ukraine “On Access to Public Information”.
