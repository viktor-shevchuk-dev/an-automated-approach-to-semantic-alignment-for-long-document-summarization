Foreign business entities that conduct foreign economic activity in the territory of Ukraine shall be entitled to establish their representative offices in the territory of Ukraine. Branches and representative offices of foreign banks shall be accredited by the National Bank of Ukraine under the Law of Ukraine “On Banks and Banking”. The central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of economic development shall carry out the registration of representative offices of other foreign business entities within sixty working days from the date of submission of the documents for registration by a foreign business entity.
