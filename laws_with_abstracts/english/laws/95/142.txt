Heads of state and municipal healthcare facilities shall be appointed to the position by the authorised executive authority of the administration of the owner of a healthcare facility on a competitive basis by concluding a contract with them for a period of three to five years. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall approve the procedure for holding a competition for the position of the Head of a state or municipal healthcare facility and the procedure for concluding a contract with the Head of a state or municipal healthcare facility, as well as the standard form of such a contract.
