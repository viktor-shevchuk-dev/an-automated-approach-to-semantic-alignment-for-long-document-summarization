Ukrainian authorities apply uniform sanitary-hygienic requirements to the planning and development of settlements; construction and operation of industrial and other facilities; cleaning and disposal of industrial and municipal emissions, wastes and garbage; maintenance and use of residential, industrial and office premises and territories in which they are located; organisation of food and water supply; production, use, storage, transportation and disposal of radioactive, toxic and potent substances; keeping and slaughter of domestic and wild animals, as well as other activities that may threaten the sanitary-epidemiological well-being of territories and settlements.
