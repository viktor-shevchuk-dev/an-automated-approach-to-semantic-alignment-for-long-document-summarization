Tertiary (highly specialised) medical care shall mean medical assistance delivered on the scheduled outpatient and inpatient conditions or in emergency situations that presupposes consultations, diagnostics, treatment of diseases, injuries, poisonings, pathological conditions, management of physiological conditions (during pregnancy and childbirth) using advanced equipment and/or highly specialised complex medical procedures; referring patients based on their medical symptoms for secondary (specialised) medical care or tertiary (highly specialised) medical care in another specialisation.
