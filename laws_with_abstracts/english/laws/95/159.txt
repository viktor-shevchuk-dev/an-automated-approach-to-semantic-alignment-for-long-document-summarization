Contracts for medical service shall be concluded within the budget funds provided to the field of healthcare for the relevant budget period, by virtue of the value and volume of medical care services whose customer is the state or local government authorities. The value of a medical care service shall be calculated based on the cost structure required to deliver such a service in accordance with industry standards in the field of healthcare. The method of calculating the value of medical care service, the list of paid medical care services shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
