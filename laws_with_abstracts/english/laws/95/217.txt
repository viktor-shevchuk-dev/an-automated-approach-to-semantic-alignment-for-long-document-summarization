The state promotes a healthy lifestyle by disseminating scientific knowledge on healthcare, organisation of medical, environmental and physical education, implementation of measures aimed at improving the hygienic culture of the population, creating the necessary conditions and medical control for physical education, sports and tourism, development of a board of medical and sports facilities, dispensaries, recreation centres and other health facilities, to combat habits harmful to human health, as well as the establishment of socio-economic incentive system for people leading a healthy lifestyle.
