In order to ensure the territorial accessibility of quality medical care to the population, a hospital district shall be created. A hospital district shall mean a set of healthcare facilities and individual entrepreneurs registered in the manner prescribed by law and licensed for the right to conduct economic activity in medical practice, providing medical service to the population within the relevant territory. Main healthcare facilities shall be identified within a hospital district. The procedure for establishing hospital districts and the list of main healthcare facilities shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
