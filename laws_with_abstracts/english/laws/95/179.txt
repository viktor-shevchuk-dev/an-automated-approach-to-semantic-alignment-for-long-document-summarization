At the state and municipal healthcare facilities delivering secondary and tertiary medical care, with which the heads of budget funds have concluded contracts for delivering medical service to the population, as well as by decision of the owner of the healthcare facility (the authority entitled by him or her) supervisory boards shall be formed with the members of the public (with their consent) to be compulsorily involved. Supervisory boards may also be formed at the discretion of the owner at other healthcare facilities.
