3. Individuals and legal entities of Ukraine, regardless of ownership, participate in the replenishment of information resources of the national system of scientific and technical information. At the same time, the legislation of Ukraine may establish mandatory standards for their participation (except for that implemented on a contractual basis). The materials and technical documentation provided by enterprises and organisations shall be used by bodies and services under the conditions established by enterprises and organisations that are owners of these materials unless the legislation provides otherwise. The bodies and services of scientific and technical information, to which such information is transmitted, guarantee the protection of intellectual property, trade secrets, and legitimate interests of legal entities and individuals who are creators of information on scientific and technical advances.
