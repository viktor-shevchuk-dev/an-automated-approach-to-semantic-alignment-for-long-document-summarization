In case of conducting the inspection at the location of the market supervision authority, the documents referred to in subclause “d” of clause 1 and subclause “b” of clause 2, part two of this Article shall be provided by a manufacturer within ten working days of receiving a written request from the market supervision authority, sent by letter with a notice of service, except for cases where it is necessary to translate these documents, which must be provided no later than within thirty working days.
