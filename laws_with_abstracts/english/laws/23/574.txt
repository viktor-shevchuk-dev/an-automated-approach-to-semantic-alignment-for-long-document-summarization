The report with the act of inspection, explanations of an individual, including an individual entrepreneur, or the head of a legal entity or a person authorised by him/her (if any), and documents related to the case, shall be handed over to the head of the market supervision authority or his/her deputy no later than the next working day following the day of its drawing-up, and he/she is to consider the case within 15 calendar days from the date of receipt of the report and relevant documents, and to decide on the imposition of a penalty. Each penalty shall be executed as a separate resolution.
