If the market supervision authority has established that the products meet the established requirements, but pose a risk to the life and health of citizens, or to other aspects of protection of public interests, it shall immediately require the relevant business entity to take measures within a specified period to eliminate such risk, withdraw products from circulation and/or recall them from the market, depending on the nature of the identified risk, in accordance with the methodology of restrictive (corrective) measures approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
