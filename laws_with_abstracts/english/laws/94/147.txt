The variety shall be considered suitable for dissemination in Ukraine if it is different, homogeneous and stable, may be used to satisfy the needs of society and is not prohibited for dissemination on the grounds that it is a threat to human life and health, may cause harm to fauna and flora, or threat to environmental protection. The criteria for prohibiting the dissemination of varieties in Ukraine shall be devised by the Competent Authority and approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
