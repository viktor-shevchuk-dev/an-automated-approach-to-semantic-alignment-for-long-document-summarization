1. The right to the dissemination of a variety in Ukraine shall arise from the date of the decision on state registration of intellectual property rights to dissemination of the variety. Concerning the varieties of genus and species not included in the list of genus and species whose varieties are examined for suitability of the variety for dissemination, approved by the Competent Authority, the decision on the state registration of rights to a variety shall be considered a decision on state registration of a variety at the same time.
