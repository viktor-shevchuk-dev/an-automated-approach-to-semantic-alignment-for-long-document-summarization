Invitation to tender for the right to receive the facility on lease shall be carried out by the State Property Fund of Ukraine on the recommendation of the authorised management body within 10 working days from the date of receipt of the submission. Submission of the authorised management body must contain information about the facility offered for lease (name, characteristics, etc.); the period for which the facility is leased; the schedule of annual reduction of state support for partial coverage of production costs for coal mining enterprises.
