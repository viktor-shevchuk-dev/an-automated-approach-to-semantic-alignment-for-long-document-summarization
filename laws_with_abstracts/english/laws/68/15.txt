Article 7. Funding of the activities relating to the preparation for and decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units and the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system, ensuring radiation safety, medical and biophysical monitoring of the personnel of Chornobyl NPP and contracting organisations performing the activities on preparation for and decommissioning and decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units and the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system, social protection of Chornobyl NPP personnel and Slavutych citizens shall be provided from the State Budget of Ukraine, international technical assistance and voluntary contributions from legal entities or individuals and other sources that are not prohibited by law.
