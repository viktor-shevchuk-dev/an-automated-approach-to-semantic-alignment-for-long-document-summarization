Insured persons indicated in parts one and two, Article 22 of the Law of Ukraine “On Mandatory State Social Unemployment Insurance”, employees of Chornobyl NPP who are being retired due to pre-schedule decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP, shall be guaranteed the right to receive unemployment benefits in the amount of 100 per cent of the average salary at the last place of work (calculated for the year) during 120 calendar days, 75 per cent – during the next 30 calendar days, and 50 per cent –during the next 390 calendar days subject to their registration in the public employment service as job seekers within fourteen calendar days after dismissal.
