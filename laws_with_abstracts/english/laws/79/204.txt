The requirement of part one of this Article for the possession of a licence for import of medicinal products and their expiry dates shall not apply to imports of medicinal products that are purchased and imported into Ukraine by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare according to the list approved by the Cabinet of Ministers, subject to confirmation of the intended purpose of the import of such medicinal products into the territory of Ukraine under the procedure established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. The import of such medicinal products into the territory of Ukraine shall be conducted on the availability of a batch quality certificate issued by the manufacturer. In the absence of a licence to conduct business activities on the import of medicinal products in an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare, compliance with the legal requirements to license business activities during import into Ukraine, storage, quality control, issuance of authorisation for the release (sale) of a batch of medicinal products purchased by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare shall be ensured by engaging the business entities licensed to conduct business activities in the import of medicinal products in compliance with the legislation in the field of public procurements. The expiry date of such medicinal products shall be at least 70 per cent of the expiry date of the medicinal product specified by the manufacturer. By a separate decision of the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare, in the cases determined by it, the expiry date of medicinal products, which are purchased by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of health according to the list approved the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and subject to confirmation of the intended purpose of the import of such medicinal products, may be reduced.
