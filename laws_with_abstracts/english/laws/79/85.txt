For state registration of medicinal products based on or related to the intellectual property objects on which a patent has been issued under the laws of Ukraine, the applicant shall submit a duly certified copy of the patent or licence, which permits manufacturing and sale of the registered medicinal product, as well as a document confirming the patent maintenance in Ukraine. Applicants shall submit a statement indicating that the third-party rights protected by the patent or transferred under the licence are not infringed in connection with medicinal product registration.
