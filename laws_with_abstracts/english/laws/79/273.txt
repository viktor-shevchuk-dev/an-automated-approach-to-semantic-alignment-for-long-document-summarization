It shall be allowed to advertise the medicinal products that are available without a doctor's prescription and not included in the List of Medicinal Products Prohibited for Advertising. The List of Medicinal Products Prohibited for Advertising shall be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of healthcare. Advertising requirements for such medicinal products shall be established by the Law of Ukraine “On Advertising”. The criteria used to determine the drugs, the advertising of which is prohibited, shall be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of healthcare according to the requirements of the law. The decision to classify a medicinal product as medicinal products, the advertising of which is prohibited, shall be made during the state registration (re-registration) of the medicinal product with the entry of the relevant information into the State Register of Medicines of Ukraine. It shall be prohibited to advertise medicinal products that are used and dispensed only on a doctor's prescription as well as those included in the list of medicinal products prohibited for advertising.
