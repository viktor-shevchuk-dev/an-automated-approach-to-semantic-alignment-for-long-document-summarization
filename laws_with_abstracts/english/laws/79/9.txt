falsified medicinal product is a medicinal product that was deliberately labelled not identically (inappropriately) to the data (one or more) on a medicinal product with the appropriate name entered in the State Register of Medicinal Products of Ukraine, as well as a medicinal product which was deliberately counterfeited in another way and does not correspond to the data (one or more), including concerning the composition, specified on the medicinal product with the appropriate name entered in the State Register of Medicinal Products of Ukraine.
