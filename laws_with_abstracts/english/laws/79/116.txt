The medicinal product registered according to part four of this Article with labelling, instruction for medical use and summary of product characteristics in the original language (a language other than the state one) may be in circulation on the territory of Ukraine solely for free supply to the structural health care subdivisions of Oblast State Administrations, Kyiv and Sevastopol City State Administrations or business entities, licensed to conduct business activities in medical practice, based on the results of procurements using the state budget funds, conducted by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare for the implementation of programmes and centralised health protection measures.
