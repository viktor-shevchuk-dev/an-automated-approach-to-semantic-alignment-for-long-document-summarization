It shall be allowed to import into the customs territory of Ukraine for placement under the customs regime of a customs warehouse with the subsequent re-export only those medicinal products, which are registered in the manufacturer's country, accompanied by a quality certificate and the import of which into the customs territory of Ukraine does not contradict the Provisions of the Council of Europe Convention on the Counterfeiting of Medical Products and the Similar Crimes involving Threats to Public Health.
