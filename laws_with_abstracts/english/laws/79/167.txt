In the case of import into the territory of Ukraine of an unregistered medicinal product for supply based on the results of procurements conducted by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare for the implementation of programmes and centralised health protection measures, or a procurement conducted by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare using the local budget funds according to the provisions of Article 17 of this Law, labelling, as well as instruction for medical use/summary of characteristics of such a medicinal product may be in the original language (a language other than the state one) given that the supplier provides each package of the medicinal product imported into the territory of Ukraine with a copy, approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare, translations of the label text and instruction for medical use / summary of product characteristics in the state language and a notice issued by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare according to the procedure established by the law.
