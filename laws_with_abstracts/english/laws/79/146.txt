6. After the state registration of vaccines or other medical immunobiological preparations for the specific prevention of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) under the obligation for emergency medical use, the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare, by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, has the right to take obligations on behalf of Ukraine related to the procurement, use of vaccines or other medical immunobiological preparations for the specific prevention of coronavirus disease (COVID-19), the state registration of which has been conducted according to the requirements of this Article, including the waiver of sovereign immunity in regard to such obligations, as well as release of liability of the manufacturer and/or the registration certificate holder, including their employees, medical personnel, if the use of such vaccines or other medical immunobiological preparations was conducted according to the instructions approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare and other individuals involved in the implementation of measures related to vaccination, for any consequences caused by the use of such vaccines or other medical immunobiological preparations for the specific prevention of coronavirus disease (COVID-19).
