The reason for issuing a licence is the existence of the material and technical resources, qualified personnel, whose conformity to the established requirements and characteristics stated in the documents submitted by the applicant for obtaining the licence is subject to mandatory verification before issuing a licence within the time limits provided for the issuance of a licence, at the place of activity of the licensing authority or its territorial divisions according to the procedure established by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of healthcare.
