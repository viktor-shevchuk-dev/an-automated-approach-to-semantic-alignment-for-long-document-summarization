A business entity, which is licensed to conduct business activities in the retail trade in medicinal products, shall be liable to the final consumer for maintaining the medicinal product quality and compliance with the storage conditions specified by the manufacturer, including during the delivery of the medicinal product sold remotely to the final consumer, regardless of whether it delivers by the own delivery service or involves other business entities for this, and also bears administrative and criminal liability for violation of the rules for dispensing and delivering medicinal products under the law. Business entities that are involved in the delivery of medicinal products to the final consumer on a contractual basis shall be administratively and criminally liable for the violation of applicable requirements for the delivery of medicinal products under the law.
