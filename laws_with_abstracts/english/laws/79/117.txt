The medicinal product registered according to part five of this Article with labelling and instruction for medical use of the medicinal product that meet the requirements of parts one, two, three and five of Article 12 of this Law may be in circulation on the territory of Ukraine for the implementation of programmes and centralised health protection measures based on the results of procurements using the state budget funds, conducted by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare, as well as without restrictions on circulation according to the general requirements provided for by this Law.
