Under the Constitution of Ukraine, this Law governs relations related to freedom of movement and free choice of place of residence in Ukraine, guaranteed by the Constitution of Ukraine and enshrined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights, Convention for the Protection of Human Rights and Fundamental Freedoms and protocols thereto, other international treaties of Ukraine, as well as determines the procedure for exercising the freedom of movement and free choice of place of residence and establishes cases of their restriction.
