Transmission (retransmission) of advertising contained in programmes and broadcastings of foreign TV and radio companies, that fall under the jurisdiction of the European Union Member States or the states that ratified the European Convention on Transfrontier Television, transmitted (retransmitted) to the territory of Ukraine shall be permitted only in case it was paid to a legal entity of Ukraine for such transmission (retransmission), regardless of the means of such transmission (retransmission).
