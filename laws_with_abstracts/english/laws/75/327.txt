3. Advertising of financial services related to the attraction of people's funds or persons that provide such services shall be permitted given that the information on the person entered the State Register of Financial Institutions or the State Register of Banks or there is a relevant permit issued under laws regulating specific markets of financial services or a licence to carry out activities on the provision of the relative financial service. Such advertising, except for advertising on radio, shall contain the number assigned by the State Register of Financial Institutions or the State Register of Banks, number of the specified permit or licence, date of issue and name of the authority that issued the permit or licence.
