The legislation on seaports shall be based on the Constitution of Ukraine and shall include this Law, the Merchant Shipping Code of Ukraine, the Civil Code of Ukraine, the Economic Code of Ukraine, the Budget Code of Ukraine, the Tax Code of Ukraine, the Land Code of Ukraine, the Water Code of Ukraine, the laws of Ukraine “On Transport” and “On Natural Monopolies”, as well as other regulatory acts adopted according to the aforesaid Codes and Laws.
