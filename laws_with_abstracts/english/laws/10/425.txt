Underwater hydraulic structures (canals, operating waters of berths, etc.) located within seaport waters, which were built at the expense of private investments before the enactment hereof and included in the Register of Hydraulic Structures in the manner prescribed by law, shall, with the consent of owners of such hydraulic structures, be subject to transfer on a compensatory basis to the state ownership represented by the Ukrainian Sea Ports Authority. The value of such hydraulic structures shall be determined by an independent evaluator. Compensation for the value of hydraulic structures shall be provided under Article 27 hereof.
