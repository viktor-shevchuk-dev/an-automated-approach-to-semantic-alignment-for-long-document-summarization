4. The procedure for maintaining the Register of Hydraulic Structures shall be established by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport. Prior to the introduction of the information on berths into the Register of Hydraulic Structures and the Register of Seaports of Ukraine, other than berths recorded in the Register of Hydraulic Structures in the manner prescribed by law before the enactment of this Law, the economic activity on such berths shall be prohibited.
