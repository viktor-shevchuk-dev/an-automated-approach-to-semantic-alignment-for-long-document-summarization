For electric power facilities having a “green” tariff, after reconstruction, technical re-equipment, overhaul with increased installed capacity, the “green” tariff shall be calculated using the "green" tariff coefficient, defined by Article 9-1 of the Law of Ukraine “On Alternative Energy Sources” on the date when such power facility has been commissioned, including the power plant construction stages (start-up complex) in operation after reconstruction, technical re-equipment, overhaul, but not exceeding the coefficient used in establishing a “green” tariff for the relevant electric power facility, including the power plant construction stages (start-up complex).
