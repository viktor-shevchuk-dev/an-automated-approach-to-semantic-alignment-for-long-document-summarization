The “green” tariff for electricity produced by consumers, including by energy cooperatives, from geothermal energy by generating installations with an installed capacity not exceeding 150 kW, shall be set at the level of the retail tariff for consumers of the second voltage class as of January 2009, multiplied by the factor of “green” tariff for electricity produced by consumers, including energy cooperatives, from geothermal energy by generating installations, the installed capacity of which does not exceed 150 kW.
