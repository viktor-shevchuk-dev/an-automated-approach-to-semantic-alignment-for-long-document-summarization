The “green” tariff shall be set by the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission on electricity generated at electric power facilities, including the commissioned construction stages of power plants (start-up facilities), generating installations of private households, consumers, including energy cooperatives, from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used). The “green” tariff provided for in this Article shall be established by the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission taking into account the features specified in Article 9-4 of this Law.
