The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, at the request of the central executive authority shaping and implementing state policy in the field of the electric energy sector, annually, no later than December 1, shall establish an annual support quota and auction schedule for next year, as well as set the indicative annual support quotas for four years following the year for which the annual support quota is set. Proposals on the scope of annual support quotas shall be prepared by the central executive authority shaping and implementing state policy in the field of efficient use of fuel and energy resources, energy saving, renewable energy sources and alternative fuels, as well as by the transmission system operator taking into account international obligations of Ukraine regarding the renewable energy development, Energy Strategy of Ukraine, report on the assessment of compliance (sufficiency) of generating capacities and the plan for transmission system development, the state of implementation of the projects for the construction of renewable energy facilities.
