The state shall guarantee that for the period from 1 July 2020 to 31 December 2029 for business entities for which the “green” tariff is set or will be set under part three of this article, the “green” tariff will not be changed or cancelled, and decreasing coefficients will not be changed or applied in any other way, which may lead to losses and/or damages, and/or non-receipt of legitimately expected revenues by such business entities.
