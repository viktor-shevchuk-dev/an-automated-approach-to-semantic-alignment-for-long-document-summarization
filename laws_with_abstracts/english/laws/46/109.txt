Confirmation of the fact and date of the electric power facility commissioning, including the power plant construction stages (start-up complex), generating installations of consumers, including energy cooperatives, with the installed capacity not exceeding 150 kW, producing electricity from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and in case of using hydro energy, only by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants), shall be a certificate issued by the authorised body certifying compliance with the completed project design documentation and confirming its readiness for operation, or a declaration on the readiness of the facility for operation registered under the legislation.
