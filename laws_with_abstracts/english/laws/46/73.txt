The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine may allocate funds from the state budget for the financial support of the guaranteed buyer to pay for electricity produced from alternative sources, pursuant to the budget requests of the central executive authority shaping and implementing state policy in the field of the electric energy sector, by virtue of the calculations provided for by the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission in the amount of not less than 20 per cent of the forecast output of marketable electricity products from alternative sources for the year.
