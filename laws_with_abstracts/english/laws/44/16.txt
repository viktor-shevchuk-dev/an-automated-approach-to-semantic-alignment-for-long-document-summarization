The criminal executive legislation, the execution and serving of sentences are based on the principles of the inevitability of the execution and serving of sentences, legality, justice, humanism, democracy, equality of convicts before the law, respect for human rights and freedoms, the mutual responsibility of the state and a convict, the differentiation and individualisation of the execution of sentences, the rational use of coercive measures and the encouragement of law-abiding behaviour, the combination of a sentence with reformation influence, public participation in the activities of penitentiary authorities and institutions in cases provided for by law.
