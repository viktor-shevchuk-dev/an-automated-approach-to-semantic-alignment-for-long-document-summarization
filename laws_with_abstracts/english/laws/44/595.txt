Under the procedure established by the Code of Criminal Procedure of Ukraine, a convict may be temporarily retained in a pre-trial detention facility and transferred from a detention facility, correctional centre, disciplinary battalion, correctional facility or juvenile correctional facility to a pre-trial detention facility, when it is necessary to carry out investigative actions in criminal proceedings for a criminal offence committed by another person or by the same person for which they have not been convicted, or in connection with court proceedings.
