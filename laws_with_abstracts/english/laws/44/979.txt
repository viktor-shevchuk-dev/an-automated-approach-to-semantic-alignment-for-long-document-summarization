5. A person serving a sentence of imprisonment shall have the right to use the services of a lawyer or a legal professional of their choice during the preparation for the meeting of the disciplinary committee, who will represent their interests during the meeting of the committee. If a person serving a sentence of imprisonment does not have access to a lawyer or a legal professional, the administration of the penitentiary institution shall be obliged to provide them with the opportunity to seek legal aid from the legal aid entities.
