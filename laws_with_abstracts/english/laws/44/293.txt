The period of serving a sentence shall not count the period of illness caused by alcohol, narcotic intoxication or the influence of drugs or other toxic substances or caused by actions related thereto, a gross violation of safety rules, intentional self-inflicted bodily injuries; the period of serving an administrative penalty of administrative detention or correctional labour, as well as remand in custody in other criminal proceedings during serving a sentence in cases when the guilt of committing a criminal offence is proved under the procedure established by law.
