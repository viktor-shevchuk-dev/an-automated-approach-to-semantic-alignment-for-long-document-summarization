3. In the case of a convict who may be released on parole or commuted the unserved portion of the sentence under Articles 81, 82 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine, the penitentiary authority or institution shall, within one month, submit a motion to the court under the procedure established by the criminal procedure legislation. After the convict has served the portion of the sentence specified in the Criminal Code of Ukraine, the administration of the penitentiary authority or institution shall be obliged to consider the possibility of granting them to release on parole from serving the sentence or commuting the unserved portion of the sentence within one month
