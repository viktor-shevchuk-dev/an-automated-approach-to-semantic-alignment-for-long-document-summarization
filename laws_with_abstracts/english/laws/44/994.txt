1. Convicts sentenced to imprisonment shall be financially liable for pecuniary losses caused to the state while serving their sentence in accordance with legislation. Convicts shall also reimburse the losses caused to the facility and the additional costs associated with terminating the escape of a convict, providing treatment to a convict who intentionally caused themselves bodily harm. If the losses were caused in the course of work, convicts shall be financially liable in the amount and on the grounds stipulated by the labour legislation. In other cases, the losses shall be reimbursed in the amount and on the grounds set out in civil law.
