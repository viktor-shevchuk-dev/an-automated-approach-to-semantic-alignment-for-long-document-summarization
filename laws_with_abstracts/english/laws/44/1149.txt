2. After serving the sentence imposed by the court, convicts sentenced to arrest, restriction of freedom or imprisonment shall be released on the first half of the last day of the sentence. If the sentence ends on a weekend or public holiday, the convict shall be released on the day before the weekend or public holiday. When periods are calculated in months, the sentence shall expire on the relevant date of the last month or, when that month does have a relevant date, on the last day of that month.
