Convicts shall be granted, including during their stay in the in-patient health care facilities, the right to telephone calls (including through mobile communication networks) without limitation of their number under the supervision of the administration, as well as to use the internet. These calls shall be paid for out of the convicts’ own funds. Telephone calls between convicts in prison facilities shall be prohibited. Telephone calls and the use of the internet shall be paid for out of the convicts’ own funds.
