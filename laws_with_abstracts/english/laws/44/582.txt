Convicts sentenced to imprisonment shall be committed for serving the sentences no later than ten days from the date the sentence becomes legally effective or from the date an order for execution of the sentence that has become legally effective is received from the court. During this period the convict shall have the right to a short visit from their members of the immediate family. The procedure for committing convicts to correctional facilities and juvenile correctional facilities shall be determined by the regulations of the Ministry of Justice of Ukraine.
