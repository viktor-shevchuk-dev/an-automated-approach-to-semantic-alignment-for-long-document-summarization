The proposals on strategic priority directions, their forecast and analytical justification shall be prepared within the state targeted programmes for forecasting the scientific, technical and innovative development of Ukraine. Such programmes shall be developed in accordance with the laws of Ukraine “On Priority Branches of Science and Technology Development” and “On State Targeted Programmes”. These proposals shall be prepared with the involvement of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, national specialised academies of sciences of Ukraine, higher education institutions and research institutes.
