Liability for offences in the field of road safety recorded automatically, or violation of the rules for stopping, parking vehicles, recorded by photographing (videorecording), is borne by an individual or head of the legal entity for which the vehicle is registered; and if information about a proper user of the relevant vehicle is entered into the Unified State Register of Vehicles – a proper user of the vehicle; and if there is no information about the head of the legal entity for which the vehicle is registered in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities, Individual Entrepreneurs and Public Organisations at the time of the request, – by the person acting as the head of such legal entity.
