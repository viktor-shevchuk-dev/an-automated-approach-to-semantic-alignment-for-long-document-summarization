Circumstances, which can serve as the ground for property responsibility of carriers, senders and recipients of cargo, luggage, and passengers, shall be certified by acts. Demands of shippers, consignees, passengers to the carriers in relation to violation of rights and legal interests are considered according to the claim and lawsuit procedure. The procedure for and terms of drafting acts, presentation and consideration of claims and lawsuits are determined by the Railway Statute of Ukraine in accordance with the current legislation of Ukraine.
