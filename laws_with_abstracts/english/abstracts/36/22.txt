International agreements of Ukraine shall be ratified by adoption of law on ratification which integral part is the text of international agreement. On the basis of law, signed and officially published by the President of Ukraine, the Speaker of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine shall sign the instrument of ratification, which shall be certified by the signature of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, if the agreement provides for exchange of instruments of ratification.
