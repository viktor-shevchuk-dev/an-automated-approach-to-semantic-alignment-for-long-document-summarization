The private law of a natural person shall be the law of the state in which s/he is a citizen. If a natural person is a citizen of two or more states, his/her private law shall be the law of that state with which the person has closer relation, in particular, has a permanent place of residence or carries out basic activity. The private law of a stateless person shall be the law of the state where the person has the place of residence, or in case of its absence â€“ the place of stay. The private law of a refugee shall be the law of the state where s/he stays. The private law of a legal entity shall be the law of the state of its location. The location of a legal entity shall be the state in which it is registered or established in other way according to the law of this state.
