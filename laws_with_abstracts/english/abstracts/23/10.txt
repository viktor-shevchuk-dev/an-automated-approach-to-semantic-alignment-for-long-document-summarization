According to Article 32 of the Law, product withdrawal from circulation or product recall is used in respect of products that are offered on the market or already supplied to the consumers (users), respectively. Product withdrawal from circulation means that the distributors return all units of the appropriate product held by them to the product manufacturer, and stop any demonstration or offering of such products to consumers (users). Product recall means returning to the manufacturers the units of appropriate product that had already been supplied to consumers (users).
