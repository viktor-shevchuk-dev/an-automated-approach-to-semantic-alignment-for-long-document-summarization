When constructing co-generational units, including the cases of construction on the basis of operating energy objects in industry or communal heating supply, within attraction of finances of an investor, such investor may acquire rights of an owner (co-owner) of a co-generational unit on grounds, defined by an investment agreement (Article 7 of the Law). Authorized persons of an owner (co-owner), an investor of a co-generational unit receive the right for access to the territory, premises and equipment (machinery) of energy objects for construction and technical service of a co-generational unit on the grounds, defined by an investment agreement.
