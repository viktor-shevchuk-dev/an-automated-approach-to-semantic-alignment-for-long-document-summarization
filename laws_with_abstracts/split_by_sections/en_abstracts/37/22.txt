Timely suspension of or restriction on the provision of transport services can be applied in the instances specified by the Traffic Rules, the Rules for Operating a Tram and a Trolleybus and the Rules for Technical Maintenance of the Subway. Under the conditions of the state of emergency or the state of martial law, the provision of transport services can be restricted or suspended, while the network of municipal electric transport can be reduced. In the instance of emergency situations, natural calamities, accidents, and catastrophes that have resulted in disturbances in the operation of municipal electric transport, carriers shall take immediate steps to eliminate their consequences.
