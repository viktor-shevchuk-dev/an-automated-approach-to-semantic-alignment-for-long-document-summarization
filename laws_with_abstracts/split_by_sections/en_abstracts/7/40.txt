According to Article 65 of the Law, the asset management company fee (except for the asset management company of a venture fund) is calculated as a percentage of value of net assets. The fee of the asset management company of a venture fund is calculated as a percentage of value of net assets and/or the increase in value of net assets. The maximum amount of the asset management fee (including the bonus), the procedure for its accrual and payment are established by the Commission. The asset management fee is paid with monetary funds.
