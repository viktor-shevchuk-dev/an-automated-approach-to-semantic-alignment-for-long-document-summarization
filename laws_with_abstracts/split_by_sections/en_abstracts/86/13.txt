Payments for the use of bowels may be levied as one-time payment and (or) regular payments which are determined on the basis of respective ecological and economic calculations. The norms of payment for the use of bowels and the procedure for its levy shall be set by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Payment for the use of bowels does not exempt the users from other obligatory payments that are envisaged by the legislative acts of Ukraine. Payment for the use of bowels may be performed in money, as well as au naturel.
