Pursuant to the Law, ecological network is a unified territorial system created with the purpose of improvement of conditions for forming and renewal of environment, increase of natural resource potential of Ukrainian territory, saving landscape and biological diversity, places of settlement and growth of valuable kinds of flora and fauna, genetic fund, ways of migration of animals through joining territories and objects of nature reserve fund, as well as other territories which are especially valuable for environmental protection and in accordance with laws and international obligations of Ukraine are subject to special protection.
