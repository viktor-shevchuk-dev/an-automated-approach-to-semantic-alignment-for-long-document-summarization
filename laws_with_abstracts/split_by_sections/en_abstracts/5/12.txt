According to Article 15 of the Law, extraction (catching) of aquatic biological resources for the purposes of aquaculture is carried out throughout the fishing season, and, if necessary, during the breeding season, according to the legislation. Spawners and rearing groups of aquatic biological resources for the purposes of aquaculture are caught in water bodies (parts thereof) using the field and research fishing instruments, provided appropriate quotas and permits for extraction of aquatic biological resources.
