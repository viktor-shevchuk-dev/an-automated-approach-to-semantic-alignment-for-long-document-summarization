New brands of wine products shall be approved by the head of the subject of economic activity on the basis of the conclusion of the Central Branch Taste Panel of the wine industry and/or the taste panel of the relevant scientific institution, and/or the taste panel of the branch public union acting in accordance with the procedure approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping state farm policy, policy in the field of agriculture. The right to approve new brands of still wines and vintage cognacs of Ukraine shall be granted to enterprises and organisations that have their own stable raw material base and appropriate capacity for their production.
