Introduction of any quantitative regulation measures on mixing, processing or use of rectified grape ethyl alcohol, rectified fruit ethyl alcohol, grape alcohol distillate and grain distillate of foreign (non-Ukrainian) origin in accordance with the Customs Code of Ukraine, in certain quantities or proportions in the production of alcoholic beverages shall be carried out exclusively under the laws of Ukraine, taking into account the norms of international treaties of Ukraine ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.
