Central executive authority implementing the state policy in the sphere of physical culture and sport, in competition and according to the procedure approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, selects all-Ukrainian public association of physical culture and sport orientation of veterans of physical culture and sport that will represent Ukraine in relevant international organisations, at Global and European Games of veterans of physical culture and sport and in other international sports competitions, if other is not provisioned by the requirements of relevant international organisations and/or international agreements of Ukraine.
