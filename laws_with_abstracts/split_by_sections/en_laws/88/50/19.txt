Purchase, storage, transporting and use of sport weapons, ammunition for such weapons, operation of shooting ranges, shooting lanes and stands are organised in accordance with legislation by physical culture and sport entities that develop kinds of sport that are recognised in Ukraine, where rules of such sports provision use of firearms weapon (except combat firearms weapon) or pneumatic weapon, small arms weapon, blank fire-only fitted weapon and edged weapon. Tracking and certification of sport weapons are performed according to the procedure defined by the legislation.
