According to clause “g” of Article XX “General Exclusions” of General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT 1947), local consumption of domestic rough timber is restricted to 25 million cubic metres per year for the period of ban on the export of rough timber outside the customs territory of Ukraine in the export customs regime (code 4403 according to UKT ZED) as envisaged by Article 2 -1 of this Law.
