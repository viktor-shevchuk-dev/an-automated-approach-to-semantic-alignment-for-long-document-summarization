Obtaining a special permit for the use of the oil and gas potential subsoil area located within the territorial sea or the Exclusive (marine) Economic Zone of Ukraine shall mean the simultaneous acquisition of the right to use the offshore area within this area for work provided for by the special permit for use of oil and gas potential subsoil. If it is impossible to place appropriate technological facilities for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil within the offshore area of the oil and gas potential subsoil area, these facilities shall be located in the offshore area outside the oil and gas subsoil potential area. The location of any technological facilities for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil during the entire period of validity of the special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil within the territorial sea and the Exclusive (marine) Economic Zone of Ukraine shall be approved by central government bodies, implementing public policy in the field of state border security and protection of state sovereignty of Ukraine in its Exclusive (marine) Economic Zone, marine and river transport safety, fisheries, and the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the field of environmental protection.
