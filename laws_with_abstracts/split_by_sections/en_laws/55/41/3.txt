The functions of dispatch (operational and technological) management of the Unified Gas Transmission System of Ukraine shall be performed by the operator of the Unified Gas Transmission System of Ukraine, which shall be determined by the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the oil and gas complex and in cases provided for in law - by the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the oil and gas complex, with the consent of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.
