If the price at which crude oil and natural-gas condensate are sold at stock auctions is lower than the opening price, determined in accordance with part two of this Article, business entities, defined in part one of this Article shall pay a fine to the state budget in the amount of 100 per cent of the difference between the cost calculated from the opening price and actual sales prices. The collection of such fines shall be carried out by the tax authorities at the request of the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the oil and gas complex.
