Such a request may be submitted within 2 months from the date of termination of the circumstances that caused non-compliance with the time frame of 31 months established by Part 2 of this Article, or within 12 months from its expiration, whichever comes first. In this case, as of the date of submitting the request, the applicant shall perform all actions on the application provided by this Law, which were to be performed on that date.
