9. A holder of the patent for an invention, except for a secret invention (secret utility model), shall have the right to submit to the NIPO for the official publication a statement of the willingness to grant any person a permit to use the patented invention (utility model). In this case, the annual fee for maintaining the validity of intellectual property rights to an invention (utility model) shall be reduced by 50 per cent starting from the year, which follows the year of the publication of such statement.
