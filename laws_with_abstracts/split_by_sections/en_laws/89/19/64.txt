The applicant may submit the said application and pay the fee for the qualification expert appraisal within 3 years of the application submission date, and another person may do it after the publication of information about the application for the invention, but not later than 3 years of the application submission date. In this case, the other person does not participate in resolving issues related to the application. Only the expert appraisal conclusion on the application approved by NIPO is sent to him/her.
