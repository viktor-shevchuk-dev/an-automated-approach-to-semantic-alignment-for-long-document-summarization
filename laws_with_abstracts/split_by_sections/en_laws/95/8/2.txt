Application of precautionary measures to a person against whom the application of coercive medical measures is envisaged or the issue of their application has been resolved, coercive medical measures against persons who have committed socially dangerous acts, compulsory treatment of persons who have committed criminal offences and have a disease dangerous for the health of other persons, diminution of rights of other citizens in the form of compulsory treatment, involuntary hospitalisation or due to the establishment of quarantine shall be allowed solely on the grounds and in the manner prescribed by law.
