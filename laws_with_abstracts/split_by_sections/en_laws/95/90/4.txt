In the interests of treatment of a person with coronavirus disease (COVID-19) confirmed by laboratory testing, the methods of treatment and/or prevention of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) may also be applied if such methods of prevention and/or treatment have been officially approved by the authority of the United States of America, Member States of the European Union, Great Britain, the Swiss Confederation, Japan, Australia, Canada, the People's Republic of China, the State of Israel for use in the treatment and/or prevention of coronavirus (COVID-19) in the relevant country or within the territory of the European Union pursuant to the decision of central executive authority shaping and implementing state policies in the field of healthcare, subject to obtaining consent for medical intervention in accordance with these Fundamentals.
