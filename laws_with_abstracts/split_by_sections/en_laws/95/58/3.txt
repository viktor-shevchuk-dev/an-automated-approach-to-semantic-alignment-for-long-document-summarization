Brain death is defined by the complete and irreversible cessation of all its functions, which is registered with a functional heart and artificial lung ventilation. The council of physicians at the healthcare facility, where the patient has been admitted shall diagnose the human brain death based on diagnostic criteria for human brain death. Upon confirmation of the human brain death, the council of physicians shall draw up an act to be signed by all council members and attached to the patient's medical records. Physicians involved in the removal of anatomical materials and their transplantation, as well as the transplant coordinator of the healthcare facility, may not participate in the medical council.
