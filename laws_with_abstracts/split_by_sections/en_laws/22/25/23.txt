15. The chairperson of the governing body shall be obliged to appoint the winner of the competition as the head of a state or communal cultural institution not later than two months after the competition is announced. The basis for issuing an order to appoint the head of a state or communal cultural institution shall be the signing of a contract. The terms and conditions of the contract shall be published on the official website of the governing body no later than the day following its signing.
