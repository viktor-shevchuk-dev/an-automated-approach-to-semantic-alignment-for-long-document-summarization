If the public partner decides so, the state, utility enterprise, institution, organisation, business association, 100 per cent of shares (stakes) of which belong to the state, territorial community, or the Autonomous Republic of Crimea may assist the public partner in the agreement concluded under the public-private partnership, and they can be entrusted with the performance of certain obligations in the agreement concluded under the public-private partnership. A public partner bears subsidiary liability for the obligations of such an enterprise, institution, organisation, or business entity in accordance with the agreement concluded under the public-private partnership.
