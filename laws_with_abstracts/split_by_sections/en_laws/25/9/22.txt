The conclusion on the results of the analysis of the public-private partnership implementation efficiency regarding public property is subject to coordination with the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of public-private partnership, and other authorities defined in the Procedure for the analysis of public-private partnership implementation efficiency approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, government authorities. The conclusion based on the results of the analysis of the public-private partnership implementation efficiency on communal property objects and property owned by the Autonomous Republic of Crimea shall be agreed with the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of public-private partnership, if state support is provided for such conclusion through funding from the state budget.
