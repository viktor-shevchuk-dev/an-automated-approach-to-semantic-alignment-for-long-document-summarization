Transfer of the public-private partnership object to the private partner, including its further reconstruction, restoration, overhaul, and technical re-equipment performed by the private partner, shall not involve conveyance of the title to this object to the private partner and shall not terminate the right of state or communal ownership for such object. Such objects shall be subject to their return to the public partner after the expiry of the appropriate agreement in compliance with the procedure stipulated by the agreement concluded under the public-private partnership
