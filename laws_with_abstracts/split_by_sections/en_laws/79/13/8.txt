Labellings, instructions for medical use/summaries of characteristics of the medicinal products purchased by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare, the state registration of which is provided under Article 9-1 of this Law or the registration certificate validity period of which is renewed under this Law, may be given in the original language (a language other than the state one), if the supplier provides each package of the medicinal product imported into the territory of Ukraine with a copy of translations of the label text and instruction for medical use/summary characteristics of the medicinal product in the state language, signed by the applicant or his authorised representative; confirmation of the intended purpose of such import, provided under the procedure established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
