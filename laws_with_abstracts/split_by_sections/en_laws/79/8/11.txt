Information provided in an application for state registration of a medicinal product and attachments thereto (registration information) under provisions of this Law and other regulatory acts of Ukraine, shall be subject to state protection against disclosure and unfair commercial use. The central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare shall be obliged to protect such information against disclosure and to prevent unfair commercial use of such information, as well as to provide free access to all the results of preclinical studies and clinical trials of medicinal products (reports on preclinical studies and reports on clinical trials, drawn up in the form established by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of healthcare), which is public information.
