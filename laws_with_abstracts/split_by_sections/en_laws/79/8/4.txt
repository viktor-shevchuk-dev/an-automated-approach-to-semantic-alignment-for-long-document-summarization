The application shall be accompanied by: materials of the registration dossier, the content and volume requirements of which shall be established by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare; materials on methods of quality control of medicinal product; package label text; a document confirming the payment of the registration fee. The application for state registration of a medicinal product, registered by the competent authority of the United States of America, the Swiss Confederation, Japan, Australia, Canada, the centrally authorised medicinal product, registered by the competent authority of the European Union and used on the territory of these countries or European Union Member States, shall be accompanied by materials of registration dossier; materials on methods of quality control of medicinal product; a graphic image of the medicinal product package design and label text of the primary and secondary (if any) packages, written in language for labelling medicinal products under the requirements of this Law; instruction for medical use of the medicinal product, written in the language under the requirements of this Law; and a document confirming the registration fee payment.
