If the state registration of a medicinal product is performed for procurement, which is carried out by an individual authorised to procure in the field of healthcare, such a medicinal product may be registered according to the state registration procedures specified in this Article. The state registration of such a medicinal product may be conducted according to the procedure provided for in part four of this Article or according to the procedure provided for in part five of this Article at the applicant's option. The applicant's right to register the relevant medicinal product under any of the procedures provided for in this Article shall not deprive them of the right to register the relevant medicinal product under any other procedure for the state registration of a medicinal product specified in Article 9 of this Law according to the requirements of the legislation.
