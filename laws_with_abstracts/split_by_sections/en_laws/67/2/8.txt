4. Subjects of foreign economic activity of Ukraine who have conducted the export or import of works or services under a barter agreement shall be obliged to notify the customs authorities (if goods are imported or exported under such an agreement) or tax authorities (if works or services are imported or exported under such an agreement) of the fact of exporting goods (works, services) within five working days from the date of signing an act or other document certifying the performance of works or services. Failure to submit or late submission of such information entails the accrual of default interest in the amount of one per cent of the value of exported goods (works, services) per each day of delay. The total amount of the accrued default interest shall not exceed the value of exported goods (works, services).
