1. A telecommunications operator shall collect charitable donations using a charity telecommunication message among subscribers on the basis of an agreement on public fundraising concluded with a non-profit organisation, as well as a charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions) or a territorial community. In this case, a power of attorney shall not be required to be issued to the operator from a non-profit organisation, as well as a charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions), or a territorial community.
