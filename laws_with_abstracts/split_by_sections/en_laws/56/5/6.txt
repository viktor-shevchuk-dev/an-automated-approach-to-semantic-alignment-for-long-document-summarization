5. Financial institutions shall have the right to provide factoring services taking into account the requirements of the Civil Code of Ukraine and this Law. The financial institution that provides factoring services may provide services related to the accounting of cash claims, the provision of a surety for the debtor's to perform its obligation under the cash claims of suppliers of goods (services) and the presentation of cash claims on behalf of suppliers of goods (services) or on its own behalf, as well as other services aimed at obtaining money from the debtor.
