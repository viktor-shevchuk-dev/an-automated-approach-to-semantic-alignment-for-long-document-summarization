3. A seaport shall develop in accordance with the seaport development plan, which is made taking into account the development plans of marine terminals, as well as enterprises, the main products and/or raw materials of which are objects of export-import transactions and are served as cargo in the seaport. The development plan of each seaport shall be approved by the Ukrainian Sea Ports Authority and shall be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport. The seaport development plan must comply with the short-term Ukraine Seaports Development Strategy and should contain a detailed layout of the seaport territory.
