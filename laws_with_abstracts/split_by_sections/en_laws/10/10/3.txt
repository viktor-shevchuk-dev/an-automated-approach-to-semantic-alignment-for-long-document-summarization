The Ukrainian Sea Ports Authority or the owner of a marine terminal shall provide free of charge premises for the organisation of a border crossing points of the state border of Ukraine, where border, customs control and passage through the state border of Ukraine of persons, vehicles, cargo and other property are carried out. The control shall be organised and carried out in the manner prescribed by the legislation of Ukraine. In the appropriate cases and in the manner prescribed by the legislation of Ukraine, the level of ionizing radiation inspections, phytosanitary, veterinary and sanitary control, government control over compliance with legislation on food, feed, animal by-products, animal health and welfare, medical examination of passengers, crews, teams, etc. are carried out in order to identify persons with symptoms of infectious diseases.
