Stevedoring companies, owners (users) of marine terminals shall, in the manner, to the extent and under the terms and conditions determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and with their consent, participate in the development of a plan for localisation and mitigation of accidents (catastrophes), in purchase and/or maintenance of equipment, devices and mechanisms, reimbursement of expenses for liquidation of accidents on the seaport territory and in seaport waters, as well as on the territory and in the waters of marine terminals.
