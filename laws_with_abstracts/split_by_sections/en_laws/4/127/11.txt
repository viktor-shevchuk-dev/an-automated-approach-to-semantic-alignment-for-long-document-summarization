2. Non-executive and executive staff of civil protection service are entitled to annual regular leave at a full material compensation and financial recreational support in the amount of monthly wage. Leave of non-executive and executive staff of civil protection service whose time of service (in calendar terms) calculated following the procedure established for non-executive and executive staff of civil protection service is under 10 years - 30 calendar days, 10 to 15 years - 35 calendar days, 15 to 20 years - 40 calendar days, over 20 years - 45 calendar days.
