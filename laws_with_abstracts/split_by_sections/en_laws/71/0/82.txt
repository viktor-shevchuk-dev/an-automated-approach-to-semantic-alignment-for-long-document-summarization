86) “traditional foodstuff” means a foodstuff with special characteristics which clearly distinguish it from other foodstuffs of the same type as a result of the use of traditional ingredients and/or due to traditional composition and/or the use of traditional production/processing technology. A traditional foodstuff is a foodstuff that is used in practice and/or meets the specifications that have existed for the last 30 years before determining whether the foodstuff falls under the category of traditional ones. Changes in packaging and labelling that do not change the special characteristics of a traditional product do not affect its definition as traditional;
