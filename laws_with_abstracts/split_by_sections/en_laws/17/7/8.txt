6. Decisions to deny issuance of the documents for resolving the issue of recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection shall be made in respect of applications that are clearly unsubstantiated, i.e. if the applicant has no conditions provided for by clauses 1 and 13, part 1, Article 1 of this Law, as well as if applications are abusive: if the applicant, in order to be recognised as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection impersonates someone else; and in respect of applications filed by persons who had been denied recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection owing to the absence of conditions provided for by clauses 1 and 13, part 1, Article 1 of this Law unless the specified conditions have changed.
