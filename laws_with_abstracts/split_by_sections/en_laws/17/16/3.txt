3. A person who received the court’s notification on confirming the decision to deny issuance of documents for resolving the issue of recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, on denying the recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, on cancelling the decision on recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, must leave the territory of Ukraine within the determined period unless such a person has other lawful grounds for staying in Ukraine provided for by the Law of Ukraine “On the Legal Status of Foreigners and Stateless Persons”.
