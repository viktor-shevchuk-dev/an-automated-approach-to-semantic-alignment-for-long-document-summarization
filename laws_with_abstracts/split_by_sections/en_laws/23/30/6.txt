Bringing products in compliance with the established requirements shall be carried out at the place of residence (location) of consumers (users) or at the location of distributors, manufacturers (importers) or enterprises engaged in maintenance and repair of products, as well as at other places where it is possible to conduct such bringing in compliance. Business entities in the supply chain of the relevant products shall be required to cooperate with each other, in order to bring products in compliance with established requirements.
