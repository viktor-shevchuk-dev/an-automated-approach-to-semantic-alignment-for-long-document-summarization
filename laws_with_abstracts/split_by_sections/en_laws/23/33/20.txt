The period of execution of the decision must be justified and determined taking into account the nature of the risk and/or non-compliance with the established requirements, the volume of products to be brought into compliance with the established requirements, withdrawn from circulation or recalled, and the real capabilities of a business entity to properly implement this decision. The period of execution of the decision may be changed by the market supervision authority under a substantiated request of a business entity. Such a request may be filed at any time during the execution of the decision, but before the expiration of the period for the execution of such a decision.
