3. Decisions on restrictive (corrective) measures may be amended or revoked by relevant market supervision authorities following the results of analysis of notifications of business entities about their implementation of decisions on restrictive (corrective) measures and/or the results of inspections of the state of execution of these decisions by business entities, as conducted in accordance with Article 34 of this Law. These decisions may also be amended following the results of consideration by market supervision authorities of petitions, explanations, objections and information submitted by business entities in accordance with parts ten and fifteen of this Article.
