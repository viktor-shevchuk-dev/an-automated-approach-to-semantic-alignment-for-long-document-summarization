Making of the archival set of source materials of national films and films created within the territory of Ukraine in the current year shall be financed from the State Budget of Ukraine in the next year upon the submission of the State Film Fund of Ukraine based on the decision of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the sphere of cinematography. Where the making of the archival set of film source materials of national films or films created within the territory of Ukraine is carried out at the expense of private legal entities and individuals, the cost of such making shall be reimbursed to the maker from the State Budget of Ukraine.
