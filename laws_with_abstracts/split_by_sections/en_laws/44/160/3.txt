Persons with first and second group disabilities and persons who have reached the retirement age set out in Article 26 of the Law of Ukraine “On Compulsory State Pension Insurance” shall be referred to the care centres for persons with disabilities and the elderly, if necessary, with their consent. Minors who are deprived of parental care shall, where necessary, be placed in residential schools by the children’s affairs services or guardianship or custody shall be established for them.
