1. Convicts shall be entitled to short visits of up to four hours and long visits of up to three days. Short visits shall be granted to relatives or other persons in the presence of a representative of the facility. Long visits shall be allowed with the right to live together and only with members of the immediate family (spouses, parents, children, adoptive parents, adopted children, siblings, grandparents and grandchildren). Long visits may also be granted to spouses who live together as a family but are not married, provided that they have minor children in common. Charges for the use of short-term and long-term visiting rooms shall be paid by the convicts or their relatives or other persons at their own expense.
