1. If a convict does not fulfil the obligations set out in this Code, the Law of Ukraine “On Probation” and also those imposed on them by the court, or systematically commits offences that entail administrative penalties and indicate their unwillingness to embark on the path of reformation, the authorised probation authority shall file a submission to the court to revoke the release from serving the sentence with probation and commit the convict to serve the imposed sentence.
