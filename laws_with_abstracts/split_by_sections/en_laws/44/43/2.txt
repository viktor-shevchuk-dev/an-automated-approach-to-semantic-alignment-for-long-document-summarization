Deductions shall be made from the entire amount of earnings, without excluding taxes and other payments from this amount and regardless of any claims against the convict under executive documents, for each month worked upon payment of wages. For part-time workers, deductions shall be made from the earnings at each place of employment. Deductions under a court judgement shall be made starting from the day following the receipt of the court judgement and notification by an enterprise, institution or organisation, but not before the court judgement becomes effective.
