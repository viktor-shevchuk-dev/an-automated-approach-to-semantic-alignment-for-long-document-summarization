2. Convicts shall be relocated in custody following the rules of detention: men separately from women; minors separately from adults; remand prisoners who are being prosecuted in the same criminal proceedings – separately from each other; convicts who have previously worked in courts, prosecution bodies, the judiciary or law enforcement agencies – in isolation from other categories; convicts sentenced to life imprisonment – separately from other categories. Convicts with active pulmonary tuberculosis and mentally ill persons – separately among themselves and separately from healthy persons and, if necessary, following the doctor’s opinion, – accompanied by a medical officer.
