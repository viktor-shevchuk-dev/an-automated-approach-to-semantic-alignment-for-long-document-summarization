9. Agreements on the sale and purchase of securities of the collective investment institution entered into prior to the entry into force of this Law and the terms and conditions whereof provide for payment of the value of such securities by instalment, shall be performed on the terms and conditions specified therein. From the date of entry into force of this Law, it shall be prohibited to extend the term of such agreements and/or to amend them in regards to the terms of payment of securities of the collective investment institution.
