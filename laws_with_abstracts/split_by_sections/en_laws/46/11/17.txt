For other business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes), not specified in part 3 of this article, the “green” tariff shall be derived by multiplying the “green” tariff established by the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission, under Article 9-1 of this Law by a coefficient equal to 1.
