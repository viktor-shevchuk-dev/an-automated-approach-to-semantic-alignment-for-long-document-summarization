The price bid of the bidder participating in the auction for the distribution of part of the support quota for electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes) producing electricity from other alternative energy sources (except for wind energy, solar energy, blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used), may not be higher than 12 Eurocents per 1 kilowatt-hour.
