The State shall guarantee that the business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources at the commissioned electricity facilities, shall be subject to the procedure for stimulating the electricity production from alternative energy sources established pursuant to the provisions of this Article on the date of power facilities commissioning, including the commissioned power plants construction stages (start-up complexes), producing electricity from alternative energy sources. In the event of amendments to the legislation governing the procedure for stimulating the production of electricity from alternative energy sources, business entities may choose a new stimulation procedure.
