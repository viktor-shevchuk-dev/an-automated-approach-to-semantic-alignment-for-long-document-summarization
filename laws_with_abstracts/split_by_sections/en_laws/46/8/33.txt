For business entities, private households and consumers, as well as energy cooperatives generating electricity using alternative energy sources, the “green” tariff shall be set until 1 January 2030. The fixed minimum “green” tariff amount for business entities, private households and consumers, as well as energy cooperatives, shall be set by converting into euro the “green” tariff calculated according to the rules of this Law, as of 1 January 2009 at the official exchange rate set by National Bank of Ukraine as of the specified date.
