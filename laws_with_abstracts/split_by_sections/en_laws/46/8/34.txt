The “green” tariff for electric power facilities commissioned before 31 December 2024, and private households and consumers, including energy cooperatives, with which the contract of electricity sale has been concluded before 31 December 2024, cannot be less than the fixed minimum “green” tariff amount, which as of the date when the quarterly meeting of the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission took place is converted into national currency at the average official exchange rate of the National Bank of Ukraine for the last 30 calendar days, preceding the date of such meeting.
