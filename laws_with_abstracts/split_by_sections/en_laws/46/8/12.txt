“Green” tariff for business entities producing electricity from biogas generated from biomass as a result of biodegradation, biogas obtained by forced gasification of biomass, biogas obtained from designated places or facilities where storage operations are carried out and/or landfilling of wastes generated from biomass as a component of industrial or domestic waste shall be set at the level of the retail tariff for consumers of the second voltage class as of January 2009, multiplied by the “green” tariff for electricity produced from biogas. For the purposes of this Law, biogas shall be considered to be gas from biomass.
