The level of use of the equipment produced in Ukraine at the power facilities, including the at the commissioned power plants construction stages (start-up complexes) producing electricity from alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used), shall be defined as the sum of the corresponding specific percentages of equipment pieces. If there are several identical pieces of equipment at the respective electric power facility (stage/start-up complex), the specific percentage of such piece of equipment shall be taken into account when calculating the level of use of the equipment produced in Ukraine only if each of these identical pieces is of the Ukrainian origin.
