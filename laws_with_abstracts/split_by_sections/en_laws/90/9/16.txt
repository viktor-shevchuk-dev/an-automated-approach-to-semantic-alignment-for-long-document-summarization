5. Recognition of the industrial design as protected shall not be affected by the disclosure of information about it by the author, his/her successor or a person who received from the author or his/her successor such information, directly or indirectly, within 12 months before the date of application to the NIPO or, if priority is declared, its priority. In this case, the burden of proving the circumstances of disclosure rests with the person interested in the application of the provisions of this paragraph.
