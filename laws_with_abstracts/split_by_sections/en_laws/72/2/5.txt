Annual quotas on the import of livestock products shall be sold at auctions held by the Ministry of Agriculture and Food of Ukraine within 60 calendar days from the date of publication of statistical data on the actual volume of domestic production of livestock products and the volume of their consumption (including imported) in the year before the current one. Quotas that remain unsold within the specified period shall not be subject to further sale or use. A state duty in the amount established by the legislation shall be charged for the auction from buyers of import quotas. Revenues received from the quota sale shall be credited to the State Budget of Ukraine.
