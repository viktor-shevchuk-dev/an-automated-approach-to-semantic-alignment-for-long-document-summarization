Executive authorities of Kyiv, Sevastopol and Simferopol city councils, city councils of cities which are administrative centres of oblasts, shall ensure the setup, equipment and operation of parking areas within the cities with the number of parking spaces there amounting to at least 10 per cent of the population for Kyiv and at least 5 per cent of the population for Sevastopol, Simferopol and cities that are oblast centres.
