Training, retraining and advanced training of vehicle drivers are carried out in accredited institutions, regardless of their form of ownership and subordination, which, based on the results of certification, received a corresponding certificate. Training, retraining and advanced training of drivers is carried out by specialists who meet certain qualification requirements of the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of health care. The list of requirements for institutions, qualification requirements for specialists who carry out such training shall be determined by a joint act of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine, central executive authorities in charge of shaping the state policy in the fields of education and science, transport and occupational safety.
