Provisions of part one of this Article do not apply in cases defined by parts six and seven of Article 30 of the Law of Ukraine “On the Fundamentals of Social Security of Persons with Disabilities in Ukraine” regarding places for free parking, nor to persons and vehicles which, in accordance with the law and/or the decision of relevant city, township, village council, are exempted from the payment of the cost of services for using paid parking sites for vehicles in specially allocated (marked) places on these sites.
