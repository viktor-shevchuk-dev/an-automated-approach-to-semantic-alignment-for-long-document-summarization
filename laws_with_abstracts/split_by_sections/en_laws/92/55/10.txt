The National Police shall also control the legality of the operation of vehicles on the public street and road network, observance of the rules for parking vehicles established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine in terms of ensuring road safety at parking lots; and in cases and in the manner prescribed by the law, shall temporarily detain and deliver a vehicle, including using special vehicles (when a detained vehicle significantly interferes with road traffic), to special sites or parking lots for temporary storage; shall temporarily seize the driver's licence under the law.
