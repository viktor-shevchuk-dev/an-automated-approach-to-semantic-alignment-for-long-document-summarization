An agreement for permanent use of the religious building of St. Andrew's Church of the National Sanctuary “Sophia of Kyiv” shall contain the following essential terms: the name of the religious building as an object of state ownership and as a nationally significant architectural site, the conditions and regime of use, preservation, protection and maintenance in a proper condition of the nationally significant site, access to such site in accordance with the legislation on the protection of cultural heritage, other conditions in accordance with the legislation on the protection of cultural heritage.
