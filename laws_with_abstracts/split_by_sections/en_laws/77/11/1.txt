Bribing the buyer's (customer's) employee, acting official shall be understood as giving or offering to him, directly or via third parties by a buyer's (customer's) competitor material or other types of benefits for improper performance or non-performance by an employee, official of the buyer (customer) of his official duties that arise from concluding or are related to the conclusion of a supply agreement between the supplier and the buyer (customer) for goods, provision of services, any other types of agreement or for non-conclusion of an agreement.
