Tariffs must take into account the cost of thermal energy and ensure the profitability of the business entity. Profitability shall be established by the authority competent to set tariffs. Tariffs for thermal energy produced and supplied by autonomous heating systems shall be formed and set separately for each apartment building equipped with an autonomous heating system, taking into account the cost of production and supply of thermal energy in such systems, as well as the profitability of the business entity conducting such activities.
