In the event of the state of emergency and special period in accordance with the laws of Ukraine “On the Legal Status of the State of Emergency”, “On Mobilisation Preparation and Mobilisation”, heat-generating and heat supply companies located in the area where a special period or state of emergency is introduced shall be obliged to comply with the orders of the authorities implementing measures of the state of emergency or special period in the relevant territory, on the supply of heat energy to consumers regardless of the contract.
