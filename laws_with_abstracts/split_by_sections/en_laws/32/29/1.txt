Business entities that have concluded contracts for the supply of energy products with heat-generating or heat supply entities, in case of restriction of energy product supply must notify the local executive authorities in writing no later than 24 hours before the introduction of restrictions, and the deadlines and the scope of restrictions must be specified. The scope of restrictions shall not lead to the shut-down of heat-generating equipment, except in the cases provided for in part seven of Article 19 of this Law.
