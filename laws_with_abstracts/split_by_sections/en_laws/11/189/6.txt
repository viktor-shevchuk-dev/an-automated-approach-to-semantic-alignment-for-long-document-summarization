6. The term of validity of the decision of the investigating judge on keeping a person under house arrest shall not exceed two months. Where necessary, the term of house arrest may be extended at the request of a public prosecutor within the pre-trial investigation in the manner prescribed by Article 199 hereof. The total period of detention under house arrest during the pre-trial investigation shall not exceed six months. Upon termination of this period, the ruling concerning application of the measure of restraint in the form of house arrest shall be valid no longer, and the measure of restraint shall be deemed revocated.
