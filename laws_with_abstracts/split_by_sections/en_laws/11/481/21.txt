6. The court shall make sure during the court session that the agreement was concluded by the parties thereto voluntarily, that is without use of compulsion, threats or promises or any other circumstances other than those provided for by the agreement. In order to ascertain voluntary nature of the agreement the court shall have the right, where necessary, to request documents, including any complaints of the suspect or accused, filed by them during criminal proceedings and decision taken as a result of their consideration, as well as summon and interview persons in the court.
