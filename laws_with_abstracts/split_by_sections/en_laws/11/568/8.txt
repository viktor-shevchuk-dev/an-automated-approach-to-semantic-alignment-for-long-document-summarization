2. A request from a foreign competent authority for international legal aid shall be executed within one month of the date of its receipt by the actual executor. Where taking complex and large-scale procedural actions is required, particularly those subject to approval by the public prosecutor or those that may be conducted only on the grounds of the ruling by an investigating judge, the time period for its execution may be extended by a central authority of Ukraine or an authority empowered to conduct relations with competent foreign authorities in accordance with part 3 of Article545 hereof.
