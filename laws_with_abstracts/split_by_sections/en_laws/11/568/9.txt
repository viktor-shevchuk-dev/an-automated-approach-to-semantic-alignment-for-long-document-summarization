The documents drawn up by a pre-trial investigation agency, an investigator, a public prosecutor, or a judge to execute a request for international legal aid shall be signed by the officials concerned and sealed by the respective authority. The documents obtained from other departments, institutions or enterprises regardless of their ownership structure following the execution of a request shall be signed by their general managers and sealed by the respective department, institution or enterprise. A pre-trial investigation agency or an investigator shall refer the records of the executed request to a public prosecutor monitoring compliance with the law during a pre-trial investigation for the verification of the integrity and legitimacy of investigative (detective) and other procedural activities that have been performed.
