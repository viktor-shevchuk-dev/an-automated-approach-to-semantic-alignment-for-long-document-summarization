4. The court concurrently with adopting a judgment in the end of court proceedings shall dispose the issue of revoking property attachment. The court shall revoke property attachment in particular, in cases of acquittal of the accused, termination of criminal proceedings by court unless the property is subject to asset forfeiture, non-imposition of a court punishment in the form of confiscation of property and/or non-application of asset forfeiture, leaving the civil action undecided or dismissal of the civil claim.
