1. The objectives of criminal proceedings shall be the protection of individuals, society and the state from criminal offences, the protection of rights, freedoms and legitimate interests of the parties to the criminal proceedings, as well as the provision of prompt, comprehensive and impartial investigation and trial in order that everyone who has committed a criminal offence were prosecuted in proportion to his/her guilt, no one innocent were accused or convicted, and no one were subjected to ungrounded procedural coercion and that an appropriate legal procedure was applied to each party to criminal proceedings.
