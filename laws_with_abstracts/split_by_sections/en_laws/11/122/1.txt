1. Procedural actions shall be conducted within the time limits prescribed by this Code. A period shall not be deemed missed, where a complaint or any other document was delivered to the post office or handed to a person authorised to accept it, before its expiry and, for individuals who are kept in custody or stay in treatment and prevention or psychiatric care facility, special educational institution, where a complaint or any other document was submitted to an official of the respective institution before its expiry.
