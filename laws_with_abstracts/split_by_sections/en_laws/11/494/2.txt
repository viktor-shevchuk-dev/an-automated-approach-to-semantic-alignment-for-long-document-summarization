Criminal proceedings against an underage person, particularly criminal proceedings against a group of persons of whom at least one is underage, shall be conducted by an investigator who is specially authorised by the chief officer of a pre-trial investigation agency to conduct pre-trial investigations against underage persons. In the course of criminal proceedings against an underage person, including proceedings in the matter of application of compulsory reformatory measures, the investigator, public prosecutor, investigating judge, court and all other persons participating therein shall perform procedural actions in a manner which intrudes the least on the underage personâ€™s usual way of life and otherwise corresponds to his/her age and psychological profile, explain the substance and meaning of the procedural actions and decisions, have him/her heard during adoption of procedural decisions, and take other measures intended to prevent negative impact on the underage person.
