3. After the sentence has been pronounced, the presiding judge shall advise the defendant, defence counsel, his/her legal representative, victim, his/her representative and the representative of the legal entity in whose respect proceedings are taken of their right to file a plea for pardon, the right to review journal of court session and submit written comments thereto. The defendant committed to custody as a measure of restraint shall be advised of the right to submit motion to be brought to the court session of the court of appellate instance.
