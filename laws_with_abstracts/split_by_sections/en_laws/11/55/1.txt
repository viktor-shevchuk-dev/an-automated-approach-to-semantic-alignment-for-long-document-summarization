1. Investigator, public prosecutor, investigating judge or court shall engage a defence counsel in an individual procedural action in accordance with the procedure set forth in Article 49 hereof exclusively in urgent cases, where the procedural action is required immediately, and the defence counsel who was informed in advance cannot appear to participate in such procedural action or send a replacement, or where the suspect or accused person is willing to have a defence counsel engaged, but either there was not enough time to engage defence counsel or the appearance of a counsel chosen is not possible.
