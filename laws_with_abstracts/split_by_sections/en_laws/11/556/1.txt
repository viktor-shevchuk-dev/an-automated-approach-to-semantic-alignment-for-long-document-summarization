Where as a result of execution in Ukraine of the request for international legal aid, information was obtained which under law is deemed state secret, such information may be transmitted to the requesting party exceptionally through the designated (central) authority of Ukraine, where such information will not harm the interests of Ukraine or such other state which has provided it to Ukraine, only where the treaty on mutual protection of information was concluded with such party, and in accordance with the rules and requirements set forth in such a treaty.
