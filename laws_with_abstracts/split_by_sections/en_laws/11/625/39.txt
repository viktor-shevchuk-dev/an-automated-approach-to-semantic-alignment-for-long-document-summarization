19. Not later than within three months after publication of this Code, the local general courts, courts of appeals of the oblasts, of the cities of Kyiv and Sevastopol, Court of Appeals of Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the High Specialised Court of Ukraine for consideration of civil and criminal cases shall hold the meetings of their judges in order to elect the judges empowered to conduct criminal proceedings with regard to the underage persons specified by the Law of Ukraine “On Judicial System and Status of Judges”.
