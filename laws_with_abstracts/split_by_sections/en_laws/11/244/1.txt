1. Investigator or public prosecutor may execute the ruling to authorise a search of home or any other property of a person. The victim, a suspect, defence counsel, representative, and other participants to the criminal proceedings may be invited to attend. Whenever investigator or public prosecutor needs assistance in issues requiring special knowledge, they may invite specialists to participate in the search. The investigator or public prosecutor shall take adequate measures to ensure that persons whose rights and legitimate interests may be restricted or violated shall be present during such search. Regardless of the stage of this investigative action, the investigator, public prosecutor, or other official involved in the search shall admit a defence counsel or attorney to the scene, whose authority shall be confirmed in accordance with the provisions of Article 50 of this Code.
