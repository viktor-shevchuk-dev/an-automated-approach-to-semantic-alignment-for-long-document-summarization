9. In criminal proceedings concerning the criminal offences stipulated by Articles 209 and 209-1 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine, pre-trial investigation shall be conducted by the investigator of the body that initiated the pre-trial investigation or to the jurisdiction of which the criminal offence that preceded the legalisation (laundering) of property obtained from proceeds of crime belongs, except when these criminal offences are classified under this Article to the jurisdiction of the National Anti-Corruption Bureau of Ukraine.
