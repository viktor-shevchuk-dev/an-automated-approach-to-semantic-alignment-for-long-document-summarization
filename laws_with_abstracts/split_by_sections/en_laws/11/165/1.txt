1. The investigating judge or the court shall dismiss the motion for suspension from office where the investigator or the public prosecutor fails to prove existence of reasonable grounds to believe that this measure is necessary to stop criminal offence, stop or prevent unlawful behaviour of the suspect or accused, who, if holding the office, may destroy or forge items and documents of essential importance for the pre-trial investigation, or exert illegal influence on witnesses and other participants to the criminal proceedings, or otherwise illegally obstruct criminal proceedings.
