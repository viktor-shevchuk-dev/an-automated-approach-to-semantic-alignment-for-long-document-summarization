Losses from damage to or loss of sacrificed cargo to indemnified by way of adjustment of the general average shall be determined according to the value at the time of unloading, which shall be based on the invoice issued to the consignee, or if there is no invoice, on the shipment value thereof. The value at the time of unloading shall include the value of insurance and freight, except where the freight is at the risk of interests other than the cargo.
