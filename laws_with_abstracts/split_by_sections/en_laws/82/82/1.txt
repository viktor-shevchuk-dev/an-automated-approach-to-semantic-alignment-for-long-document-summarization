While staying at the seaport any vessel shall be obliged to comply with valid laws and regulations of Ukraine, including those related to port security and navigation in the port, customs, border, sanitary (phytosanitary) regimes, pilotage, towage, salvage and lifting operations, anchorage and provision of berths, loading and unloading of cargo, embarkation and disembarkation of persons, services related to loading and unloading operations and any other port services, port charges, prevention of natural environment pollution.
