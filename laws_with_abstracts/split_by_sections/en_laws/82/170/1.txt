The carrier shall not be liable for loss, shortage of or damage to the cargo if it proves that they resulted from the actions or omissions of the master, other persons of the vessel’s crew and the pilot in steering or operating the vessel. For loss, shortage of or damage to the cargo caused by the actions or omissions of named persons during the loading, stowage, unloading or delivery of the cargo, the carrier shall be liable as provided in Article 176 of this Code.
