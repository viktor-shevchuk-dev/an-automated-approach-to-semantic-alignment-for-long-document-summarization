Any provision in a contract concluded before the occurrence of an event which caused the death of a passenger or injury to health or loss of or damage to luggage, which purports to relieve the carrier from liability towards the passenger or to set a lower limit of liability than that specified in Article 194 of this Code, and any provision which purports to shift the burden of proof set for the carrier shall be null and void. However, the invalidity of this condition does not invalidate the contract for carriage, which shall continue to be governed by the rules of this Chapter.
