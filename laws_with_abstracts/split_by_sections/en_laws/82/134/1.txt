The consignor shall be entitled to claim the return of the cargo to the port of departure before the departure of the vessel or the delivery of the cargo in an intermediate port or the delivery to a person other than the one stated in the bill of lading, subject to presentation of all copies of the bill of lading issued to the consignor or provision of appropriate security and subject to the rules of this Code on the repudiation of the contract for carriage by sea. The same right shall apply to every legal holder of all copies of the bill of lading issued to the consignor.
