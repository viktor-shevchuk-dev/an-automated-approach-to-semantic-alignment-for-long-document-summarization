The unit of account referred to in Articles 181, 194, 309, 318, 352 of this Code shall be the unit of “special drawing right” as defined by the International Monetary Fund. The amounts referred to in Articles 181, 194, 309, 318, 352 of this Code shall be converted to the national currency of Ukraine at the official exchange rate of that currency and the unit of “special drawing right” published by the National Bank of Ukraine on the day of restrictive fund establishment, and if no restrictive fund is established – on the day of payment.
