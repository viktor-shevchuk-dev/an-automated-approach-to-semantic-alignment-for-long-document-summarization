An agency agreement shall be terminated by agreement of the parties, as well as in the event of: revocation of the powers of a commercial agent by an entity it represents, or refusal of a commercial agent to continue to conduct commercial mediation under an agreement concluded by the parties without determining the term of its validity; withdrawal of either party to the agreement due to its termination or death; occurrence of other circumstances that terminate the powers of a commercial agent or an entity it represents.
