An economic company in the authorised capital of which more than 50 per cent of shares (equities) belong to a territorial community, as well as an economic company in which 50 per cent or more of shares (equities) belong to an economic company in which a territorial community has a 100 per cent share, shall publish information about its activities, except in cases established by law, by posting it on its own web page (website) or on the official website of the management entity that performs corporate rights management functions in it, within the period and in the manner prescribed by a respective local council.
