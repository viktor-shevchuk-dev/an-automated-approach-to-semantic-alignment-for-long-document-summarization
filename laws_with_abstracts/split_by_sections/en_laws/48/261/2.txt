2. For non-fulfillment of an obligation to accept agricultural products directly from a manufacturer, as well as in the event of refusal to accept products presented by a manufacturer within the timing and in accordance with the procedure agreed by the parties, a contractor shall pay a manufacturer a fine of five per cent of the value of the rejected products, taking into account surcharges and discounts, and also compensate for losses caused to a manufacturer; as for perishable products, a contractor shall pay their full cost.
