1. Leasing shall be deemed as an economic activity aimed at investing own or attracted financial resources, which consists in providing under a lease agreement by one party (a lessor) for the exclusive use of the other party (a lessee) property for a certain period of time that belongs to a lessor or is acquired by it in ownership (economic management) on behalf of or by agreement of a lessee from a respective supplier (seller) of the property, provided that a lessee effects regular lease payments.
