In the absence of an entitled party, its evasion from acceptance of performance or other delay in its execution, an obligated party under the monetary obligation shall be entitled to deposit the money due from it or to transfer securities under the obligation to the deposit of a notary office or private notary, which shall notify an entitled party. Placing money (securities) to the deposit of a notary office or a private notary shall be deemed the fulfillment of an obligation.
