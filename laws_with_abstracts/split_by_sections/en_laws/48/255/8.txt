8. Should s buyer (consignee) refuse to accept goods that do not meet the quality standards, technical conditions (if any), samples (standards) or the terms of the agreement, a supplier (manufacturer) shall dispose of the goods within ten days, and for perishable goods within 24 hours from the date of receipt of a buyer’s (consignee’s) notification of the refusal of goods. Should a supplier (manufacturer) fail to dispose of the goods within the specified period, a buyer (consignee) shall be entitled to sell them on the spot or return them to the manufacturer. Perishable goods shall be subject to on-site sales in all cases.
