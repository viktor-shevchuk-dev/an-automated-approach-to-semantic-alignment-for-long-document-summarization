1. A public association with the legal entity status, in order to perform its statutory purpose (aims), has the right to own, use and dispose of funds and other property, which, in accordance with the law, was transferred to such public association by its members (participants) or by the state, acquired as membership fees, donated by citizens, enterprises, institutions and organisations, acquired as a result of the entrepreneurial operation of such an association, entrepreneurial activity of legal entities (companies, enterprises) created by it, as well as property acquired at one's own expense, temporarily provided for use (except for orders) or on other grounds not prohibited by law.
