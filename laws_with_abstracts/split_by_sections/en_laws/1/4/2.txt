2. A decision to impose, cancel or amend sanctions against a foreign state or undesignated group of persons performing a particular type of activities (sectoral sanctions), provided for in clauses 1–5, 13–15, 17–19, 25, part 1 of Article 4 of this Law, shall be made by the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine, enacted by a decree of the President of Ukraine and approved within 48 hours from the date of issuance of the decree of the President of Ukraine by a Resolution of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine. The relevant decision shall enter into force upon the adoption of the Resolution of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine and shall be binding.
