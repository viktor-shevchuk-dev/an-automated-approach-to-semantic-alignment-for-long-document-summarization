Single permission or conclusion shall be issued to the entity of international transfers of goods for negotiations related to the conclusion of specific foreign economic agreements (contracts) for international transfers of goods, or for specific transfers of goods in accordance with these agreements (contracts) and shall be valid for the established term, but not more than one year. This period can be extended by the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of State Export Control, by virtue of a reasoned request of the entity of international transfers of goods, but not more than the term of the foreign economic agreement (contract).
