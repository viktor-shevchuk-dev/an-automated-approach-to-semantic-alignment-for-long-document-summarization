The licence application shall contain the following data: full name of foreign economic activity entity, surname and name of its head, name and code of goods under the Ukrainian Classification of Goods of Foreign Economic Activity (UKT ZED), name of the manufacturer, consumer of goods, code and name of the state (states) of origin and destination – for export, code and name of the state (states) of origin and departure – for import, the validity period of the licence, quantity and value of goods, code and name of the customs office, full names and addresses of seller and buyer, type of agreement, payment currency, basic and additional units of measurement of goods, approval of the executive authorities (if necessary), grounds for licence request, special licence terms.
