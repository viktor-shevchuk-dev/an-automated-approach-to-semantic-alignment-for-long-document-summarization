The decision on granting state credits and guarantees shall be made by the bank for foreign economic activity of Ukraine within the limits of state crediting for foreign economic activity in the currency effective in the territory of Ukraine and in foreign currency upon the proposal of foreign economic activity entities and based on the expert assessment results of the feasibility study of the object of crediting and terms of credits payback. The bank for foreign economic activity of Ukraine shall notify the applicant for state credit (state guarantee of commercial credit) of its decision within one month from the submission date of the specified application. In the absence of the above-mentioned expert assessment of the object of credit, refusal of state crediting (state guarantee of commercial credit) to the foreign economic activity entity on grounds of inexpediency shall not be allowed.
