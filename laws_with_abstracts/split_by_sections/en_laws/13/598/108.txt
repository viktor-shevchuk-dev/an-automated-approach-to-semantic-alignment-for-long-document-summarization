6-1. Registration of non-residents in the customs authorities in accordance with part 1 of Article 455 of this Code is mandatory effective July 1, 2021. Customs authorities shall start registration of non-residents in accordance with part 1 of Article 455 of this Code 180 days after the date of entry into force of the Law of Ukraine “On Amendments to the Customs Code of Ukraine on Certain Issues of Functioning of Authorised Economic Operators”.
