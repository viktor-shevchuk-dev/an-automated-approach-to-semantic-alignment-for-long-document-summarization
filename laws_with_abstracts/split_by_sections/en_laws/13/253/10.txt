8. Customs procedures performed by local customs authorities after set hours are payable by the party concerned and the fee is set by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine following the procedure identified by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy. This fee can be paid by completing a customs receipt. This fee is not payable for customs procedures performed by customs authorities outside designated simplified procedure locations provided for an AEO-C certified organisation (except when customs officials are called by such AEO).
