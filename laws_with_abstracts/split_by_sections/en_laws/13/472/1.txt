1. Committing violations of the customs rules provided by part three of Article 469, Article 470, part three of Article 478, Article 481 of this Code, as a result of an accident, the action of force majeure or illegal actions of third parties, as evidenced by relevant documents, as well as errors in the customs declaration, which did not lead to improper exemption from customs duties or the reduction of their amount, to failure to comply with tariff and/or non-tariff foreign economic regulation activities, if such errors are not made systematically (Article 268 of this Code), does not entail administrative liability provided by this Code.
