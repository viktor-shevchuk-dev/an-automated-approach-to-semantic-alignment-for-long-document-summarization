2. Carriers are responsible for the movement or actions aimed at moving goods across the customs border of Ukraine with concealment from customs control by means of submitting the documents containing false information to the customs authority as a basis for the movement of these goods (Article 483 of this Code), only if this information relates to the number of cargo spaces, their markings and numbers, and carriers have not taken measures to verify the veracity of this information or if such verification is not possible, no record is made to the international automobile consignment note (CMR).
