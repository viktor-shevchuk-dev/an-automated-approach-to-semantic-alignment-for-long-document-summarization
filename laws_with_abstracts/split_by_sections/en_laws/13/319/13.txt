10. When the customs clearance of goods or transit carriage of goods is completed and in the absence of violation of obligation to pay customs payments by a person, the financial guarantee shall, within two hours upon receipt by the customs authority of proof of actual performance of the obligations secured with a guarantee, be released (returned) by such a customs authority. During the release (return) of individual (one-time) financial guarantee on paper, all of its copies shall bear an imprint of a relevant customs security put by the customs authority.
