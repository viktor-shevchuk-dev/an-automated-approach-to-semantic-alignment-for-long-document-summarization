4. A person who has committed a violation of customs rules shall apply to the head of the customs authority with a statement of any form with a request to terminate the case of this violation of customs rules by means of compromise. The fact of submission of such application shall be recorded in accordance with the procedure specified in parts three and four of Article 264 of this Code. In the absence of legal grounds for termination of the case of violation of customs rules by means of compromise, the customs authority provides the applicant with a reasoned response on the reasons for non-application of the compromise procedure within one working day following the day of application.
