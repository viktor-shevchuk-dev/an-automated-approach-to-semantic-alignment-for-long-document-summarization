3. Foreign goods stored in the customs regime of the customs warehouse, prior to the expiration date or storage period established by parts one and two of this Article, shall be declared for import into the customs territory of Ukraine under another customs regime or re-exported. If foreign goods during storage in the customs regime of the customs warehouse were spoiled or damaged as a result of accident or force majeure event, it shall be allowed to declare them for free circulation in the customs territory of Ukraine, as if they were imported being spoiled (damaged) provided that such confirmation of these facts may be declared.
