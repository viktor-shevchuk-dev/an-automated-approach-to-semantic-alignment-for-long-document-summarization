14. Control probes (samples), as well as the remaining tested probes and samples damaged during the testing (analysis, expert examination), shall be stored by a specialised expert examination and testing agency of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state customs policy (its separate unit) within 60 days upon their receipt. Within that period the declarant or person authorised by him/her may appeal against the decision taken by the customs authority based on the results of testing (analysis, expert examination) in the manner prescribed by this Code.
