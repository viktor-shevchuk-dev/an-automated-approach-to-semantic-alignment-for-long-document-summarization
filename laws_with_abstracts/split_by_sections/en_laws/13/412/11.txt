9. In case of refusal of the owner of the goods whose customs clearance has been suspended on suspicion of infringement of intellectual property rights, to place such goods in the customs regime of destruction or elimination after giving the customs authority consent to their destruction or failure to object to their destruction in accordance with part 3 of this Article, including non-compliance with the term set by part 6 of this Article, the right holder shall be considered authorised by the goods owner to place such goods under the customs regime of destruction or elimination.
