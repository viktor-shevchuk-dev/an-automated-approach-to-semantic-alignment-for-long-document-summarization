4. Special equipment intended for handling, protection and storage of cargo that moves along with the commercial means of transport across the customs border of Ukraine shall be admitted similarly to the vehicle provided that such equipment is used for the intended purpose and re-exported (imported) along with that vehicle. Special equipment may be brought into or out of the customs territory of Ukraine separately from the commercial means of transport under the same terms and conditions, provided that it can be confirmed that the equipment is designed for specific commercial means of transport brought into (or out of) the customs territory of Ukraine before it.
