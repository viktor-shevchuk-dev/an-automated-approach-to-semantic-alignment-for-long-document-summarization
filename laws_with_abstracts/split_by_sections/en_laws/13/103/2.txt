If the vessel carries out cabotage transportation of identical goods between the same points on a regular basis, the document referred to in part 1 of this Article shall be the ground for carriage during the period determined by a carrier and approved by the customs authority. The customs authority that completed the customs formalities shall notify the customs authorities of its appointment. In this case, prior to loading the goods, only a list of those goods shall be presented to the customs authority, and for unloading only a list of goods to be unloaded shall be provided.
