1. Violation of customs rules is an administrative offence, which is an illegal, culpable (intentional or negligent) actions or inactivity that infringe the established by this Code and other legislation of Ukraine the procedure for moving goods, commercial vehicles across the customs border of Ukraine, before their appearance to the customs authorities for customs control and customs clearance, as well as transactions with goods under customs control or control over which are entrusted to customs authorities by this Code or other laws of Ukraine, and for which this Code provides for administrative liability.
