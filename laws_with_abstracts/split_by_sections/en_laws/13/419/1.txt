Forms, the procedure of submission and applications consideration, the procedure of submission, suspension, revocation of permits for activities, specified in Article 404 of this Code, as well as the rules of these activities and the procedure for monitoring their implementation shall be approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy unless otherwise provided by this Code. Requirements for territories, premises, tanks, refrigerators or freezers, indoor or outdoor areas that may be used in carrying out the activities specified in Article 404 of this Code, are established by this Code and the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy.
