5. Provision or disclosure of impersonalised summary information for the statistical purposes, impersonalised analytical data, impersonalised information on specific export-import operations entered by declarants in the customs declaration in accordance with part 8, Article 257 of this Code, except for clauses 2, 4, subclauses “b”, “c”, “f”, “h”, “j” of clause 5, clauses 6, 7 and 9, part 8 of this Article, including information related to the customs value of goods, depersonalised information on the common issues of customs operations, depersonalised information on related to law violations as well as public discussions in mass media and society on customs affairs shall not be considered to be a disclosure of restricted information.
