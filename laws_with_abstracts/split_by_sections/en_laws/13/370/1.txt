When crossing the customs border of Ukraine, goods required to mitigate the aftermath of acts of God, accidents, disasters, epidemics, as well as live animals, organs and other anatomical human materials required for the purposes of transplantation, goods with limited shelf-life or special storage treatment, goods for military purpose, radioactive materials, photo, audio and video products for mass media, goods of international technical and humanitarian aid, goods transported under the industrial cooperation agreements, goods transported under the TIR procedure, shall be subject to priority customs treatment.
