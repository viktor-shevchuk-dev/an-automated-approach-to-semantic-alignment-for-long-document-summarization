The amount of profit and general expenses, which include direct and indirect costs associated with the sale of these goods, shall be taken as a whole. The numerical value of expenses for the purposes of subtracting this amount shall be determined on the basis of information provided by the declarant or its authorised representative, unless this information is incompatible with the data obtained from the sale in Ukraine of imported goods of the same class or type. If the information provided by the declarant or its authorised representative is incompatible with such data, the amount for the calculation of profit and general expenses may be based on other relevant information as opposed to the one provided by the declarant or its authorised representative.
