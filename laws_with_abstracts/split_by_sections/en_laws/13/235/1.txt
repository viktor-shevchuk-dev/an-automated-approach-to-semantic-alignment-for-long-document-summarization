Customs clearance and control over the movement of stores to commercial means of transport shall be carried out as envisaged by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy. Stores shall be admitted to commercial means of transport under cover of accompanying documents. Stores export shall be declared under cover of accompanying documents with customs instrumentality attached and acknowledgement of receipt of stores by the administration of commercial means of transport within not more than 30 calendar days from the date when customs instrumentality is attached.
