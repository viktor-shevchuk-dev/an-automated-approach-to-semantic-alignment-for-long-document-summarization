3. In case of destruction or complete damage of personal vehicles placed in the transit customs regime, which are being moved for the purpose of transit through the customs territory of Ukraine, due to an accident or force majeure circumstances, their transit shall be suspended and the deposit shall be returned to the person who has paid a collateral, his/her heir or a person authorised by them, provided that such person provides the customs authorities with sufficient evidence of the destruction or complete damage of such vehicles.
