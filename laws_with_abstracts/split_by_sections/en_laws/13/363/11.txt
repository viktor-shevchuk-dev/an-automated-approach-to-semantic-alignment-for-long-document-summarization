11. If before or at the time of the audit the original copies of source documents, accounting and other registers, financial and statistical reports, and other documents related to computation and payment of customs charges and other issues relating to the state customs affairs are seized by the law enforcement and other authorities, those authorities shall provide the copies of such documents to the customs authority for audit. Those copies must be provided within three working days upon receipt of a written request from the customs authority, sealed and signed by the officials of the law enforcement and other authorities that have seized the original documents.
