3. State registration of changes in the information on a legal entity contained in the United State Register as a result of notarisation of the transaction involving the alienation (transfer) of the founder’s (member’s) interest in the charter (authorised) capital (unit trust) of the legal entity (except for joint-stock companies, limited liability companies, additional liability companies), as a result of the certificate of the right to inherit the founder’s (member’s) interest in the charter (authorised) capital (unit trust) of the legal entity (except for joint-stock companies, limited liability companies, additional liability companies) shall be carried out by a notary, who performed the relevant notarial act, immediately after making a certifying inscription on the document or signing the document, which is issued by them, except for cases of notarisation of a transaction with legal effect being associated with the occurrence of certain circumstances, and other cases stipulated by this Article.
