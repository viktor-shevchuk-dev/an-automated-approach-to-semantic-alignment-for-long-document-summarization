In case, in 60 days after receiving written notification on the grounds for losing the all-Ukrainian status by the public association, the number of separate units of the public association according to the data of the United State Register does not meet the requirements of part 1, Article 19 of the Law of Ukraine “On Public Associations”, a state registration entity shall adopt a decision on loss of all-Ukrainian status by the public association and make an appropriate entry in the United State Register. A copy of this decision shall be provided (send by registered mail with a return receipt) to the public association not later than the day following its adoption.
