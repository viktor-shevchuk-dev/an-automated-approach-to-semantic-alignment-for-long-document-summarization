2) cadet – a person who has been duly admitted to the higher military educational institution (institution of higher education with specific training conditions), military institute as a unit of the institution of higher education for the purpose of obtaining higher education degree, and who has been assigned a military rank of private, sergeant, sergeant major or a special rank of private or junior commanding staff, or had such rank at the time of admission. Cadet status may be granted to certain categories of persons studying at non-military institutions of higher education as stipulated by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
