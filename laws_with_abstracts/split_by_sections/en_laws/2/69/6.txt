3. Research conducted at the expense of the state and local budgets shall be funded by government authorities and local governments in charge of the institutions of higher education separately from funding the educational activities. Fundamental research shall be funded in the first place along with applied research activities conducted within the main directions of science and technology development. Government authorities and local governments in charge of the institutions of higher education shall determine the amount of separate budgetary funding for scientific activities based on the results of the institutions' scientific activities. Institutions of higher education shall determine the subject of research activities on a competitive basis and independently approve subject plans of scientific activities.
