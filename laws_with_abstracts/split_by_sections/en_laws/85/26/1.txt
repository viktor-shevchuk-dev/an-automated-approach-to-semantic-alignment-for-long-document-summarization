Railway transport lands shall include lands provided for use to railway transport companies and organisations in accordance with the legislation of Ukraine in force. These lands shall include lands that are railway allotment strips, namely lands provided for the railway line and its equipment, stations with all buildings and structures of energy, locomotive, carriage, track, cargo and passenger facilities, signalling and communication, water supply, sewage, protective and reinforcing plantings, service and cultural on-site buildings, other facilities that ensure the railway transport operations.
