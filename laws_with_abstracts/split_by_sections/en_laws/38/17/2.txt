Public administration bodies of environmental network facilities, owners and users of land plots located within the scope of the territories and facilities of the environmental network shall, in accordance with the established procedure, make proposals on provision of finance for measures related to the formation, preservation and inexhaustible use of the environmental network from the state budget of Ukraine. Provision of finance is provided in accordance with the approved lists of measures from the state budget of Ukraine, local budgets, as well as from other sources not prohibited by law.
