If a person believes that value judgments or opinions degrade his/her dignity, honour or goodwill, as well as other moral rights, they may enjoy the right granted by the legislation and shall have the right to their own interpretation of the case in the same media in order to substantiate the groundlessness of the disseminated judgments by evaluating them otherwise. If a subjective opinion is expressed in a brutal, humiliating and indecent manner, which degrades the dignity, honour or goodwill, a person who expressed his/her opinion or evaluation in such a manner may be obliged to compensate for the caused non-pecuniary damage.
