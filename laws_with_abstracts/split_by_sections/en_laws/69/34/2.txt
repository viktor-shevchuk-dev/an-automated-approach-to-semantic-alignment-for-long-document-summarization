The lessee who intends to exercise the pre-emptive right to enter into the land lease contract for a new term, shall notify the lessor before the expiration of the land lease contract within the period established by this contract, but not later than one month before the expiration of the land lease contract. In case of death of the lessor before the expiration of the land lease contract, the lessee, who intends to exercise the pre-emptive right to enter into the land lease contract for a new term, shall notify the heir of the land plot within one month from the day he became aware of the transfer of ownership of the land plot.
