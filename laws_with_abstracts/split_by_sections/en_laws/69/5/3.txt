In the event of termination of the land lease contract before expiry at the initiative of the lessor of the land plot (except for termination of the lease contract before expiry due to the failure of the lessee to perform its obligations), the right of lease of which is acquired as a result of the auction proceeds, the lessor shall compensate the lessee for the costs of its acquisition within the time limits determined by the provisions of the contract and the law, as well as for the losses of the lessee in case of termination of the lease contract before expiry, unless otherwise provided for by the lease contract.
