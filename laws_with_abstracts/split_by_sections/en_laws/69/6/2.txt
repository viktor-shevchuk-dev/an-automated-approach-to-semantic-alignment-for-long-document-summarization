In case of conviction to imprisonment or incapacitation of the lessor, the right to lease a land plot shall pass to one of his or her family members who has expressed a desire and can, in accordance with the law, become a lessee, and in case of their refusal or absence of such persons, it shall pass to persons who used this land plot together with the lessee, with their consent, if this does not contradict the requirements of the Land Code of Ukraine and this law.
