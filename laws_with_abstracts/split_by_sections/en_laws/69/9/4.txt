If any third person offers to conclude a lease contract for the same land plot, the person (except for the person who has a pre-emptive right to renew the lease contract in accordance with Article 33 of this Law), who has the right to use a significant part of the area of agricultural lands, shall have a pre-emptive right to conclude a lease contract on the provisions not worse than the provisions for the specified person. If any third person who has a pre-emptive right to renew the lease contract in accordance with Article 33 of this Law, offers to conclude a lease contract for the same land plot, the pre-emptive right stipulated in this part shall not apply.
