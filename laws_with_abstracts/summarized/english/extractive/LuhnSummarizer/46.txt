Definitions For the purposes of this Law, the following definitions shall apply: aerothermal energy shall mean the energy which is saved up in the form of heat energy in the air environment; alternative energy sources shall mean renewable energy resources which include solar, wind, geothermal, hydrothermal, aero thermal energy, wave and tidal energy, hydro energy, biomass energy, landfill gas, gas from sewage treatment plants, biogas, and secondary energy resources which include blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, methane gas for coalbed degasification and conversion of waste energy potential of technological processes; alternative energy engineering shall mean the sector of energy engineering, providing the generation of electrical, thermal and mechanical energy from alternative energy sources; geothermal energy shall mean energy stored in the form of thermal energy under a solid layer of the land surface; hydrothermal energy shall mean energy stored in the form of thermal energy in surface waters; energy generated from alternative sources shall mean electrical, thermal and mechanical energy that is produced at alternative energy facilities and can be a marketable product intended for sale; alternative energy facilities shall mean energy generating and other equipment that produces energy by means of alternative energy sources, the share of which is not less than 50 per cent of the installed capacity of all energy sources involved in the facility; alternative energy sector shall mean activity related to the use of alternative energy sources for the production, supply, transportation, storage, transmission and consumption of energy produced from alternative sources; renewable energy sources shall mean renewable non-fossil energy sources, namely solar, wind, aero thermal, geothermal, hydrothermal energy, wave and tidal energy, hydro energy, biomass energy, landfill gas, gas from sewage treatment plants, biogas; wind power plant shall mean a group of wind power plants or a separate wind power plant, equipment and facilities located in a certain area, which are functionally interconnected and constitute a single complex designed to produce electricity by converting the kinetic wind energy into electricity; wind electrical installation shall mean an electrical installation that converts the kinetic wind energy into electrical energy; production of pieces for the electric power facility equipment shall mean activities related to the production, which includes the implementation of a sufficient level of production and technological operations, the results of which change the classification code of goods according to the Ukrainian Commodity Coding System (UKT ZED) at the level of any of the first four characters, and the operations carried out in the production process should not be simple assembly operations pursuant to the provisions of customs legislation; guarantee of electricity origin shall mean a document issued by an entity authorised by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine at the request of the electricity producer, which confirms that the share or a certain amount of electricity is produced from renewable energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used); “green” tariff shall mean a special tariff at which the electricity generated by electric power facilities, in particular at the commissioned construction stages of electric power plants (start-up facilities) that use alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) is purchased; micro-hydroelectric power plant shall mean an electric power plant that produces electricity through the use of hydro energy, the installed capacity of which does not exceed 200 kW; mini hydroelectric power plant shall mean an electric power plant that produces electricity through the use of hydro energy, the installed capacity of which is more than 200 kW, but does not exceed 1 MW; small hydroelectric power plant shall mean an electric power plant that produces electricity through the use of hydro energy, the installed capacity of which is more than 1 MW, but does not exceed 10 MW; stage of a power plant construction shall mean a group of electrical installations or a separate installation, equipment and facilities located in a certain area, which are functionally interconnected and form a single complex designed for electricity generation, which according to the construction design documentation is a part of the electric power facility. The stage of a power plant construction may comprise start-up complexes; administrator of the electronic trading system shall mean a legal entity designated by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine under the law to be responsible for ensuring the functioning of the electronic trading system; support quota distribution auction (auction) shall mean a method of determining business entities that acquire the right to obtain support in electricity production from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used); auction price shall mean the price of 1 kilowatt-hour of electricity offered by the business entity, which has been recognised as the winner of the support quota distribution auction; electronic trading system shall mean a two-tier information and telecommunication system consisting of a central database and electronic platforms that interact through the application programming interface, which is provided in the form of open access code and determines the electronic trading system functionality. Incentives for the production of electricity from alternative energy sources provided for in this article shall apply to: business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes), regardless from the installed capacity of such facilities, construction stages (start-up complexes), which were commissioned by 1 January 2020; business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes), which have been commissioned since 1 January 2020, and in accordance with Article 9-3 of this Law shall not be obliged to participate in auctions; business entities intending to produce electricity from alternative energy sources (except for blast-furnace and coke-oven gases, and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes) and in accordance with part 4 of Article 71 of the Law of Ukraine “On Electricity Market” have concluded electricity sale contracts at a “green” tariff until 31 December 2019 and put such facilities into operation within two years from the date of these contracts for the facilities producing electricity from solar energy, and within three years from the date of conclusion of these contracts for the facilities generating electricity from other types of alternative energy sources; consumers of electricity, including energy cooperatives, private households, generating installations of which produce electricity from alternative energy sources and for which a “green” tariff has been set. Within the framework of the annual or additional annual support quota by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine at the request of the central executive authority shaping and implementing state policy in the field of the electric energy sector, the following measures may be taken: designation of certain oblasts (regions) for the construction of renewable energy facilities; determination of the maximum values for the capacity of the electric power facility or its construction stage (start-up complex), in respect of which the business entity may acquire the right to support at the relevant auction; proposal of the land plots for construction of renewable energy facilities with certain technical parameters and technical requirements for the connection to the electric network; proposal of the roofs and/or facades of buildings and other capital structures leased for the construction of renewable energy facilities producing electricity from solar energy with certain technical parameters and technical requirements for connection to the electric network. at the level of 0.4, for business entities producing electricity from solar energy at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes) with an installed capacity from 1 MW to 75 MW, commissioned from 1 April 2021; at the level of 0.4 for business entities producing electricity from solar energy at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes) with an installed capacity of 75 MW and more, commissioned from 1 January 2020; b) for business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes), commissioned before 30 June 2015 and whose “green” tariff is lower than or equal to the marginal (maximum) “green” tariff, by multiplying the “green” tariff established by the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission, under Article 9-1 of this Law by a coefficient equal to 1; c) for business entities producing electricity from alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used) at electric power facilities or their construction stages (start-up complexes), commissioned before 30 June 2015 and whose “green” tariff is higher than the marginal (maximum) “green” tariff, at the level of the marginal (maximum) “green” tariff.
