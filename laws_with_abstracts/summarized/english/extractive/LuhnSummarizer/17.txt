For the purposes of this Law, the following definitions shall apply: 1) refugee shall mean a person who is not a national of Ukraine and who owing to a well-founded fear of becoming a victim of persecution for reasons of race, religion, ethnicity, nationality (citizenship), being a member of a particular social group or political opinion, is outside the country of his/her nationality and is unable to enjoy the protection of such a country or unwilling to enjoy such protection owing to such fear, or, not having nationality (citizenship) and being outside the country of his/her previous permanent residence, is unable or unwilling to return to it owing to the specified fear; 2) child separated from the family shall mean a person under eighteen years old who arrives or has arrived at the territory of Ukraine unescorted by parents or either of them, grandfather or grandmother, adult brother or sister, custodian or guardian appointed under the legislation of the country of origin, or other adult persons who prior to arrival in Ukraine, voluntarily or owing to the practice of the country of origin have undertaken the responsibility for bringing up the child; 3) certificate on the application for protection in Ukraine shall mean a document, which confirms that a person stays on the territory of Ukraine on legal grounds for a period to commence when a person files a relevant application for recognition as a refugee or as a person in need of subsidiary protection and is valid for exercising the rights and performing the obligations provided for by this Law and other laws of Ukraine until the status of such a person is finally determined or such a person leaves the territory of Ukraine; 4) subsidiary protection shall mean the form of protection granted in Ukraine on an individual basis to foreigners and stateless persons who arrived in Ukraine or stay in Ukraine and cannot or are unwilling to return to the country of nationality or the country of previous permanent residence owing to circumstances specified in clause 13, part 1 of this Article; 18) temporary accommodation centre for refugees shall mean a place of temporary accommodation of persons who applied for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, in whose respect a decision on issuing documents for resolving the issue of recognition as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection is made, who have been recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection; 19) refugee status shall mean recognition of a foreigner or stateless person as a refugee by a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection; 20) period of stay on the territory of Ukraine shall mean a period determined by the legislation of Ukraine, international treaties of Ukraine, during which a foreigner or stateless person may stay in Ukraine on legal grounds; 21) temporary protection shall mean a form of protection, which is an exceptional practical time-bounded measure, and is granted in Ukraine to foreigners and stateless persons who arrived in Ukraine en masse and cannot return to the country of permanent residence owing to circumstances specified in clause 14, part 1 of this Article; A central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall organise the work on: 1) acceptance from foreigners and stateless persons of applications for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection; 2) performing fingerprinting of persons who have applied for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection; 3) facilitation of placement of children separated from the family to relevant child care centres or families; 4) assistance to children separated from the family in searching for their parents or other their legal representatives; 5) making decisions on issuing the documents for resolving the issue of recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or cancelling the decision on recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection; 6) considering the applications for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, and drawing up a written conclusion on recognition or on denying the recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection; 7) issuance of certificates on the application for protection; 8) issuance of certificates and travel documents for travelling abroad to persons recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection; 9) issuance of certificates to persons granted temporary protection; 10) re-registration of persons recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection; 11) registration of persons granted temporary protection; 12) determining the places for temporary accommodation of persons who have applied for recognition as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection in whose respect a decision on issuing documents for resolving the issue of recognition as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection is made, and referring them to the temporary accommodation centres for refugees; 15) facilitation in the employment of persons in whose respect a decision on issuing documents for resolving the issue of recognition as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection is made, and who have been recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection; 17) keeping the records and personal files of persons who have applied for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection in whose respect a decision on issuing documents for resolving the issue of recognition as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection is made, and who have been recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection; 18) assistance to persons who have filed for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, persons recognised as refugees or persons in need of subsidiary protection, or person granted temporary protection, in searching for and reunification with their family members in or outside Ukraine, and sending inquiries concerning the presence in those countries of family members of the specified persons and existence of legal grounds for family reunification;
