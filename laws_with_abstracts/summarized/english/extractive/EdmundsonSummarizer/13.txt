THE CUSTOMS CODE OF UKRAINE Section I GENERAL PROVISIONS Chapter 1. Fundamentals of customs affairs Article 1. Customs legislation of Ukraine 1. The customs legislation of Ukraine shall consist of the Constitution of Ukraine, this Code and other laws of Ukraine regulating matters referred to in clause 7 of the Code, international treaties of Ukraine ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine as well as regulatory acts issued on the basis and in pursuance of the Code, and other legislative acts. 2. Relationships associated with the collection of customs payments shall be regulated by this Code, the Tax Code of Ukraine and other taxation laws of Ukraine. 4495-VI
