LAW OF UKRAINE This Law regulates the particular legal procedure for exporting and selling timber and lumber. Article 1. The certificates of origin of timber and lumber produced thereof shall be issued along with a consignment note by enterprises that are permanent timber producers. Persons, guilty of the violation of the procedure for issue and turnover of certificates of origin of timber and lumber produced thereof, shall bear liability under the law. The central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of forestry shall ensure the maintenance and publishing on its website of an e-list of issued certificates of origin of timber and lumber produced thereof, as well as the registration of issued certificates in the state information web portal “Single Window for Foreign Trade” in the form of electric documents, certified by the e-signature on the day such certificates are issued.
