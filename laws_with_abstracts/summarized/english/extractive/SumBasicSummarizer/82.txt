Identification of a vessel A vessel shall have its own name. shall be determined by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport. CONTRACT FOR CARRIAGE OF CARGO BY SEA Article 133. In such a case, the consignor shall not be liable to the carrier for loss caused by the carriage of the said cargo, except for a general average. Limitation period for claims for compensation for pollution damage from vessels and nuclear damage A three-year limitation period shall apply to claims for compensation for pollution damage from vessels and nuclear damage.
