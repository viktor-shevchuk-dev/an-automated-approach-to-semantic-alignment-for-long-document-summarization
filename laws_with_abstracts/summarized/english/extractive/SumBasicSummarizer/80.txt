Section III STATE REGULATION OF SAFETY IN THE FIELD OF RADIOACTIVE WASTE MANAGEMENT Article 9. Specialised enterprises for radioactive waste management enter into relevant agreements with local executive authorities and local governments. Storage and disposal of radioactive waste Storage and disposal of radioactive waste are allowed only in specially designated radioactive waste repositories. The amount of radionuclides to be disposed of is regulated by norms, rules and standards on nuclear and radiation safety. The special regime on the territory of the radioactive waste repository and in the sanitary protection zone is regulated in accordance with the law.
