Article 2. Authorities performing government control in the field of dangerous cargo transportation Government control in the field of dangerous cargo transportation shall be carried out by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of transport, local state administrations and local government authorities, as well as other designated executive authorities, according to their competence. Conditions of dangerous cargo transportation The conditions of dangerous cargo transportation shall be specified by the regulatory acts that regulate the transport activity. In case of road transportation of dangerous cargos, the transportation conditions shall be established by the National Police authorities. Requirements for vehicles transporting dangerous cargos Vehicles transporting dangerous cargos shall meet the requirements of safety, labour and environment protection, and have the appropriate markings and a certificate of admission for transportation of dangerous cargos in cases prescribed by law. This Law shall enter into force on the date of its publication. President of Ukraine
