Article 2. Powers of the Central Executive Authority in Charge of Forming and Implementing State Policy in the Field of Intellectual Property 1. Application 1. The date of submitting an application shall be established by clauses 10 and 11, Article 10 of this Law. A request for legal protection to a trademark under the international registration in Ukraine shall be sent to the International Bureau of WIPO. NIPO shall issue a certificate within a month after the state registration of a trademark. If the grounds for termination of the certificate apply to certain goods or services, the certificate shall be terminated only with regard to such goods or services.
