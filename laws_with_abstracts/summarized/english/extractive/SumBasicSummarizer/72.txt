Article 1. 32/98-VR of 16 January 1998} Full rates of import duty on goods of the third to twenty-fourth groups under the Ukrainian Classification of Goods of Foreign Economic Activity (UKT ZED), as well as on excisable goods, shall be established at preferential rates. In doing so, agricultural products subject to quotas are not exempted from import duty. A specific quota volume within the specified 10 per cent shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine based on the volume of domestic production of a particular type of livestock products and the volume of their consumption (including imported) in the year before the current one. Revenues received from the quota sale shall be credited to the State Budget of Ukraine. 32/98-VR of 16 January 1998, No. 644/97-VR of 19 November 1997, as revised by Law No.
