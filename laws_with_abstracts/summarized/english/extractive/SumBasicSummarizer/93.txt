Article 2. Subjects of relations regulated by this Law are state authorities, local and regional governments, legal entities and citizens of Ukraine, international organisations, foreign legal entities and citizens, and stateless persons. Right to Scientific and Technical Information 1. Determining the National System of Scientific and Technical Information and Its Composition 1. Information resources of scientific and technical information that are the property of the state are recognized as state scientific and technical information resources. The materials and technical documentation provided by enterprises and organisations shall be used by bodies and services under the conditions established by enterprises and organisations that are owners of these materials unless the legislation provides otherwise.
