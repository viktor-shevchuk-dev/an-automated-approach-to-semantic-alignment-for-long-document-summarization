Article 3. According to this Law in the territory of Ukraine, the following legal regimes for foreign business entities shall include: – national regime, which means that foreign business entities have a scope of rights and obligations no less than the business entities of Ukraine. The customs authorities shall: – exercise customs control in Ukraine under the current laws of Ukraine. Licensing of the export (import) of goods shall be carried out in the form of automatic or non-automatic licensing. A licence shall be issued if the application and other submitted documents are executed in compliance with the requirements established by the legislation. Economic Relations of Ukraine with Other States Economic relations of Ukraine with other states shall be governed by the relevant international agreements and regulations of international law. As a state, Ukraine shall not be liable for the actions of foreign economic activity entities.
