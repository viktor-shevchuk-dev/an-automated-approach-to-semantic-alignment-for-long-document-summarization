Law determines state policy on the development of the grain market as a priority sector of the agro-industrial complex of Ukraine .<n>Law is aimed at the creation of legal, economic and organisational conditions for the competitive production of grain . Grain storage facility failure shall mean the inability of the grain storage facility being a participant of the Guarantee Fund for the fulfilment of obligations under warehouse documents for grain .<n> grain processing products shall mean flour, cereals, compound feed, gluten, malt, grain processing by-products, including, but not limited to, mill offal, cake and meal .<n> grain market shall mean a system of goods-money relations arising between its participants in the course of grain production, storage, trade and use . This Law applies to all grain market participants .<n>It is based on the Constitution of Ukraine and consists of this Law, the laws of Ukraine and other regulatory acts . Grain storage entities shall guarantee quality assurance and compliance with the standards of natural grain losses .<n>The main principles of the state policy on grain market regulation shall be: ensuring the state’s domestic demand for food, seed, coarse and industrial grain, as well as measures to increase its export . Grain supplies Ukraine’s grain supplies shall include: the grain in the state reserve seed pool; grain market participants’ own grain supplies .<n>It is forbidden to establish restrictions on the movement of grain and grain processing products .<n>The quality of grain and grain processing products produced in Ukraine or imported into the customs territory of Ukraine must comply with state standards and other regulatory documents . Grain and grain processing products recognized as unsuitable for food following laboratory tests shall be subject to examination .<n>Costs associated with the transportation, storage, examination or destruction of grain and grain processing products unsuitable for food shall be paid by the relevant owner .<n>Each party to the grain warehouse storage agreement shall be entitled to demand a grain quality test when shipping the grain . Grain accepted for storage under a regular warehouse certificate or double warehouse certificate may not be alienated .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall set the maximum fee (tariff) for storing the grain purchased for the state budget funds . Grain storage facility shall be liable for the loss, shortage or damage to the grain accepted for storage in the manner provided for by law .<n>The grain storage facility shall be exempt from the liability for loss, shortage and damage to the grain caused by force majeure . The Guarantee Fund shall operate in compliance with the law of Ukraine .<n>The main element of the system of guaranteeing the fulfilment of obligations under warehouse documents for grain shall be the activity of the Guarantee Fund .<n>The Guarantee Fund shall be subsidiarily liable for the obligations of grain storage facilities being the members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund . Grain accepted for storage under a regular or double warehouse certificate may be the subject of a pledge during the grain storage period through the pledge of the relevant certificate .<n>New warehouse documents for grain shall be issued in exchange for previously issued ones .<n>If the grain is accepted for storage for exoneration, a corresponding entry shall be made in both parts of the double warehouse certificate for the grain . The holder of the warehouse and pledge certificate shall be entitled to dispose of the grain stored in the grain storage facility .<n>The right to claim the first endorser of the pledge certificate shall expire upon the receipt of funds .<n>If the loan repayment term specified in the pledge certificate has expired, the grain storage facility shall be obliged to sell the grain . A warehouse certificate must contain the information provided for in clauses 1, 2, 4–8, part 2 of Article 38 hereof .<n>The holder of the regular warehouse certificate shall be entitled to dispose of the grain stored in the grain storage facility .<n>If the warehouse certificate holder possesses no pledge certificate but has paid the debt thereunder, the grain storage facility shall issue the grain to such a holder in exchange for a warehouse certificate . The Guarantee Fund shall be a legal entity and possesses separate property, which is the item of state assets .<n>The Guarantee Fund shall have an independent balance sheet, as well as current and other bank accounts .<n>It shall operate by virtue of the Regulations on the Guarantee Fund for the Fulfilment of Obligations Under Warehouse Documents for Grain to be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . The Guarantee Fund shall not be entitled to any actions other than those provided for by law .<n>The Guarantee Fund’s Administration Board shall determine the grain value reimbursement amount annually, by 30 July . The Guarantee Fund shall obtain the right of recourse to the insolvent grain storage facility in the amount of reimbursement provided to the holder of obligations under warehouse documents for grain . The Guarantee Fund shall inspect its members (temporary members) to confirm the failure of a grain storage facility .<n>Other government authorities shall be entitled, in the manner prescribed by law, to involve the Guarantee Fund in their inspections of grain market participants .<n>The Guarantee Fund shall be obliged to comply with the requirements of the law on commercial secrecy regarding the information that became known to it in the course of its operation . The Guarantee Fund shall publish its annual report as of 1 January, as well as information on its operation as of 1 July .<n>The reliability of the Guarantee Fund’s annual report must be confirmed by an independent audit organization . The provision of grain storage services to individuals and legal entities with the issuance of warehouse documents for grain by persons not being the members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund shall be prohibited.<n>To become a member (temporary member) of the Guarantee Fund and obtain the right to operate as a grain storage facility, a person shall be obliged to submit an application to the Guarantee Fund in the form developed by the Guarantee Fund’s Administration Board.<n>The following signs shall constitute exclusive grounds for a refusal to accept the entity/individual as temporary members of the Guarantee Fund: non-compliance of the applicant’s granary with the mandatory requirements; the applicant’s non-compliance with the requirements for the financial solvency of the Guarantee Fund member, established by the Guarantee Fund not earlier than in the previous calendar year; the applicant’s owner, members of the applicant’s management body or their close persons as provided for in part 4 of Article 49 hereof .<n>The temporary participant shall be deemed included in the temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund unless within three (3) years upon the grain storage facility acceptance to the temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund such grain storage facility has breached the obligations under part 8 hereof . Grain storage facilities shall be transferred to the category of temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund for a period not exceeding thirty (30) days .<n>Additional supervision over the operation of the grain storage facility shall be established during the period of the grain storage facility transfer to the category of temporary participants of the Guarantee Fund .<n>The exclusion from the temporary membership of the Guarantee Fund shall mean the exclusion from the membership of the Guarantee Fund . The Guarantee Fund’s regulations shall be subject to state registration and take effect upon the date of their publication .<n>Members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund shall be entitled to challenge the deeds of the Guarantee Fund if they are adopted contrary to the law . Sources of Guarantee Fund’s formation of property and funds shall include: membership fees of temporary members of the Guarantee Fund .<n>The funds of the Guarantee Fund shall not be included in the State Budget of Ukraine .<n>The funds of the Guarantee Fund unused in the reporting year may not be withdrawn and shall be carried over to the next year . If the funds of the Guarantee Fund exceed 20 per cent of the total value of grain under the warehouse documents for grain issued by grain storage facilities being members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund in the previous year, grain storage facilities shall cease paying regular contributions .<n>Members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund shall pay contributions to the Guarantee Fund in the manner and on terms and conditions provided for by this Law .<n>In case of untimely or incomplete transfer of membership fee, regular contribution or special contribution, grain storage facilities being members (temporary members) of the Guarantee Fund shall pay a penalty in the amount of double the discount rate of the National Bank of Ukraine from the outstanding sum for each day of delay . Loan from the state budget shall be provided to the Guarantee Fund on an interest-free basis for a period not exceeding twelve (12) current calendar months .<n>The day-to-day operation of the Guarantee Fund shall be managed by the Directorate .<n>The Audit Committee shall supervise the operation of the Guarantee Fund . The Administration Board of the Guarantee Fund shall consist of three persons appointed by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.<n>The general meeting of the Guarantee Fund members shall be a collegial management body, which includes all the Guarantee Fund members (other than a temporary member) and makes decisions on the issues associated with the operation of the Guarantee Fund. When voting at the general meeting of the Guarantee Fund members, each Guarantee Fund members shall have the number of votes pro rata its share in the total amount of grain accepted for storage under warehouse documents for grain in the previous calendar year .<n>The votes of each of the Guarantee Fund member shall be counted annually by the Directorate . The procedure for the Audit Committee shall be established by the Guarantee Fund’s Administration Board .<n>An international treaty for export and import of grain and grain processing products shall be delivered for implementation to the State Agent for Export and Import of Grain and Grain Processing Products under international treaties .<n>The State Agent for Export and Import of Grain and Grain Processing Products under international treaties shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Export and import of grain and grain processing products under international treaties shall be funded out of the state budget .<n>International cooperation within the scope of this Law shall be achieved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>Within their competence, they shall ensure the implementation of measures for the integration of Ukraine into the European Union in terms of the grain market .
