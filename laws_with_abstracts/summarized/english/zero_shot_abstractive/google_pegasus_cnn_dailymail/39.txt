This Law shall define the basic legal and organisational principles of ecological audit .<n>The provisions of this Law shall apply to all business entities, regardless of ownership and type of activity . Ecological audit in Ukraine may be voluntary or mandatory .<n>Cases of ecological audit shall be used by an environmental auditor to determine the compliance of the ecological audit entity with the requirements of Ukrainian legislation on environmental protection and other criteria of the ecological audit . mandatory ecological audit shall be carried out at the request of interested executive authorities or local governments .<n>An ecological audit can be performed by a person (environmental auditor) who has a relevant degree .<n>The ecological audit customer shall have the right to compensation for damages caused by illegal actions of the ecological audit providers . The ecological audit provider shall have the right to: determine independently the forms and methods of ecological audit .<n>The law shall guarantee the independence of the environmental auditor by means of: regulating his/her activity by law . Ecological audit shall be carried out by virtue of an agreement between the customer and the provider .<n>The results of the ecological audit shall be presented in the form of an ecological audit report . The certificate for the right to conduct an ecological audit shall be issued by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of environmental protection .<n>The validity of the certificate can be terminated before expiry or suspended for up to one year by the body that issued the certificate .<n>The central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of environmental protection shall maintain a register of environmental auditors and legal entities entitled to conduct an ecological audit . A mandatory ecological audit can be financed from the State Budget of Ukraine or local budgets .<n>International cooperation in the field of ecological audit shall be implemented in accordance with the laws and international treaties of Ukraine . Make proposals to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine on bringing the laws of Ukraine in compliance with this Law .<n>Within its competence, ensure adoption of regulatory legal acts resulting from this Law .<n> Ensure that central executive authorities bring their regulatory acts in line with this Law .
