This Law determines the legal, economic and organizational principles of activities of pipeline transport .<n>The pipeline transport system of Ukraine consists of: main pipeline transport; industrial pipeline transport .<n>Relations of pipeline transport business entities located in Ukraine and owned by other states are regulated by this Law . Law defines rules of design, construction, operation, overhaul and reconstruction of pipeline transport infrastructure .<n>Intended for transportation of hydrocarbons, chemical products, water and other products and substances . Main pipeline transport has an important economic and defense significance and is the property of the state of Ukraine .<n>Main pipeline transport infrastructure belongs to municipal enterprises or private business entities by right of ownership .<n>Privilege of state-owned enterprises, subsidiaries of the National Joint-Stock Company “Naftogaz of Ukraine”, as well as enterprises, institutions, organizations established as a result of their reorganization, is prohibited . Disposal of fixed assets that are state owned objects, of shares and stakes in the statutory capital of state-owned enterprises that carry out activities in transportation and storage in underground gas storage facilities is forbidden .<n>Only underground gas storage facilities (one or several) can be transferred . This article does not apply to the alienation of fixed assets of state-owned enterprises that carry out activities for transportation by main pipelines and storage in underground gas storage facilities .<n>Naftogaz of Ukraine’s subsidiaries and enterprises established by it can not be involved in a bankruptcy procedure .<n>For a special period, direct management of pipeline transport enterprises and control over their activities are executed by the Ministry of Defence of Ukraine . Pipeline transport lands include land plots on which are built ground surface and above ground pipelines and their structures .<n>Local bodies of state executive power, representative bodies and local self-government bodies, land owners and land users have a priority right to be supplied with the transported products .<n>State control on pipeline transport is carried out by relevant ministries and government departments, state supervision and control bodies and other specially authorized state bodies . Land within protected areas is not withdrawn from general purpose use, but is used with restrictions (encumbrances) in accordance to the law or contract .<n>Pipeline transport enterprises, institutions and organisations accept, store, transship and transport by pipelines .<n>The foreign economic activity of pipeline transport enterprises, institutions and organizations is regulated by acts of the legislation of Ukraine . Pipeline transport enterprises must ensure supply to customers of hydrocarbons, chemical products, water and other products and substances according to contracts .<n>Lineage transport enterprises must monitor pipeline supplies of toxic chemical products, liquefied hydrocarbon gases, and wide light hydrocarbon fraction . Collection, processing, saving and analysis of information about safety of pipeline transportation process .<n>Recovery from disaster aftermaths (floods, fires, snowstorms, etc.)<n> accidents and catastrophes that caused disruption to the operation of the pipeline transport . Liability for violation of the legislation on pipeline transport assumes disciplinary, administrative, civil and criminal liability that is established by the legislation of Ukraine .<n> Enterprises, institutions and organisations, that manufacture and supply equipment, pipes and other hardware for pipeline transport infrastructure are responsible for quality and technical level .<n>Construction and installation organisation during the first year of pipeline transport infrastructure operations must repair all defects related to the construction and installation that were discovered during the first year of operations at its own cost .<n> Reimbursment of damage and costs Local state executive authorities, representative bodies and local self-government bodies, enterprises, institutions and organisations, and also citizens, must reimburse damages incurred to the pipeline transport enterprises according to the procedure and in the amount prescribed by the current legislation of Ukraine . Damages incurred to the health and to the property of citizens due to the accidents at mentioned infrastructure facilities must be fully covered .<n>Money on such accounts shall not be subject to arrest, and transactions with such accounts may not be suspended .
