This Law is aimed at ensuring the protection of people and the environment from the harmful impact of radioactive waste .<n>The Law applies to all activities with radioactive waste . Radiation safety during radioactive waste management is non-exceeding the permissible limits of radiation exposure on personnel, the population and the environment .<n>Radioactive wastes are tangible objects and substances, the activity of radionuclides or radioactive contamination of which exceeds the limits established by current regulations . Expenditures for financial support of activities in the field of radioactive waste management are provided annually in the State Budget of Ukraine .<n>As part of the special fund of the State Budget of Ukraine, the State Fund for Radioactive Waste Management is created .<n>The Fund's money is allocated for the implementation of budget programmes, which involve works and measures to design, build, commission, operate, decommission or shut down the facilities intended for radioactive waste management . Procedure for using the Fund's money shall be established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>The main administrator of the Fund's money is the central executive authority responsible for public administration in the field of radioactive waste management . jurisdiction of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea in the field of radioactive waste management includes: Participation in the formation and implementation of the state policy of Ukraine in the field of radioactive waste management .<n>Public administration and public regulation during radioactive waste management in accordance with the laws of Ukraine and the laws of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea . State regulation of safety in the field of radioactive waste management is carried out by: The Ministry of Environmental Protection and Nuclear Safety of Ukraine .<n>Public administration in the field of radioactive waste management is carried out by ministries and other central executive bodies designated by the President of Ukraine . Right to radioactive waste management is given to legal entities and individuals who have a duly issued permit of the state regulatory body for nuclear and radiation safety .<n>Radioactive waste becomes the property of the state since signing the document on the transfer of radioactive waste from the licensee . Relationships between enterprises that generate radioactive waste and specialised enterprises for radioactive waste management are built on a contractual basis .<n>The state keeps records of radioactive waste to avoid the uncontrolled accumulation of radioactive waste .<n>The regulations on the State Register of Radioactive Waste is approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . The State Cadastre of Radioactive Waste Repositories is entrusted to the state administration body in the field of radioactive waste management .<n>The regulation on the State Cadastre of Radioactive Waste Repositories is approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . responsibilities to ensure physical protection during radioactive waste management rest with the licensees .<n>Operation of radioactive waste repositories is allowed after obtaining a licence for the right to manage radioactive waste . The regime on the territory of the radioactive waste repository and in the sanitary protection zone is regulated by law .<n>The responsibility for the preservation of documents characterizing the object, warning signs, fences, etc. rests with the public administration body in the field of radioactive waste management .<n>The decision to construct a facility intended for radioactive waste management is made by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine or the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . The results of the environmental impact assessment and conclusions of the expert review should be available to the public .<n>The project of the radioactive waste repository must contain two safety assessments .<n>The decision to select a site for the construction of the radioactive waste repository shall be made by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine or the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Radioactive waste repository or facility intended for radioactive waste management shall be commissioned in the presence of a duly issued licence for operation .<n>Land plots reserved for radioactive waste repositories or facilities intended for radioactive waste management shall be provided for the entire period of operation and mothballing .<n>Citizens who live or work in the area of radioactive waste repositories and facilities intended for radioactive waste management are guaranteed the right to compensation for damage caused by these repositories and facilities . The law may provide for liability for other violations in the field of radioactive waste management .<n>The legal basis for international co-operation in the field of radioactive waste management is the rules of international treaties to which Ukraine is a party .
