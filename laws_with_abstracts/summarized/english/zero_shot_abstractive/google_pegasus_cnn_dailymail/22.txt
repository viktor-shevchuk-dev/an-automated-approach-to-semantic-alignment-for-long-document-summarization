This Law shall define the legal basis for the activities in the field of culture .<n>It aims at regulating public relations associated with the creation, use, distribution, preservation of cultural heritage and cultural values . Cultural space is the field to carry out cultural activities and meet cultural, information and leisure needs of citizens .<n>National cultural state target programme is a programme aimed at creating favourable conditions for national cultural development, preserving national cultural heritage, meeting intellectual and spiritual human needs and a target regional programme in the field of culture . The legislation of Ukraine on culture shall consist of the Constitution of Ukraine, this Law and other laws regulating activities in the field of culture, international treaties on culture ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine and other regulatory acts. The main principles of the state cultural policy include: recognition of culture as one of the main factors of the identity of the Ukrainian people .<n>The state cultural policy forms, grounds, conditions, and procedure for providing state support to culture . The State shall, as a matter of priority, create conditions for: development of the culture of the Ukrainian people, indigenous peoples, and national minorities of Ukraine; preservation, reproduction, and protection of the historical environment; aesthetic education of citizens, primarily of children and youth; expansion of the cultural infrastructure in villages; protection, promotion, and support of cultural diversity as one of the most important factors of sustainable development of the State. The Action Programme for the Development of Culture shall not contradict the Action Programme of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>The State shall guarantee the free use of the languages of all national minorities of Ukraine .<n>The right of a person to implement creative activity can be exercised both on a professional and amateur basis . Citizens have the right to access to cultural values and cultural goods .<n>Children of pre-school age, pupils, students, pensioners, persons with disabilities shall have the right to attend state and communal cultural institutions .<n>Plast, scouting public associations determined by the Law of Ukraine shall have the right to use state and communal cultural institutions . The State shall create conditions for the cultural development of citizens of Ukraine of all nationalities .<n>Citizens of Ukraine of any nationality shall have the right to preserve, develop, and promote their culture . Support for domestic producers in the field of culture .<n>Support for socially important cultural events .<n>Dissemination and promotion of literary and art works . Preservation of cultural values on the territory of Ukraine, protection of cultural heritage and the historical environment.<n>The removal of printed publications, source materials of films and phonograms, other artistic and literary works, documents, monuments from libraries, museums, archives and film funds, the destruction of cultural values for ideological or political reasons shall be prohibited.<n>The documents on cultural heritage objects and information on cultural values shall be preserved by means of establishing an insurance fund of documentation.<n>The State shall create conditions for professional creative activity in the field of literature, theatre, music, choreography, circus, fine and decorative and applied arts, museum work, architecture, cinematography, journalism, information, design, research, restoration, and other activities aimed at the development of culture. The status of a professional creative worker and the legal basis for the professional creative activity shall be determined by this Law .<n>The State shall ensure the creation of conditions for the investment and innovation activities in the field of culture .<n>The funds received from privatisation of state-owned cultural objects, including objects of unfinished construction, shall be credited in full to the State Budget of Ukraine . Objects of state and communal ownership that are inscribed or are to be inscribed in the State Register of National Cultural Property may not be the subject of the pledge .<n>Heads of state and communal cultural institutions shall be appointed by entering into a contract for a period of five years based on the results of a competition .<n>The right to appoint, conclude a contract with heads of cultural institutions on the temporarily occupied territories and dismiss them shall be granted . Head of a state or communal cultural institution may be appointed as part-time head of a state or communal cultural institution located on the temporarily occupied territories solely in the relevant functional area .<n>Head of a state or communal cultural institution may compete for the position of artistic director in the respective institution .<n>Head of a state or communal cultural institution may employ artistic staff in the number exceeding the manning table by virtue of a civil law contract . Head of a national cultural institution may be a person having higher education, at least three years of work experience in the field of culture, speaking the state language .<n> capable of performing the respective job duties in terms of his/her competencies and moral qualities .<n>A person shall not be appointed as the head of a state or communal cultural institution if he/she: has been declared incapable or partially capable by a court judgment . The competition committee shall consist of nine members.<n>The members of the competition committee shall be impartial and independent.<n>No interference in the activities of the competition committee by any authority or the competition participants shall be permitted. All interested persons may take part in the competition for the position of head of a state or communal cultural institution .<n>A person taking part in the competition shall submit the following documents within 30 days from the announcement of the competition . All interested persons may, within seven days after the publication of the information on the candidates, submit suggestions and comments on the candidates to the competition committee .<n>The competition committee shall review the documents submitted by the candidates for the position of head of a state or communal cultural institution for compliance with the qualification requirements .<n>A person shall not be allowed to participate in the selection of candidates for the position of head of a state or communal cultural institution if he/she does not meet the requirements specified in Article 21-1 of this Law . The competition committee shall interview the candidates and hear public presentations of draft one- and five-year development programmes of cultural institutions.<n>The competition committee shall decide on the winner of the competition by means of voting and publish this decision on the official website of the governing body.<n>The chairperson of the governing body shall be obliged to appoint the winner of the competition as the head of a state or communal cultural institution not later than two months after the competition is announced. The basic network of cultural institutions shall be formed by the executive and local authorities .<n>Existing cultural institutions and educational institutions in the field of culture shall be automatically included in the basic network . The status of a national institution (establishment) of Ukraine may be granted to several cultural institutions (establishments)<n>The fee for the output of paid services shall be set annually by the state and communal cultural institution in the national currency of Ukraine . The State shall create a favourable tax regime for individuals and legal entities allocating their funds for the development of culture .<n>The mechanism of tax promotion for supporting cultural activity shall be determined by the tax legislation of Ukraine .<n>The terms and conditions of remuneration of cultural workers of state and communal cultural institutions and educational institutions in the field of culture shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . The State shall ensure the appointment and payment of pensions to cultural workers in accordance with the legislation .<n>The benefits for the free use of housing with heating and lighting shall be provided if the average monthly total family income calculated per person for the previous six months does not exceed the income that entitles to tax social benefit in accordance with the procedure established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . To represent and protect the interests of professional creative workers and amateurs .<n>The State shall promote international cultural exchange .<n>The State shall commit to satisfy the national and cultural needs of Ukrainians residing outside Ukraine . ‘A professional creative worker’ is a person who is professionally engaged in a creative activity resulting in the creation or interpretation of works in the field of culture and art .<n>‘Creative activity’ is an individual or collective creativity that results in the creation or interpretation of works of cultural value .
