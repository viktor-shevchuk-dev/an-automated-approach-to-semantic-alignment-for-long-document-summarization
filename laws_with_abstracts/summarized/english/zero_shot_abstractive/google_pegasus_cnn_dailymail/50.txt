This Law defines the legal, economic, and organisational framework for the state regulation of innovation activities in Ukraine .<n>It is aimed at supporting the development of the Ukrainian economy through innovation . The legislation of Ukraine in the field of innovation activities is based on the Constitution of Ukraine .<n>The main principles of the state innovation policy shall be: focusing on an innovative way of development of the Ukrainian economy . Powers of the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and local governments in the field of innovation activities . Powers of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine in the field of innovation activities .<n>Powers of central executive authorities in the field of innovation activities . Powers of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local state administrations, executive local government authorities in the field of innovation activities .<n>Central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of scientific-technical and innovation activities .<n>Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local state administrations, executive local government authorities in the field of innovation activities . State control in the field of innovation activities shall be carried out for ensuring compliance with the requirements of the legislation on innovation activities .<n>The state control in the field of innovation activities shall be carried out by: .<n>the central executive authority implementing a unified state tax policy; .<n>the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local governments within their powers; . State support of the implementation of an innovation project shall be provided on the condition of its state registration .<n>The state registration of innovation projects shall be performed by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of innovation activities .<n>The examination for qualifying innovation projects shall be performed at the expense of the subjects of innovation activities that submit projects for state registration . State registration of an innovation project shall not provide for any obligations regarding budget lending for its implementation or another state financial support .<n>An innovative product shall be the result of an innovation project and scientific and research and (or) development of new technology (including information technology) or products with the production of an experimental sample or experimental batch . Products that meet the following requirements may be recognised as innovative .<n>An innovative product may be recognised as innovative products if it is not intended for replication .<n>An innovation enterprise may function as an innovation centre, business incubator, technopolis, technology park . Financial support for innovation activities at the expense of the State Budget of Ukraine, the budget of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, and local budgets .<n>The State innovation financial and credit institution shall be subordinated to the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of innovation activities . Funds from the return of loans issued by a State innovation financial and credit institution to the subjects of innovation activities at the expense of the State Budget of Ukraine shall be lent to the special fund of the State Budget of Ukraine .<n>The funds of the State innovation financial and credit institution received by it from the budget of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea or oblast and raion budgets under paragraph 6, Part 1 of Article 10 of this Law shall be spent exclusively for financing relevant regional or local innovation programmes and projects . Law of Ukraine "On State Secret"<n>Local governments may establish municipal ad hoc non-bank innovation financial and credit institutions .<n>Funds of a municipal innovation financial and credit institution shall be formed at the expense of the relevant local budget . Law on municipal innovation financial and credit institution .<n>Financial support by a municipal innovation financial and credit institution for innovation projects by administrating loans or leasing property .<n>Financial support for the implementation of innovation projects may be provided by a municipal innovation financial and credit institution in the form of consecutive tranches . The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, within six months from the day of publication of this Law, shall bring its regulatory acts in line with this Law .<n>President of Ukraine L.
