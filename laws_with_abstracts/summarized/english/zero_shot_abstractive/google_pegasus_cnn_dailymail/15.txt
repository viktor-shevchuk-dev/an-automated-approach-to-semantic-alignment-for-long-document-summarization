The purpose of the Law is to provide an innovative model of economic development by concentrating state resources on priority directions of scientific and technical production upgrades .<n>The priority directions of innovation activities shall be divided into strategic and medium-term priority directions .<n>The medium-term priority directions shall be defined for a period of up to 5 years and shall be aimed at implementing the strategic priority directions . The proposals on strategic priority directions shall be prepared within the state targeted programmes for forecasting the scientific, technical and innovative development of Ukraine .<n>They shall be prepared with the involvement of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, national specialised academies of sciences of Ukraine, higher education institutions and research institutes .<n>The procedure for preparing proposals on strategic priority directions shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Medium-term priority directions of the national level and their justification shall be formed by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of innovations .<n>The amounts of funds allocated for the implementation of priority directions of the national and sectoral levels shall be determined annually by the Law “On the State Budget of Ukraine”. The amounts of funds allocated for the implementation of priority directions of the regional level shall be determined annually by the corresponding budgets .<n>The state shall introduce measures to: 1) develop innovation infrastructure (innovation centres, technology parks, science parks, technopolises, innovation business incubators, technology transfer centres, innovation clusters, venture capital funds, etc.)<n> Monitoring of the implementation of priority directions of innovation activities shall be exercised by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of scientific and technical and innovation activities . In connection herewith, paragraphs 2 through 7 shall be considered paragraphs 3 through 8, respectively; 8) in paragraph 2, Part 6 of Article 13, the words “implemented by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine” shall be deleted; .<n>In connection herewith, paragraph 3 shall be regarded as paragraph 4; in paragraph 4, the words “from the priority sectoral directions of innovation activities” shall be replaced with the words “approved within the framework of medium-term priority directions of the sectoral level; .<n>Part 1 of Article 10 after paragraph 1 shall be supplemented with a new paragraph to read as follows: “shall prepare and submit proposals to the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the relevant councils on medium-term priority directions of innovation activities of the regional level” Within six months after the effective date of this Law, the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall bring its regulatory acts in line with this Law .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall provide for measures of priority support for the implementation of the priority directions defined by the Law .
