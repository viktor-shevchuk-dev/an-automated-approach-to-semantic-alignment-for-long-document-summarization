cargo transit by air, road, rail, sea and river transport across the territory of Ukraine . this Law defines the principles of organisation and implementation of cargo transit . it does not apply to the transit of mail, baggage, weapons, narcotic drugs, psychotropic substances, hazardous waste, and movement of goods by means of pipelines and power lines . transit of cargos is carried out by the appropriate road, rail, water and air routes . transit involves their carriage by one mode of transport without transhipment to another . dry bulk, liquid bulk, solid bulk, break-bulk, general cargo, and cargo-luggage accepted for carriage under an agreement (contract) transit of cargos may be accompanied (where appropriate) by a bill of lading . customs cargo declaration must be submitted to the customs authorities . transit of goods by sea and river transport is declared by submitting a cargo manifest . in the case of cargo transit by rail transport, a CIM consignment note is submitted . customs clearance of excisable cargo transit by any mode of transport shall be carried out only under the condition of submitting a customs cargo declaration . transit services (works) are intended for consumption and use outside the customs territory of Ukraine . agreements (contracts) for provision (performance) of transit services are concluded between residents and non-residents . the control of cargos in transit is established exclusively by the laws and international treaties of Ukraine . cargo transit is carried out at checkpoints on the state border of Ukraine of the relevant vehicles . the central executive authority for transport coordinates the activities of the parties to transit operation . ensuring adoption of the regulatory acts provided for in this law . take measures to conclude international treaties on the joint control of cargos in transit . ensuring accession to the international convention on the harmonisation of frontier controls of goods signed in Geneva in 1982 .
