this Law defines the legal, economic, organisational and financial basis for government regulation of activities in the field of technology transfer . it aims to ensure the effective use of the scientific, technical and intellectual potential of Ukraine, the manufacturability of products, the protection of property rights . patent freedom to operate is a characteristic of technologies and/or their components which means it is possible to make free use of them in a certain state without the risk of violating the intellectual property rights . dual-use technologies â€“ technologies that, in addition to civilian purposes, can be used for the development, manufacturing or use of weapons, military or special equipment . legislation on government regulation of activities in the field of technology transfer is based on the constitution of Ukraine, the civil code of Ukraine and the economic code of ukraine . technology transfer entities are enterprises, scientific institutions, organisations, higher education institutions and others directly involved in the development, transfer and application of technologies and/or their components . technology transfer entities shall interact with each other within the framework of: 1) development of technologies and/or their components; 2) sharing experiences and information on scientific and technological achievements; 3) consultations on the main issues of scientific and technical as well as technological policy . the main task of the authorised body for the development and implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer shall be to ensure state policy on technology transfer, acquisition, safeguarding and protection of intellectual property rights . authorised body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer . main tasks are to determine the most effective ways of using and transferring technologies . the body shall foster investment attraction in technology transfer and identify, prevent and suppress violations . central executive authorities shall submit proposals to the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine in coordination with the authorised body for the development and implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer . national academy of sciences of Ukraine and branch Academies of sciences shall contribute to the development of competitive, import-substituting technologies and their components . u.s. calls on ukraine to join the eu in promoting technology transfer . ukrainian government says it is committed to ensuring the protection of intellectual property rights . central executive authorities take measures to prevent unfair competition, counterfeiting and unauthorised distribution of technologies and dissemination of confidential information, distribution of dual-use technologies without the permission of the holder of property rights . national academy of sciences and branch Academies of sciences establish divisions for transfer of technology, innovation and intellectual property .
