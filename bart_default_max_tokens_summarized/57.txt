This Law defines the legal and organisational framework for an integral system of shaping and implementing priority branches of science and technology development in Ukraine. Priority branches are scientifically, economically and socially justified branches of long-term scientific and technical development. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall develop and implement a state targeted programme for forecasting scientific, technological and innovative development. priority branches of science and technology development, prepared under the state targeted programme for forecasting scientific, technological and innovative development of Ukraine. The list of priority thematic scientific research and technological development branches shall be compiled by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of science. The amounts of funds allocated for the implementation of each of the priority branches of science and technology development shall be determined annually by the law on the State Budget of Ukraine. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall organise systematic monitoring of priority branches. Summarised information on the implementation and relevant outcomes shall be submitted to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine annually.
