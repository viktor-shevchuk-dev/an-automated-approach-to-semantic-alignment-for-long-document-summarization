LAW OF UKRAINE The Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine decrees: 1. 2. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall: ensure the development of the National Programme for the Adaptation of the Ukrainian Legislation to the Legislation of the European Union no later than 30 April 2003; envisage costs for funding the National Programme for the Adaptation of the Ukrainian Legislation to the Legislation of the European Union in the draft laws on the State Budget of Ukraine for 2003 and subsequent years. 3. This Law shall enter into force on the day of its publication. A special priority is the comprehensive protection of human rights and freedoms. In view of this, the National Programme should be elaborated.
