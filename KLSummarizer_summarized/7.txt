Article 2. Final Provisions 1. 2. 48, Article 483; 2010, No. 3. The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine within three months from the date of publication of this Law shall: prepare and submit for determination to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine proposals for amending Ukrainian laws to bring them into line with this Law; bring its regulatory acts in line with this Law; ensure that ministries and other executive authorities bring their regulations in line with this Law. 3392-VI
