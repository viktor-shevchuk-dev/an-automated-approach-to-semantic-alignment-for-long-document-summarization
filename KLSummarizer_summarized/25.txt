I. Proposals for the conclusion of international treaties of Ukraine in the name of Ukraine, the Government of Ukraine shall be submitted to the President of Ukraine or the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, respectively. Powers to negotiate and sign international treaties of Ukraine are granted to: a) international treaties concluded in the name of Ukraine – by the President of Ukraine; b) international treaties concluded on behalf of the Government of Ukraine – by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine; c) interdepartmental agreements – in accordance with the procedure established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
