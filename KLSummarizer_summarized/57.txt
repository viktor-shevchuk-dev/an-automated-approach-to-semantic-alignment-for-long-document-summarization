The main purposes of this Law are as follows: developing the fundamental legal principles of further operation, decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units, and the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system; establishing the peculiarities of the legal regime for the use of international technical assistance provided free of charge and on a non-refundable basis for further operation, preparation for and decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units, the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system and the provision of social protection of Chornobyl NPP personnel; establishing the work funding procedure relating to preparation for and decommissioning and decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units, works in regard to radioactive waste management, activities on the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system and ensuring the social protection of Chornobyl NPP personnel and elimination of adverse social consequences in Slavutych town related to Chornobyl NPP shutdown, as well as determining the peculiarities of annual budgeting of expenditures of the State Budget of Ukraine for funding the above-mentioned activities. The effect of the Law extends to the activities related to the further operation, decommissioning of Chornobyl NPP power units, the transformation of the Shelter Object into an environmentally safe system and the provision of social protection for Chornobyl NPP personnel. The duration of the benefit payment shall be included in the total duration of the unemployment benefit payment and may not exceed it. Final Provisions 1. 2. 3. 309-XIV
