The Laws of Ukraine on geographical names have been published on Wednesday. The Republic of Ukraine has signed a law which regulates the use of geographical sites located in the territory of other states. The laws on geographical names in Ukraine have been published by the government. The name of administrative and territorial units in the territory of Ukraine is to be removed from a single populated place or raion. The National Assembly of Ukraine has approved a proposal to make geographical sites named in line with the law. The law on the naming and renaming of geographical sites in Ukraine has come into effect. The name of geographical sites formed by a decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine is to be named in line with the law established in the country. The Ukrainian government has approved the creation and maintenance of the State Register of Geographical Names. The new law on geographical names in Ukraine has come into force. The government of Ukraine has passed a law which makes it easier to enforce the law.
