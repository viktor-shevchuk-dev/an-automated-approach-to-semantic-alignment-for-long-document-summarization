Providing subventions to implement investment projects is based on the following main principles: 1) the principle of objectivity and openness – the subvention recipient is determined by transparent procedures; 2) the principle of unity – the distribution of funds shall ensure the implementation of the national values and tasks system of innovation development and contribute to reducing the differences in living standards of different regions of the country; 3) the principle of balanced development – providing state support to territories taking into account their potential; 4) the principle of targeted use of funds – the subvention is used exclusively to the purpose defined by its provider, taking into account the forecast and software documents of the country's economic and social development and the relevant territory, the prediction of the local budget.
