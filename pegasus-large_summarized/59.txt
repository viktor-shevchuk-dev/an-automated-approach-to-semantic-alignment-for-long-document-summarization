Tariff policy on road transport should satisfy business interest, provide the development of road transport, supplement the introduction of new technologies of transportation, the use of modern types of vehicles, as well as promote the resolution of the following tasks: increasing the possibilities of business entities to meet the needs of consumers in services, attract investment in the development of road transport and achieve stable economic conditions of work; stimulating competition and the emergence of new businesses entities that belong to road transport; ensuring a balance between effective demand for services and the amount of costs for their provision; ensuring stability, transparency and predictability of tariffs. Principles of road transport development The development of road transport by agreement with other central executive authorities shall take place in accordance with the program of development and improvement of transport, prepared by the central executive authority, ensuring the component and implementation of state policy in the field of transport and approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Component and keeping of the register of intercity and suburban public bus routes that do not go beyond the territory of the region (intraregional routes) is entrusted to the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea or regional state administrations, which approve the network and passports of intercity and suburban bus routes that do not go beyond the territory of the region (intraregional routes). Component and keeping of the register of public metropolitan bus routes is entrusted to the executive authority of the village, urban village, city council of the respective locality, which approves the network and passports of metropolitan bus routes. Features of labour organisation and control over the work of drivers of vehicles In order to organise safe work and effective control over the work of vehicle drivers, road carriers are obliged to: organise the work of drivers of vehicles, their modes of work and rest in accordance with the requirements of the legislation of Ukraine; bring about the measures aimed at ensuring road safety; provide the compliance with the requirements of labour protection legislation; bring about the organisation and control over timely passing of medical examination by drivers, to provide them with sanitary and household premises and the equipment. Requirements for vehicles and their parts Design and technical state of vehicles as well as their part shall meet the requirements, procedure for determination of which is defined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and ensure the following: safety of people who use vehicles or participate in traffic; compliance with standards for emissions of pollutants, greenhouse gases, electromagnetic interference, noise level and other factors of negative impact on people and environment; prevention of road damage by vehicles and their arrangement; efficient use of energy resources, parts and consumables; protection against illegal use of vehicles and prevention of cargo damage; preservation of safety properties from the moment of production of the vehicle to its utilization; compliance with other legal requirements. In a vehicle for the international transportation of perishable goods, in addition to documents confirming their compliance with the requirements defined in this Article, there must be documents on admission to the transportation of such cargos, issued in the manner determined by the authority. Legal entities are executants of maintenance and repair of vehicles, meeting the following requirements: have own or rented instrumentalities for maintenance and repair, meeting the following requirements defined by the legislation; maintenance and repair works are performed by the staff of the required level of professional qualification according to the types of these works; have production facilities, instrumentalities for maintenance and repair, meeting the requirements defined by the legislation. Control over technical state of vehicles by road carriers shall be performed in a manner, defined by the central executive authority, ensuring component and implementation of state policy in the field of transport. Significant terms of the contract on maintenance and repair of vehicle are the following: name and location of the parties under this contract; list of works of maintenance and repair and term of their execution; value of works and procedure of calculation; the list of components (materials) used by the executor, as well as given by the customer to the executor for performance of works of maintenance and repair of vehicles; list of documents given to the customer for the confirmation of maintenance execution or repair and warranty obligations of the executor regarding the performed works. Duties and responsibilities of the executor under the contract on maintenance and repair of vehicles The executor under the contract on maintenance and repair of vehicles is obliged to: render the capability to the customer (authorised by him person) to perform personal random control over the execution of works under the contract subject to compliance with safety requirements for labour protection provided by legislation; eliminate defects free of charge defined during the acceptance of works under the contract; perform the works free of charge or reimburse the costs of the customer related to the elimination of defects caused by improper performance of the contract; guarantee compliance of the technical state of the vehicle with the determined requirements within the maintenance and repair of this vehicle carried out by him; confirm documentary the types and amount of executed work and render relevant documents to the customer indicating the date of execution; perform warranty obligations, provided to the customer by him. Basic rights and responsibilities of a road carrier and a self-employed road carrier, performing transportation of passengers on contractual terms Road carrier and self-employed road carrier, performing transportation of passengers on contractual terms shall have the right to: cancel the travel of own vehicles due to the circumstances which he could not have predicted and could not have prevented by compensation of the funds to passengers (or the customer of services) paid by them for the transportation; restrict or terminate the transportation in case of natural disaster, epidemic, epizootic or other emergency; cancel the travel of vehicles in case of threat to the lives of passengers or the safety of cargo; indicate in the luggage check the state of the luggage with external damage or refuse to transport it in case the passenger denies to perform such an indication. Self-employed road carrier, providing the transportation of passengers on contractual terms, is obliged to: keep vehicles in proper technical and sanitary conditions, ensure their intime submission for boarding and departure of passengers; provide the transportation of passengers to the bus stop or route destination without additional expenses, in case of termination of the travel due to technical problems of the vehicle; provide the verification of presence of passenger documents, necessary to enter the country of destination and the countries according to the route of destination, before the beginning of passenger international road transportation and refuse to provide international transportation of passengers who have not presented the necessary documents at his request; undergo indispensable technical control of the vehicle according to the established procedure with an obligatory mark in the service book; undergo annual medical control with a certificate of the established sample. The relations between road carrier, performing the transportation of passengers on suburban and intercity public bus routes, which go beyond the territory of the region (interregional routes) and executive authorities, local authorities shall be defined by the permit of executive authorities and local authorities for the service of bus route, in which the following information is set: a list of public routes (travels) to be served by the road carrier, terms of transportation organisation, quality indicators for transport services for the population, term of road carrier work. Owners of bus stations are obliged to conclude a contract with road carrier, performing the transportation of passengers on public bus routes, only on hand of contract with the executive authorities and local authorities on the organisation of transportation on public bus routes or permit of the executive authorities and local authorities to provide service of public routes, passing through this bus station. Road carriers with a quantity of vehicles of ten or more are obliged to organise recurrent training for road transport managers and specialists, whose actions are connected with provision of road transport services, for a term of once per five years, and regarding transportation safety, labour protection and fire safety, for a term of once per three years in the manner, defined by the central executive authority, ensuring component and implementation of state policy in the field of transport. Services of bus stations Bus stations provide passengers with services, connected with the travel on public bus route, and road carriers, performing passenger transportation on contractual terms, the services, connected with departure and arrival of buses according to the schedule. Obligatory services that shall be provided by bus stations to road carrier, shall include the following: selling of tickets; organisation of arrival and departure of bus from the equipped platforms; informing the driver regarding the road traffic conditions for the route. Internal transportation of cargo and rules for their provision Internal transportation of cargo shall include the transportation of cargo between the points of departure and destination, located in Ukraine and complex of support operations, connected with this transportation, as well as technological transportation of cargo, performed within the scope of one production object without leaving on public roads. Basic rights and responsibilities of the driver of the vehicle during the transportation of cargo of internal communication During internal transportation of cargos the driver of the vehicle shall have the following right: to refuse to accept for the transportation the cargo, which does not meet the established requirements or requirements for sealing of cargo, if the seal is not clear or damaged; to request from the owner of cargo (a person authorised by him) proper fixing of the cargo on the vehicle in accordance with the requirements established by the legislation and cleaning of its body from the remains of the cargo. Basic rights and obligations of the executor under the contract on cargo transportation by road transport Road carrier shall have the following right: to refuse from acceptance of cargo for transportation if the customer has not prepared the cargo or necessary documents, or the changes to the details of these documents have been done without prior agreement with him; to refuse from cargo for transportation if the customer provides him with the cargo for transportation, which is not stipulated by the contract on transportation, the packaging of the cargo does not meet the requirements defined by law, damaged packaging or the seal is not clear, etc; to receive compensation from the customer if the vehicle has been damaged during cargo operations or during the transportation of cargo due to a fault of customer. Non-residents of Ukraine while performing the international transportation of cargo, shall possess the following: permission of Ukraine; permission regarding the coordination of terms and modes of transportation in case of exceeding weight or dimensional restrictions or document about payment of fare for heavy-weight (oversized) vehicles, in case the exceeding of weight (dimensional) restrictions in accordance with the legislation is less than seven percent; certificate on vehicle registration; certificate of conformity of the vehicle regarding traffic safety and environmental safety of requirements of the legislation of Ukraine, unless otherwise provided for by the international treaties of Ukraine; cargo documents.
