Transport in the system of Public production Transport is one of the most important fields of public production and is intended to meet the needs of the population and public production in transportation. The purpose and objectives of the public administration in the field of transport Public administration in the field of transport should provide: timely, comprehensive and high-quality satisfaction of the population's and public's production needs in transportation and needs of the defence of Ukraine; advocacy of citizens' rights during their transport service; safe operation of transport; compliance with the required rates and proportions of national transportation system development; advocacy of Ukraine's economic interests and the legitimate interests of transport companies and organisations and consumers of transport services; creating equal conditions for the development of transport company business activities; limiting monopolism and developing and competition; coordinating various types of transport operation; licensing of certain types of activities in the field of transport; protecting the environment from the harmful effects of transport. Transport management bodies assist the authorities and self-government in the exercise of their powers for the social and economic development of transport, together with them implement the environmental protection programmes, develop and carry out coordinated measures to ensure the continuous operation of transport in the event of natural disasters, accidents, catastrophes and during the elimination of their consequences, coordinate the work related to the prevention of accidents and violations in transport, as well as organise the interaction of various modes of transport in order to use them more effectively, improve the service quality. Fundamentals of business activities of transport companies The transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage and mail, provision of other transport services, operation and repair of communication routes are carried out by railways, shipping companies, business entities in seaports, automobile, aviation, road enterprises and organisations if this is provided for by their charters. Licensing of certain types of activities in the field of transport Licensing of certain types of activities in the field of transport is introduced for: ensuring the safety and reliability of transport operations; monopolism limitation and competition development; creating equal conditions for the development of business activities of transport companies; Licences for carrying out activities in the field of transport are issued in accordance with the legislation. Vehicles, structures, financial resources, transport equipment, communication routes assigned to enterprises, associations, institutions and organisations of the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of transport, road management, tourism and infrastructure, are national property and belong to the unified transport system. In order to ensure the proper operation of structures and other transport facilities, as well as to protect the land from the negative impact of these facilities, protected areas with special land use conditions may be established on land provided for use by transport companies. Transport companies shall have the right to: determine the term and schedule of transportation; assign regular and additional runs and routes of transportation; offer a level of comfort for passengers choice; require passengers, senders and recipients of cargo to comply with the requirements of this Law, codes (charters) of certain types of transport and other regulatory acts of Ukraine governing transport activity. Responsibility of transport companies Responsibility of transport companies for non-fulfilment or improper fulfilment of obligations for the transportation of passengers, baggage, as well as liability to passengers for late transport delivery is determined by the codes (charters) of certain types of transport and other legislative acts of Ukraine. The list of goods that must be accompanied by the personnel of the departmental paramilitary guard on railway transport along the entire route of the Railways of Ukraine is approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of transport. Central and local executive authorities and local self-government, bodies and units of the National Police, civil defence, Armed Forces of Ukraine provide immediate assistance in eliminating the consequences of natural disasters, accidents and catastrophes in transport and stopping illegal interference in transport activities that threaten the safety of life or health blockage of people, the safety of vehicles operation and the safeguarding of cargo. Transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage, mail by air, and in some cases by other modes of transport, are subject to mandatory control for compliance with safety requirements according to the procedure established by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in transport, road management, tourism and infrastructure in accordance with the legislation of Ukraine in force. Officials of the central executive body in the charge of implementing the state policy in the field of land transport safety, and the central executive body in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of safety in maritime and river transport, have the right to: gain unimpeded access to all vehicles and facilities of business entities, legal entities and individuals (in road transport – as well as individuals – business entities) in order to verify compliance with the legislation and international agreements of Ukraine on transport safety issues; check during working hours of business entities, legal entities and individuals on issues within their competence; make demands to business entities, individuals and legal entities requirements for the implementation of measures on transport safety required by the legislation; receive written or oral explanations, conclusions of expert examinations, audits, materials and information on relevant issues from business entities, legal entities and individuals; draw up certificates of inspection of business entities, legal entities and individuals; according to the established procedure issue obligatory directions for implementation on the elimination of infringements and shortcomings regarding infringement of rules of safety in transport; prohibit the operation of vehicles (except automobile transport), work execution and services provision; in accordance with the procedure established by the legislation submit materials on violation of requirements of regulatory legislative acts on ensuring traffic safety to the corresponding authorities to address the Powers of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport safety Central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport safety: keeps records and carries out analysis the causes of catastrophes, accidents, incidents in sea and river transport; develops preventing measures against catastrophes, accidents and incidents in maritime and river transport and monitors its implementation; keeps records of fires in maritime and river transport; performs monitoring of the conclusion of contracts of compulsory liability insurance of entities of transportation of dangerous goods by maritime and river transport; performs supervision of compliance with the requirements for the prevention of environmental pollution by maritime and river transport; performs state supervision (monitoring) of compliance with legislation requirements in maritime and river transport; prepares proposals for the appointment of conformity assessment authorities for conformity assessment in the field of maritime and river transport, participates in the development of draft technical regulations and other legislative acts on technical regulation in this area, organises training and recurrent training of certification specialists compliance; performs safety monitoring of transportation of cargo, passengers and baggage on maritime and river vessels; performs monitoring and supervision over compliance with the rules of use of maritime and river transport, public order protection and traffic safety in maritime and river transport, maintenance and operation of small size vessels bases (facilities), as well as compliance with the rules on the safety of inland waterway vessels ways and on providing the safety of cargo shipped by maritime and river transport; performs registration small-sized (small) vessels stationing bases and posting the information on its official website; performs supervision over the operation of the vessel traffic service and pilotage service; planning and design organisations, industrial railway transport companies and other enterprises, institutions and organisations regardless of ownership type in charge of its operations and development. Maritime transport and its contents Maritime transport comprises maritime transport companies that carry out the transportation of passengers, cargo, mail, vessels, vessel repair plants, maritime communication lines, as well as communication enterprises, industrial, trading, construction and supply enterprises, educational institutions, healthcare, physical culture and sport, culture institutions; planning and design organisations, and other enterprises, institutions and organisations regardless of ownership type in charge of maritime transport operations and development. River transport lands River transport lands shall include lands provided for use for: ports, specialised berths, quays and boatyards with all the technical buildings and facilities that service the river transport; passenger terminals, halls and berths; navigations canals, navigation, energy and hydraulic facilities, technical and service facilities; bank reinforcing structures and plantations; special plantations for growing wood, including timber; communications centres, radio centres and radio stations; buildings, bank navigations signs and other and structures for waterways maintenance, vessel repair works, repair and operation bases, workshops, shipyards, stationing and repair points, warehouses, material and technical supply bases, engineering networks, service and cultural on-site buildings, other facilities that ensure the river transport operations. Automobile transport and its contents Automobile transport comprises automobile transport companies that carry out the transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage, mail, vehicle and tyre repair enterprises, automotive rolling stock, forwarding enterprises, as well as bus terminals and stations, educational enterprises, repair and construction organisations, social and community institutions, other enterprises, institutions and organisations regardless of ownership type, that ensure automobile transport operations. Traffic and road transport lands Automobile transport lands include lands provided for use for buildings and equipment of energy, garage, fuel distribution facilities, bus terminals and stations, road production structures, service and technical buildings, service stations, petrol stations, motor transport and forwarding enterprises, automotive repair plants, bases, freight yards, container and trailer transfer areas, service and cultural on-site buildings, other facilities that ensure the automobile transport operations. Urban electric transport and its contents Urban electric transport comprises urban electric transport companies that carry out the transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage, mail, rolling stock, tram and trolley bus lines, maintenance and repair depots, service premises, funicular railways, cable car ways, escalators, rolling stock repair and spare parts manufacturing plants, energy and communications facilities, industrial, construction and repair, research, development and design institutions, health care, recreation, physical culture and sports institutions and other service and cultural on-site buildings, other facilities that ensure the urban electric transport operations. Urban electric transport lands Urban electric transport lands shall include lands provided for use for separated tram tracks and their infrastructure, tracks and stations of funiculars, cable cars, escalators, tram and trolleybus depots, carriage repair plants, energy and track facilities, signalling and communication, service and cultural on-site buildings, other facilities that ensure the urban electric transport operations. Coordination of all transport modes operations Coordination of all transport modes operations shall be carried out by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of transport, roads, tourism and infrastructure. Central executive authorities in charge of implementing the state policy in the relevant areas of transport and infrastructure, within the limits set by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of transport, participate in the implementation of state policy in international cooperation in the field of security in the road, urban electric, rail, maritime and river transport, road management, civil aviation and provide the fulfilment of obligations arising from membership in international organisations.
