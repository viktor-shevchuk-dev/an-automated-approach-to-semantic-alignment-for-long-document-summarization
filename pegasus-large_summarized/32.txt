Powers of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, Local State Administrations in the Field of Heat Supply The powers of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local state administrations in the field of heat supply shall include: elaboration and implementation of local programmes and participation in the elaboration and implementation of state target programmes in this area; coordination of the activities in the field of heat supply with local governments and the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of housing and utilities; ensuring compliance with the rules and norms in the field of heat supply; exercising supervision and oversight over the supply of consumers in cities and other inhabited localities with thermal energy. The method of state supervision (oversight) in the field of heat supply is the implementation by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of supervision (oversight) in the field of heat supply, of relevant studies, checks, examinations, inspections of the heating and heat-using plants and networks, energy equipment of the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply in the order specified by the Law of Ukraine “On the Main Principles of State Supervision (Oversight) in the Area of Commercial Activity”. When implementing the state energy supervision, the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of heat supply supervision (oversight) shall be entitled to: demand from the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply elimination of the revealed breaches of legal requirements; demand the cessation of acts that impede exercising the state supervision (oversight); issue binding orders to the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply to eliminate breaches of laws and regulatory acts, norms and rules on the technical condition of heating and heat-using plants and networks, their operation, design and construction, energy use in the field of heat supply; stop operation of heating and heat-using plants and networks due to their unsatisfactory technical condition and/or organisation of their operation by issuing to consumers of thermal energy and business entities operating in the field of heat supply a motivated written resolution of the head of the central executive authority implementing the state supervision (oversight) policy in the field of heat supply, or their deputy on the inadmisibility of continuing such operation. Inspectors of the State Energy Supervision shall be entitled to: access in accordance with the procedure established by law to heating and heat-using plants and networks of the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply; receive free of charge from the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply the information necessary to perform the tasks assigned to them according to laws and regulatory acts; issue, within the limits of its powers, to the parties to relationships in the field of heat supply, mandatory instructions on the elimination of breaches of the requirements of laws and regulatory acts, norms and rules on the technical condition of heating and heat-using plants and networks, their operation, design and construction, use of energy in the field of heat supply; Procedure for Payments for Thermal Energy Produced Using Natural Gas Supplied by the Guaranteed Supplier Payment for thermal energy produced using natural gas supplied in full or in part by a guaranteed supplier shall be made by thermal energy consumers and heat supply entities that purchase the thermal energy for its further supply to consumers by transferring funds to accounts with a special mode of use opened by heat-generating and heat supply entities for crediting the funds, including from heat supply entities that receive thermal energy for its further supply to consumers, in an authorised bank. Tariffs for thermal energy for business entities producing it on installations using alternative energy sources, including heat and power plants, thermal power plants and co-generation units, for the needs of institutions and organisations financed from the state or local budget, as well as for the needs of the population shall be set at 90 per cent of the current tariff for business entities for thermal energy produced using natural gas for the needs of the relevant category of consumers. The calculation of weighted average tariffs for thermal energy produced using natural gas for the needs of the population, institutions and organisations financed from the state or local budget shall be carried out based on administrative and territorial units (the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, oblasts, cities of Kyiv or Sevastopol) by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of efficient use of fuel and energy resources, energy saving, renewable energy sources and alternative fuels, in the manner prescribed by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Weighted average tariffs for thermal energy produced using natural gas for the needs of the population, institutions and organisations financed from the state or local budget, as well as tariffs for transportation and supply of thermal energy, published on the official website of the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of efficient use of fuel and energy resources, energy saving, renewable energy sources and alternative fuels, as of the 25th day of the last month of each quarter, are used by the business entities that produce heat at plants using alternative energy sources, including heat and power plants, thermal power plants and co-generation units, to calculate the tariff for thermal energy, the tariff for the production of thermal energy for the next quarter. Tariffs for thermal energy produced and supplied by autonomous heating systems shall be formed and set separately for each apartment building equipped with an autonomous heating system, taking into account the cost of production and supply of thermal energy in such systems, as well as the profitability of the business entity conducting such activities. A business entity engaged in the transportation of thermal energy shall be obliged to connect a heat-generating entity that intends to carry out activities for the production of thermal energy upon meeting the technical specifications and concluding a contract for connection to the heating network. After the construction and commissioning of such a facility, the funds raised for its construction (bank loans, sale of bonds, financial leasing, investments, interest for the use of these funds, etc.) shall be returned by taking into account the investment component in the tariff for production (transportation) of thermal energy which remains effective during the payback period of the investments into construction of this heat supply facility. Rights and Basic Responsibilities of Thermal Energy Consumer The thermal energy consumer shall have the right to: select one or more sources of thermal energy or heat supply entities if existing technical specifications make it possible; obtain information on the quality of heat supply, tariffs, prices, payment procedures, modes of thermal energy consumption; recover losses caused as a result of the violation of rights in case he/she fulfils his/her obligations under the contract in accordance with the law; connect to the heating network in accordance with laws and regulatory acts; obtain volumes of thermal energy in accordance with both the contractual parameters and norms and rules; receive recalculation for the consumed thermal energy taking into account the advance payment and the readings of the thermal energy metering units within a month after the end of the heating period. Heat supply, heat transport and heat-generating entities shall be obliged to: inform the consumer in writing or in the media in the manner prescribed by law when changing the tariffs for thermal energy; ensure a reliable supply of thermal energy in accordance with the terms of the contract, as well as rules and regulations; make resettlements for the consumed thermal energy with consumers taking into account the advance payment and indications of heat energy metering units within a month after the end of the heating period; carry out technical record-keeping of material and energy resources, as well as fiscal metering of thermal energy. In the case of construction or renovation of buildings, roads, bridges and other facilities, the works related to the arrangement, transfer of overhead and underground heating networks and other heat supply facilities shall be performed by customers of construction or renovation in accordance with the approved design and budget documentation and requirements of the relevant regulations, as well as under the monitoring of the owners of structures or heating networks. The procedure for monitoring the implementation of investment programmes in the field of heat supply, as well as the maximum cost of investments that can be attracted by investment programmes included in the calculation of economically justified costs, and the deadline for investment programmes until full repayment shall be set by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. Crediting of funds of international financial organisations, foreign government agencies or funds raised under intergovernmental agreements, or funds in the form of loans (borrowings, grants), or funds raised by the state or business entities operating in the field of heat supply under state or local guarantees, to special accounts for credit funds of heat supply entities, opened by banks in national or foreign currency, and the use of such funds for the preparation and implementation of investment programmes (measures) in the relevant field shall be carried out in accordance with agreements concluded with international financial organisations, foreign government agencies, or intergovernmental agreements. Supply of Thermal Energy in Limited Supply Conditions of Energy Products and the Introduction of State of Emergency and Special Period Business entities that have concluded contracts for the supply of energy products with heat-generating or heat supply entities, in case of restriction of energy product supply must notify the local executive authorities in writing no later than 24 hours before the introduction of restrictions, and the deadlines and the scope of restrictions must be specified. In the event of the state of emergency and special period in accordance with the laws of Ukraine “On the Legal Status of the State of Emergency”, “On Mobilisation Preparation and Mobilisation”, heat-generating and heat supply companies located in the area where a special period or state of emergency is introduced shall be obliged to comply with the orders of the authorities implementing measures of the state of emergency or special period in the relevant territory, on the supply of heat energy to consumers regardless of the contract.
