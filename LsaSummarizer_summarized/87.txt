Central executive authorities in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the sphere of healthcare, within the limits of their powers, ensure the development of sports medicine. Central executive authorities, that have in its subordination the Armed Forces of Ukraine and other military formations established in accordance with the laws of Ukraine, law enforcement agencies, rescue and other special services, ensure proper physical training of the relevant military personnel and employees, promote the development of sport, interact with the relevant public associations of physical culture and sport orientation. Organisational and methodology provision of children and youth schools is performed by the central executive authorities implementing the state policy in the sphere of physical culture and sport. Central executive authorities can provide financial support to all-Ukrainian physical culture and sports organisations from the funds of state budget and according to the legislation. Athletes who gained prize places at international competitions and their coaches are awarded financial incentives according to the procedure, established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. The state can provide additional incentives for legal entities and individuals (residents and non-residents), who are involved in charity activities in the sphere of physical culture and sport according to the procedure defined by legislation. Coaches and referees shall undergo compulsory attestation according to the procedure defined by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the sphere of physical culture and sport.
