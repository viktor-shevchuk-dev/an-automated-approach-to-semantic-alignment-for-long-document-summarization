Priority branches of science and technology development for the period up to 2021 Priority branches of science and technology development for the period up to 2021 shall be set as follows: 1) fundamental scientific research on the most important issues in the development of scientific and technical, social and economic, social and political, and human potential to ensure the competitiveness of Ukraine in the world and sustainable development of society and the state; 2) information and communication technologies; 3) power industry and energy efficiency; 4) rational use of natural resources; 5) life sciences, new technology for the prevention and treatment of the most common diseases; 6) new substances and materials. Priority branches of science and technology development, prepared under the state targeted programme for forecasting scientific, technological and innovative development of Ukraine, shall be discussed by the scientific community and submitted to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine following the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine to adjust priority branches of science and technology development set out in Article 3 of this Law, or to replace them. Priority branches of science and technology development shall be approved by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine by amending Article 3 of this Law. The list of priority thematic scientific research and technological development branches shall be compiled by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of science, with the involvement of other relevant central executive authorities, the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine and national specialised academies of sciences based on the outcomes of the state targeted programme for forecasting scientific, technological and innovative development of Ukraine and approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine within six months following the enactment of Article 3 of this Law. Priority thematic scientific research and technological development branches shall be adjusted by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine at the request of the central executive authorities in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of science in accordance with recommendations of the National Council of Ukraine for the Development of Science and Technology. The amounts of funds allocated for the implementation of each of the priority branches of science and technology development shall be determined annually by the law on the State Budget of Ukraine. Monitoring the implementation of priority branches of science and technology development To ensure effective management and timely changes in the implemented scientific and technical policy, adjustment of priority thematic scientific research and technological development branches, objectives of state targeted programmes, state order, the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall organise systematic monitoring of priority branches of science and technology development implementation.
