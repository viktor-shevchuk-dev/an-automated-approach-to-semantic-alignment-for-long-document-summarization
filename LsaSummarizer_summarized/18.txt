It is forbidden to require business entities to obtain permit documents that are not included in the List approved by this Law. Permit documents whose issuance is required by international treaties consent to be binding by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, that are not included in the List approved by this Law, are issued under the international treaties. Issuance of permit documents in the areas of state export control, state regulation of financial services markets, protection of state secrets (activities related to state secrets), protection of economic competition and use of nuclear energy is carried out under the Laws governing relations in these areas. The establishment of other types of permit documents in the field of economic activity can be done by means of amending this Law accordingly. The need to obtain permit documents, as established by Laws, arises solely after such documents have been added to the List, except in the cases provided for in part four of this Article. To be included in part one, Article 4 of the Law of Ukraine “On the Permit System in the Field of Economic Activity” (The Official Bulletin of the Verkhovna Rada, 2005, No. 46, Article 547) the following changes: paragraph six shall be amended to read as follows: “an exhaustive list of grounds for a refusal to issue, re-issue, issue of a duplicate or revoke a permit document”; supplement with the ninth paragraph to read as follows: “list of permit documents in the economic sector”.
