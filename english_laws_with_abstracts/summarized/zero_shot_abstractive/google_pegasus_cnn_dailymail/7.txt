Collective investment activities shall be carried out after the collective investment institution is entered in the Register .<n>The collective investment institution may be open, interval and closed depending on the procedure it carries out its activities . A corporate fund shall be a legal entity established in the form of a joint-stock company .<n>No other legal entity may be spun-off from a corporate fund .
