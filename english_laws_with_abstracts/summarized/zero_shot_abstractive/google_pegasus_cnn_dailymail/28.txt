This Law defines the legal, economic, organisational and financial basis for government regulation of activities in the field of technology transfer .<n>It ensures the effective use of the scientific, technical and intellectual potential of Ukraine . Patent freedom to operate – it is possible to make free use of technologies in a certain state without the risk of violating intellectual property rights .<n>Technology market – a system of interconnections between consumers of technologies and their components and/or competing suppliers of these technologies and their components .<n> dual-use technologies – technologies that, in addition to civilian purposes, can be used for the development, manufacturing or use of weapons, military or special equipment .<n>technology transfer – the transfer of technology formalised by entering into a bilateral or multilateral agreement between individuals and/or legal entities . The legislation on government regulation of activities in the field of technology transfer is based on the Constitution of Ukraine .<n>Existing international treaties of Ukraine in the field of scientific and technical cooperation, innovation and technology transfer ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine . International cooperation in the field of technology transfer provides for: 1) conclusion of bilateral and multilateral international agreements of Ukraine on scientific and technical, technological, investment collaboration and cooperation .<n>The main task of the authorised body for the development and implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer shall be to ensure the development of state policy on technology transfer . The authorised body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer shall participate in the development and implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer . Powers of central executive authorities, the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine and branch Academies of Sciences in the field of technology transfer .<n>The authorised body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer shall interact with the relevant units of the National Police . The aim is to provide support with patenting of technologies and/or their components outside of Ukraine .<n>The aim is to ensure compliance with the requirements of international agreements of Ukraine on the man-made hazard prevention .<n>The aim is to contribute to attracting investment in the implementation of industry-specific comprehensive programmes of technological renewal of enterprises . Powers of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, local executive authorities, executive bodies of village, settlement, city, district in cities (if established)<n>Departments for transfer of technology, innovation and intellectual property can be established at enterprises, scientific institutions, organisations and higher education institutions .<n>Measures to prevent unfair competition, counterfeiting and unauthorised distribution of technologies and dissemination of confidential information . authorises body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer .<n>National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine and branch Academies of Sciences within their powers provide information and consulting support for technology transfer .<n> institutions and organisations of the state system of scientific, technical and patent information ensure: 1) development of information resources of scientific and technical, market and economic and patent information, taking into account all available sources of information about the components of technologies; 2) creation of regional branches of scientific and technical information bodies within the allowed number of employees and salary fund; 3) drafting of background reports on technologies and their components on behalf of the authorised body for the implementation of state policy in the field of technology transfer and by order of other technology transfer entities; 4) compiling of industry-specific and regional automated databases on technologies and their components developed for the budget funds, in particular on patent and market, scientific research, design, construction and testing work related to the development and use of technologies and/or their components; 5) holding of advertising and informational events, seminars, conferences, round tables, exhibitions, public discussion of information about technologies and their components in order to disseminate this information in the regions; 6) review, free of charge, by individuals and legal entities involved in the creation, use Procedure for transferring property rights to technologies developed for budget funds .<n>Property rights to technology and/or its components developed in the course of performing research and development works belong to contractors . Property rights to technologies developed or purchased for budget funds shall be mainly used within the territory of Ukraine .<n>Technology rights to technologies developed for the own funds of enterprises, scientific institutions, organisations and higher education institutions and individuals, and partially – for budget funds, the property rights to these components shall be distributed based on the agreement on their development and exercised under the terms of the agreement on their transfer .<n>The procedure for specification and execution of government contract for conducting scientific research and development, design and engineering works at the expense of budget funds shall be approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Foreign patenting of technologies or their components developed for budget funds shall be carried out at the expense of the funds of enterprises, scientific institutions, organisations and higher education institutions .<n>The purpose of conducting state expert examination is to determine the economic feasibility and utility for the state and society of implementing technologies and their components .<n>The procedure for conducting state expert examination of technologies shall be established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Provisions of this Article shall not apply to the export of technologies and/or their components regulated by the Law of Ukraine “On the State Control over International Transfers of Military and Dual-Use Commodities”. Procedure for approving the transfer of technologies and/or their components developed or purchased for budget funds shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>Funds received from the sale of technologies or technologies and equipment shall be used taking into account the specific conditions defined by this Law .<n>The procedure for approving the import of technologies or their components to Ukraine shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine . Patent and market research shall be conducted .<n>Technology transfer agreements shall include terms concerning: 1) license and its scope for use of technologies and their components; 2) territorial restrictions (prohibition to use the technology transferred under the agreement, its components within the territory not provided for in the agreement); 3) restrictions on the scope of application of technologies and their components . Civil Code of Ukraine: Specifics of technology transfer agreements .<n>It is not allowed to conclude technology transfer agreements that provide for: 1) import to Ukraine of technologies and/or their components that may harm the environment or human health; 2) making payments that significantly exceed the price of the technology and its components; 3) obligations to purchase raw materials, semi-finished products, equipment and its components from the person transferring the technology and/or its components that are not used in the manufacturing of the relevant products; 4) obligations regarding the pre-emptive sale of products manufactured using the technology to customers indicated by the person who transfers this technology and the involvement of personnel determined by it; 5) the right of the person transferring the technology to determine the sale or re-export price of products manufactured using this technology; 6) unjustified restrictions on the volume of production . Remuneration to authors of technologies and/or their components and persons who carry out their transfer .<n>An enterprise, scientific institution, organisation and higher education institution to which property rights to technologies or their components have been transferred shall pay remuneration to the authors of technologies at least once a year and no later than one month after the end of the budget year . Procedure and areas for using funds received as a result of the transfer of technologies developed for budget funds shall be determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>The state shall introduce measures to support technology transfer as a priority area of innovation activity .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall submit to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine proposals on bringing the laws of Ukraine in line with this Law .
