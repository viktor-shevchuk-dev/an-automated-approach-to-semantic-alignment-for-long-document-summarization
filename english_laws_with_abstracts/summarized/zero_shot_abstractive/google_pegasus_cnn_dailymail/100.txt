Law aims to introduce the legal regulation of all types of foreign economic activity in Ukraine .<n>Law defines audit, inspection of public reporting, accounting, primary documents and other information on financial and economic activities of business entities .<n>Gold and other precious metals in the form of ingots, plates and coins, as well as certificates, bonds, warrants and other securities denominated in gold, precious stones .<n> dumping – import of goods into the customs territory of the importing country at a price lower than the comparable price of similar goods in the exporting country . In doing so, the term re-export (re-export of goods) shall mean the sale to foreign business entities and export outside Ukraine of goods that were previously imported into the territory of Ukraine .<n>Foreign economic agreement (contract) – agreement between two or more foreign economic activity entities and their foreign counterparties aimed at establishing, changing or terminating their mutual rights and obligations in foreign economic activity .<n>Global quotas (contingents) – quotas set for goods not indicating specific countries (groups of countries) to which goods are exported or from which they are imported . Customs regulation – regulation of issues related to the establishment of duties and other taxes charged when goods are moved across the customs border of Ukraine .<n>international co-operation – the interaction of two or more business entities, at least one of which is foreign . Foreign business entities in Ukraine are subject to a number of laws and regulations .<n>These laws and regulations apply to all foreign economic activity .<n>Any foreign business entity in Ukraine is subject to these laws and regulations . Business entities of Ukraine and foreign business entities shall be guided by the following principles .<n> – exclusive right of the Ukrainian people to conduct autonomously and independently foreign economic activity in the territory of Ukraine .<n> – obligation of Ukraine to fulfil consistently all agreements and obligations of Ukraine in the field of international economic relations .<n> – right of foreign economic activity entities to voluntarily enter into foreign economic relations .<n> – exclusive right of ownership of foreign economic activity entities to all results of their foreign economic activity . Ukraine represented by its authorities, local authorities and administration bodies represented by foreign economic organisations established by them .<n>Citizens of Ukraine, foreign nationals and stateless persons who have civil legal capacity under the laws of Ukraine permanently reside in Ukraine .<n>Joint ventures between business entities of Ukraine and foreign business entities registered as such in Ukraine .<n>Other business entities in accordance with the laws of Ukraine . Intermediary operations in which the right of ownership of goods is not transferred to the intermediary shall be carried out without restrictions .<n>Entrepreneurial activity in the territory of Ukraine related to granting licences, patents, know-how, trademarks and other intangible property items by foreign business entities .<n>Exchange (barter) operations and other activities based on the forms of countertrade between foreign economic activity entities and foreign business entities . All foreign economic activity entities have equal right to conduct any types of foreign economic activity .<n>It is prohibited to seize the foreign economic activity results from the owners in any remunerative or gratuitous form without their consent .<n>All foreign economic activity entities of Ukraine shall be entitled to establish their representative offices in the territory of other states . Branches and representative offices of foreign banks shall be accredited by the National Bank of Ukraine .<n>It is prohibited to require a foreign business entity to re-register a previously registered representative office in the territory of Ukraine . Entities that are parties to a foreign economic agreement (contract) shall be capable to conclude an agreement (contract) under this and other laws of Ukraine .<n>Foreign economic agreement (contract) may be declared invalid by the court if it does not meet the requirements of the laws of Ukraine or international agreements of Ukraine . The foreign economic activity regulation in Ukraine shall be carried out by: – Ukraine as a state represented by its authorities within their competence .<n>It is prohibited to regulate foreign economic activity by acts and actions of state and non-state authorities not expressly provided for in this part . Most-favoured-nation regime shall be granted to business entities of other states based on a mutual agreement under the relevant agreements of Ukraine .<n>National regime means that imported goods originating from the WTO member states are subject to the regime not less favourable than for similar goods of Ukrainian origin .<n>State regulation of foreign economic activity Ukraine shall independently establish a system and structure of state regulation of foreign economic activity in its territory . State and its bodies shall not be entitled to interfere directly with the foreign economic activity of the entities of this activity .<n>The Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine shall be the highest body for state regulation of foreign economic activity . The National Bank of Ukraine may delegate the bank's functions assigned to it for the foreign economic activity of Ukraine .<n>The National Bank of Ukraine shall: – store and use the gold and foreign exchange reserve of Ukraine and other state valuables that ensure the solvency of Ukraine .<n>The National Bank of Ukraine may represent the interests of Ukraine in relations with central banks of other states, international banks and other financial and credit institutions . This Law and the Law of Ukraine “On Local Self-Government in Ukraine” provides for the regulation of foreign economic activity in Ukraine .<n>The Customs Authorities shall: – exercise customs control in Ukraine under the current laws of Ukraine .<n>The Antimonopoly Committee of Ukraine shall: – exercise control over the compliance with the legislation on protection of economic competition by foreign economic activity entities .<n>The Interdepartmental Commission on International Trade shall: – carry out operative state regulation of foreign economic activity in Ukraine under the legislation of Ukraine .<n>The state regulatory bodies for foreign economic activity of Ukraine may establish their territorial units (departments) if this follows from their competence defined by the laws of Ukraine . Ukraine shall establish and abolish independently taxes and benefits for foreign economic activity entities .<n>Tax rates shall be set and abolished by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine .<n>It is prohibited to apply simultaneously export-import tax and licensing and quotation regimes to the same goods . Customs measures provided for by Articles 29, 31 of this Law shall be applied to the foreign economic activity entities (foreign business entities) that carry out dumping .<n>The common customs tariff of Ukraine and the customs regulations of Ukraine shall be officially published in the publicly available mass media not later than 45 days before the date of their entry into force .<n>Ukraine shall be liable for the actions of its customs authorities under Article 34 of this Law . Crediting to foreign economic transactions in the currency effective in the territory of Ukraine and in foreign currency shall be carried out based on agreement and on the terms agreed between creditors and credit recipients in credit agreements (contracts)<n>All other credits other than settlement ones shall be secured by the property belonging to the debtor under the right of ownership and/or their non-property rights .<n>The amount of state credit (state guarantee of commercial credit) granted in the currency effective in the territory of Ukraine and/or in foreign currency to one foreign economic activity entity within one year shall not exceed 5 per cent of the relevant annual limits of state crediting of foreign economic operations . Insurance of foreign economic operations shall be carried out in Ukraine on a contractual basis and shall be voluntary unless otherwise provided for by the laws of Ukraine .<n>Insurance of export credits, foreign economic agreements (contracts) and investments shall be carried out by the Export Credit Agency .<n> Licensing of the export (import) of goods shall be carried out in the form of automatic or non-automatic licensing . Export licensing of goods shall be introduced in Ukraine in case of: significant imbalance of certain goods on the domestic market that are essential for life in Ukraine .<n> Import licensing of goods shall be introduced in Ukraine in case of: sharp deterioration in the balance of payments and external payments .<n>The need to protect human life, health, fauna or flora, environment, public morals, national wealth of artistic, historical or archaeological value or intellectual property rights protection . Licences issued by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of economic development .<n>As a rule, applicants shall apply to one executive authority to obtain a licence.<n>If it is necessary to obtain approval, it is possible to apply to several executive authorities, but no more than three . When submitting the application, documents and information that are deemed to be necessary to confirm the data stated in the application and the foreign economic agreement (contract) may be required .<n>If an automatic licensing regime is introduced, the deadline for issuing a licence shall not exceed 10 working days from the date of the receipt of the application . It is prohibited to restrict the import of goods on which certain quotas are set until such quotas are fully exhausted .<n>A licence shall be issued if the application and other submitted documents are executed in compliance with the requirements established by the legislation .<n>A fee, the amount of which shall be established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine taking into account the actual costs associated with the application of the licensing procedure, shall be charged for issuing a licence . The licensing procedures established by this Article shall not apply to the export (import) of goods specified in Article 20 of this Law .<n>The procedure of submission and consideration of applications shall be published in the official printed publications of Ukraine . This Article does not apply to operations of the National Bank of Ukraine carried out by it under the Law of Ukraine “On the National Bank of Ukraine”.<n> Conducting foreign economic operations by foreign economic activity entities without the relevant licences shall entail a fine of 10 per cent of the conducted operation value, converted into the currency of Ukraine to foreign currencies at the official exchange rate of hryvnia, established by the National Bank of Ukraine on the date of such operation. Technical regulations, pharmacological, sanitary, phytosanitary, veterinary and environmental measures, standards and requirements for goods imported into Ukraine shall be applied in accordance with the legislation of Ukraine .<n>The regime of previous import deposits shall be introduced by the National Bank of Ukraine to regulate the balance of payments of Ukraine . Foreign economic activity entities only authorised by Ukraine as a state may carry out export and import of weapons, ammunition, military equipment and special accessories .<n>The establishment of a state monopoly on export and import of other types of goods not specified in this Article shall not be allowed and may be appealed in court .<n>Foreign business entities shall enjoy equal rights and bear equal responsibilities with the foreign economic activity entities of Ukraine in case of holding a competitive tender for the state order placement . The National Bank of Ukraine (or, on its behalf, the bank for foreign economic activity of Ukraine) shall be provided with information on the amounts for the mandatory distribution of part of the proceeds in foreign currency .<n>Every foreign economic activity entity shall be entitled to obtain any information necessary for the conduction of this activity that is not a state or commercial secret . State bodies, agencies and institutions that issue regulatory acts shall be obliged to officially publish them in publicly available mass media of Ukraine .<n>Every foreign economic activity entity shall be entitled to obtain information directly from the public authorities under the Law of Ukraine .<n>The central executive authority in charge of shaping state policy in the field of statistics shall quarterly compile and publish statistical information on the state and structure of the foreign trade balance of Ukraine .<n>The Ministry of Finance of Ukraine shall compile information on the state and structure of external national debt . Economic relations of Ukraine with other states shall be governed by the relevant international agreements and regulations of international law .<n>Ukraine shall conclude, execute and denounce international agreements on foreign economic activity issues .<n>The legal status of business entities of other states in the territory of Ukraine shall be determined by the current laws of Ukraine . Ukraine and a customs union or economic group that has applied actions with discriminatory and/or unfriendly features against Ukraine shall consider and settle the dispute under the rules and procedures of such organisation .<n>In case of a positive decision on the existence of the fact (facts) of discriminatory actions, the decision on application of measures in response to discriminatory and/or unfriendly actions of other states, customs unions or economic groups shall be made by the state regulatory bodies for foreign economic activity subject to their competence .<n>In case of a positive response from the specified authorities, the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing state policy in the field of economic development shall form a working group to conduct relevant negotiations and prepare, if necessary, relevant international agreements of an interdepartmental or intergovernmental nature . The measures defined by part 3 of this Article shall be applied in response to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall decide on the application of measures in response to the actions of the aggressor and/or occupant state .<n>The decision to apply relevant measures in response to discriminatory and/or unfriendly actions of other states, customs unions or economic groups may be appealed in court within one month of the imposition of such measures . As a state, Ukraine shall not be liable for the actions of foreign economic activity entities .<n>If Ukraine participates in foreign economic activity as an entity of such activity, it shall be liable on a general and equal basis with the other foreign economic activity entities .<n>If a violation of this or related laws of Ukraine by foreign economic activity entities or foreign business entities has resulted in losses, loss of profit and/or moral damage to other such entities or the state, the entities who have violated the law shall be fully financially liable .<n>Criminal liability in the foreign economic activity shall be imposed only in cases provided for by the criminal legislation of Ukraine . As a state, Ukraine shall be fully financially liable before the foreign economic activity entities and foreign business entities .<n>As a state, Ukraine shall be liable for the actions specified in this Article with all of its property .<n>Any foreign economic activity entity or foreign business entity shall be entitled to file a claim against Ukraine as a state . Disputes arising between foreign economic activity entities, foreign business entities during such activity shall be considered by courts of Ukraine .<n>Interstate disputes which may arise as a result of the actions of Ukraine in the application of this Law shall be settled in accordance with the procedure agreed by the parties under the regulations of international law .
