This Law defines the legal and organisational framework for shaping and implementing priority branches of science and technology development in Ukraine .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall develop and implement a state targeted programme for forecasting scientific, technological and innovative development of Ukraine . Priority branches of science and technology development shall be discussed by the scientific community and submitted to the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine .<n>The list of priority thematic scientific research and technological development branches shall be compiled by the central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy in the field of science . The amounts of funds allocated for the implementation of each of the priority branches of science and technology development shall be determined annually by the law on the State Budget of Ukraine .<n>The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine shall organise systematic monitoring of priority branches of science and technology development implementation .
