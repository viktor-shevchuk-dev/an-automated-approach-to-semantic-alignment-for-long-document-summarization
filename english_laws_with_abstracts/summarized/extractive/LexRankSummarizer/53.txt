centralised wastewater disposal enterprise – a business entity operating centralised wastewater disposal facilities/systems; drinking water supply – activities related to the production, transportation and supply of drinking water to consumers of drinking water, protection of sources and drinking water supply systems; population equivalent of a settlement – the load of wastewater with organic substances subject to biodegradation, with a five-day biochemical oxygen consumption of 60 grams of oxygen per person per day; current accounts with a special mode of use for making settlements under investment programmes (hereinafter referred to as special accounts) – accounts of business entities in the field of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal, with the aim to accumulate and use funds solely for the implementation of investment programs in relevant area; drinking water bottling point – the place of spillage of drinking water (from tank trucks, wells, capturing, etc.) Objects of legal regulation in the field of drinking water and drinking water supply Objects of legal regulation in the field of drinking water and drinking water supply are public relations on the following issues: economic activities for centralised and non-centralised water supply and wastewater disposal; tariff formation for services of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal; rationing and licensing; control, accounting and monitoring; informing citizens about the quality of drinking water and drinking water supply; protection of sources and systems of drinking water supply and associated natural complexes; ensuring the rights of drinking water consumers. Principles of state policy in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal State policy in the field of drinking water, drinking water supply and wastewater disposal is based on the following principles: state administration and regulation of relations in the field of drinking water, drinking water supply and wastewater disposal; priority of drinking water supply over other types of special water use; guaranteed priority provision of drinking water for the population to ensure drinking, physiological, sanitary, hygienic and domestic needs; rational use of drinking water; scientifically grounded quality estimation of drinking water, standards for its consumption and the tariff formation for services of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal; approximation of technologies for the drinking water production, standards for wastewater treatment, as well as measuring instruments and methods of assessment in the relevant standards, technologies, means and methods adopted in the European Union; preventive nature of measures for the protection of sources and systems of drinking water supply; compliance with the optimal balance of the use of surface and subterranean waters for drinking water supply; obligatory state examination and assessment of the operational reserves of subterranean water for drinking water supply; obligatory assessment of the impact on the environment and state sanitary and epidemiological expertise of projects of economic, investment and other activities that may negatively affect the condition of sources and systems of drinking water supply; economic motivation of consumers for the rational use of drinking water; inevitability of liability in case of violation of the law in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal; compensation for damages caused as a result of violation of the law in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal; ensuring free access to information on the quality of drinking water, the condition of sources and systems of drinking water supply and wastewater disposal, the procedure of standards formation for drinking water supply and tariffs for centralised water supply and wastewater disposal services; observance of uniform rules and standards by all subjects of relations in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal; licensing of economic activities for centralised water supply and wastewater disposal; prohibition to disconnect drinking water supply and wastewater disposal facilities from the energy, gas, and heat supply systems as life support facilities of strategic importance. Guarantees of consumer rights in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal State guarantees the protection of consumer rights in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal by means of: provision of every person with drinking water of standard quality within the scientifically grounded standards of drinking water supply, depending on the area and living conditions; state support, regulation and supervision in the field of drinking water supply and wastewater disposal under this Law; implementation of organisational, scientific and technical, sanitary and epidemiological, environmental, economic, legal measures to improve the quality of drinking water, develop drinking water supply, protect sources and drinking water supply systems, rehabilitation of drinking water reserves; obligations of drinking water supply enterprises to take measures to ensure drinking water supply standards; meeting the needs of citizens in drinking water through the priority development of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal systems, the creation of reserve drinking water supply systems; obligations of central and local executive authorities, local authority, enterprises, institutions, organisations to comply with environmental requirements and sanitary legislation in the design, construction, reconstruction, putting into service and operation of water supply and wastewater disposal systems; monitoring compliance with legislation in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal, realisation of state monitoring of the condition of water and drinking water supply systems and wastewater disposal systems, assessing the impact on the environment and state sanitary and epidemiological expertise of economic and other activities related to the use of drinking water supply sources; ensuring compensation in accordance with the established procedure for harm caused to consumers of drinking water and/or services for centralised wastewater disposal by water supply enterprises and/or wastewater disposal companies due to violation of the law in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal; restrictions, temporary prohibition (stopping) through the established procedure of the functioning of drinking water supply systems that do not provide the standard quality of drinking water, as well as activities, negatively affects the quality of drinking water, disrupts the functioning of water supply and wastewater disposal systems; determination of ecological armor of drinking water supply. Powers of local authority in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal The powers of local authority in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal shall include: projects approval of urban planning programmes, master plans for the development of settlements, other urban planning documentation, subject to the legal requirements in the field of drinking water, drinking water supply and wastewater disposal; approval and implementation of local programmes in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal, participation in the development and implementation of relevant state and local programmes; consent provision for the placement of new facilities or reconstruction of existing ones within the relevant territory, whose activities may cause harm to sources and systems of drinking water supply and/or wastewater disposal systems; approval of local regulations for wastewater collection into centralised wastewater disposal systems of the relevant settlements; introduction of centralised wastewater disposal in settlements, the population equivalent of which is 2000 or more; determination of the population equivalent of a settlement, vulnerable and less vulnerable zones in accordance with the procedure for determining the population equivalent of a settlement and the criteria for determining vulnerable and less vulnerable zones, approved by the central executive authority that is in charge of state policy formation in the field of environmental protection; adoption of decisions on the state sanitary and epidemiological examination of economic projects that may negatively affect the quality of drinking water and drinking water supply systems; control over the quality of drinking water, use and protection of sources and systems of drinking water supply and wastewater disposal; providing information to citizens about the quality of drinking water and the condition of drinking water supply and wastewater disposal; tariff setting for centralised water supply and wastewater disposal services (except for tariffs for these services, which are established by the national commission that carries out government regulation in the fields of energy and utilities); restraint, temporarily banning the activities of enterprises in case of violation of the requirements of the law in the field of drinking water, water supply and wastewater disposal within the scope of their powers; establishment of rules for the use of water intake facilities designed to meet the needs of consumers in drinking water; establishment of sanitary protection zones for sources and facilities of centralised drinking water supply and sanitary protection zones for wastewater disposal facilities; restriction or prohibition of the drinking water use by enterprises for industrial purposes; coordination of investment programmes for water supply and wastewater disposal facilities that are in communal ownership; assistance in investment activities in the field of centralised water supply and wastewater disposal; solution of other issues in the field of drinking water and drinking water supply under the laws of Ukraine. Rights and obligations of drinking water supply and wastewater disposal enterprises Drinking water supply and wastewater disposal enterprises have the right to: develop and submit for approval to authorised authority tariffs and their justification for centralised water supply and wastewater disposal services; monitor the technical condition of the engineering equipment of buildings and structures, demand urgent elimination of leaks from water supply networks and equipment, ensure the installation, maintenance and replacement of commercial metering units for drinking water under the Law of Ukraine “On Commercial Metering of Heat Energy and Water Supply”; in the event that the consumer does not pay in full for the used drinking water, limit his drinking water supply to the level of the ecological armor of drinking water supply; issue permits and technical specifications for connecting consumers to centralised water supply and wastewater disposal systems, as well as for the reuse of treated wastewater and sludge, subject to compliance with the standards for maximum permissible concentrations and standards for maximum permissible discharge of pollutants; restrict or terminate the operation of centralised drinking water supply facilities in the event of the need for a prompt response to the deterioration of water quality in drinking water supply sources and the impossibility of bringing it up to the requirements of state sanitary norms and regulations, informing about such disconnection and its reasons local authorities, local executive authorities and bodies of state sanitary and epidemiological service, as well as consumers. Providing sanitary protection Sanitary protection in the field of drinking water and drinking water supply shall be ensured by: the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of environmental safety – to monitor compliance with the normes and regulations, regulating the discharge of wastewater into water bodies, and the implementation of protective measures to ensure the water quality correspondence to the passport of the water body; the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of water management – on the certification of drinking water supply sources, monitoring water quality indicators in the control sections of the water body at the points of water withdrawal, taking into account the requirements of sanitary norms and regulations for drinking water supply and drinking water sources; legal entities and individuals, whose activities affect the state of sources and facilities of water supply and wastewater disposal by means of the implementation of measures to protect water from pollution, clogging and depletion at their own expense and ensure the safety of production and supply of drinking water.
