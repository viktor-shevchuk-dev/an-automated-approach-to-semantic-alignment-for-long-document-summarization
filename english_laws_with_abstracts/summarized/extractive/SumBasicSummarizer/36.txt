Scope of the Law This law shall be applied to all international treaties of Ukraine regulated by the norms of international law and to those concluded in accordance with the Constitution of Ukraine and the requirements of this Law. Article 2. 4. International agreements of Ukraine on matters falling within the scope of powers of ministries, other central executive authorities and state collegial bodies are interdepartmental. Ratification of international treaties of Ukraine 1. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine submits these proposals to the President of Ukraine or the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, respectively. Prolongation of an international treaty of Ukraine An international treaty of Ukraine may be prolonged on the terms determined by the international treaty itself. President of Ukraine
