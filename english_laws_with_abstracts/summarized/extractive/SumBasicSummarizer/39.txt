The provisions of this Law shall apply to all business entities, regardless of ownership and type of activity. Article 2. Customers of ecological audit can be the interested central and local executive authorities, local governments, other legal entities and individuals. The criteria of the ecological audit shall be defined in the ecological audit contract. Cases of the ecological audit shall be used by an environmental auditor to determine the compliance of the ecological audit entity with the requirements of Ukrainian legislation on environmental protection and other criteria of the ecological audit. Legal reasons for ecological auditing The ecological audit shall be carried out by virtue of an agreement between the customer and the provider concluded in accordance with the requirements of this Law and other regulatory acts.
