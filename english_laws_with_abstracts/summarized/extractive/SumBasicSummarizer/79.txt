State registration of medicinal products Medicinal products shall be allowed for use in Ukraine after their state registration, except for cases stipulated by this Law. Fee for state registration of a medicinal product subject to procurement based on the results of a procurement procedure conducted by a specialised procurement organisation for implementation of a procurement agreement between the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare and the relevant specialised procurement organisation shall not be paid. For registered medicinal product, the applicant shall be issued a certificate specifying the period of validity during which the medicinal product shall be authorised for use in Ukraine. The requirements of parts one, two, three and five of Article 12 of this Law shall not apply to vaccines or other medical immunobiological preparations registered for emergency medical use under the procedure specified in this Article. The cost of selected samples and their quality control shall be included in the manufacturing expenses of the business entities where these samples have been collected.
