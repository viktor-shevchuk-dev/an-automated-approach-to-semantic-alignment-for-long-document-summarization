It is allowed to use up to 15 per cent of other grape varieties of the same botanical species. The term “ethyl alcohol” shall be used in the meaning given in the Law of Ukraine “On the State Regulation of Production and Circulation of Ethyl Alcohol, Cognac and Fruit Alcohols, Alcoholic Beverages and Tobacco Products”. Article 3. Names of other wines and new brands shall be provided in accordance with the requirements of the regulation approved by the central executive authority in charge of shaping state farm policy, policy in the field of agriculture. Such wine materials may be used only for the production of ordinary dry non-varietal table wines. The strength of sparkling champagne wines shall be not less than 10.5 volume per cent. Cognacs of Ukraine may be produced under their own name.
