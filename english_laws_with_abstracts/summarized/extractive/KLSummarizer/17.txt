The submission on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or on cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary must contain circumstances and be accompanied by documents confirming the existence of grounds for cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or on cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary. A decision on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, or on cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary shall be made by a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection following the submission of authorised officers of the central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection within one month after the submission and his/her personal file are received. Based on fully-fledged study and assessment of all the documents and materials, a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall decide to cease or deprive a person of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, as well as to cancel the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, or on the absence of grounds for cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection or cancelling the decision on recognising a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection. The decision of a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall be sent to the relevant officers of such a central executive authority within three business days the date it is made, along with the personal file of a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection. A central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall send a person in whose respect a decision on cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection was made, or a decision on recognising a person as a refugee or a person in need of subsidiary protection was cancelled, shall or hand to such a person a notification specifying the reasons for such a decision and explaining the appeal procedure within seven business days. If the court decides to grant the appeal of a person against denial to recognise a person as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, cessation or deprivation of the refugee status or subsidiary protection, a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection shall make a respective decision within seven business days after receiving such a decision. The decision on deprivation of temporary protection in respect of a person shall be made by a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection.
