LAW OF UKRAINE This Law sets out the fundamentals of state policy in the field of scientific and technical information, the procedure for its formation and implementation in the interests of scientific and technical, economic and social progress of the state. The purpose of the Law is to create a legal framework for obtaining and using scientific and technical information. This Law regulates the legal and economic relations between citizens, legal entities and the state that arise when creating, obtaining, using, and distributing scientific and technical information, as well as determines legal forms of international cooperation in this field. This Law applies to enterprises, institutions, organisations regardless of their form of ownership and citizens with the right to obtain, use, and disseminate scientific and technical information. This Law does not apply to information containing state secrets and other legally protected secrets. Section I GENERAL PROVISIONS Article 1. 3322-XII
