LAW OF UKRAINE This law defines the legal basis for protecting business entities and consumers from unfair competition. The law is aimed at establishing, developing and ensuring trade and other fair practices of competition in the implementation of economic activities in the conditions of market relations. Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS Article 1. Unfair competition Unfair competition shall be understood as any actions performed in the course of competition that are counter to trade and other fair customs in business activities. In particular, actions stipulated by Chapters 2–4 of this Law shall be qualified as unfair competition. The terms used for the purposes of this Law are determined by the Law of Ukraine “On Protection of Economic Competition”. City of Kyiv 7 June 1996
