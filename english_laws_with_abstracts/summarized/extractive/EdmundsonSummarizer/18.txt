LAW OF UKRAINE Article 1. Approve the List of Permit Documents in the Economic Sector (hereinafter referred to as the List) under the Annexe (attached). The term “permit document” is used in the meaning given in the Law of Ukraine “On the Permit System in the Field of Economic Activity”. It is forbidden to require business entities to obtain permit documents that are not included in the List approved by this Law. Permit documents whose issuance is required by international treaties consent to be binding by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, that are not included in the List approved by this Law, are issued under the international treaties. Issuance of permit documents in the areas of state export control, state regulation of financial services markets, protection of state secrets (activities related to state secrets), protection of economic competition and use of nuclear energy is carried out under the Laws governing relations in these areas. 3392-VI
