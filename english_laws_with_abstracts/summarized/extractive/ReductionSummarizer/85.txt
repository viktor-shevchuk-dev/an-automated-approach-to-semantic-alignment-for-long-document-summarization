The purpose and objectives of the public administration in the field of transport Public administration in the field of transport should provide: timely, comprehensive and high-quality satisfaction of the population's and public's production needs in transportation and needs of the defence of Ukraine; advocacy of citizens' rights during their transport service; safe operation of transport; compliance with the required rates and proportions of national transportation system development; advocacy of Ukraine's economic interests and the legitimate interests of transport companies and organisations and consumers of transport services; creating equal conditions for the development of transport company business activities; limiting monopolism and developing and competition; coordinating various types of transport operation; licensing of certain types of activities in the field of transport; protecting the environment from the harmful effects of transport. Responsibilities and rights of transport companies Transport companies are obligated to: meet the transportation needs of citizens and organisations; deliver passenger service during long haul transportation with good-quality drinking water, food, the possibility to fulfil other biological needs; provide high-quality and timely transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage, mail; execute state tasks (contracts) to meet the needs of defence and security of Ukraine; ensure transportation safety; ensure safe transportation conditions; prevent accidents and incidents, eliminate causes of industrial injuries; protect the environment from the harmful effects of transport; provide citizens' rights to benefits for transport use. Officials of the central executive body in the charge of implementing the state policy in the field of land transport safety, and the central executive body in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of safety in maritime and river transport, have the right to: gain unimpeded access to all vehicles and facilities of business entities, legal entities and individuals (in road transport – as well as individuals – business entities) in order to verify compliance with the legislation and international agreements of Ukraine on transport safety issues; check during working hours of business entities, legal entities and individuals on issues within their competence; make demands to business entities, individuals and legal entities requirements for the implementation of measures on transport safety required by the legislation; receive written or oral explanations, conclusions of expert examinations, audits, materials and information on relevant issues from business entities, legal entities and individuals; draw up certificates of inspection of business entities, legal entities and individuals; according to the established procedure issue directions obligatory for implementation on the elimination of infringements and shortcomings regarding infringement of rules of safety in transport; prohibit the operation of vehicles (except automobile transport), work execution and services provision; in accordance with the procedure established by the legislation submit materials on violation of requirements of regulatory legislative acts on ensuring traffic safety to the corresponding authorities to address the issue of bringing the party at fault to administrative or criminal responsibility; involve in the inspections representatives of central and local executive authorities, public organisations, research, development and design institutions, expert organisations, etc. Powers of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy on land transport safety Central executive authority in charge of shaping the state policy on land transport safety: keeps records and carries out the analysis of causes of catastrophes, accidents, traffic incidents in automobile, urban electric, rail transport; develops preventive measures to prevent catastrophes, accidents, traffic incidents in automobile, urban electric, rail transport and monitors their implementation; keeps records of fires on automobile, urban electric, railway transport; carries out the supervision of compliance with the requirements for the prevention of environmental pollution by automobile, rail transport; carries out the state supervision (monitoring) over compliance with the requirements of the legislation, regulations in automobile, urban electric, railway transport; prepares proposals for the appointment of conformity assessment bodies for conformity assessment in the field of automobile, urban electric, railway transport, participates in the development of draft technical regulations and other legislative acts on technical regulation in this area, organises training and recurrent training of conformity assessment specialists; carries out technical investigation of traffic incidents, catastrophes, accidents, occurrences in automobile, urban electric, railway transport; exercises other powers defined by the laws and assigned to it by the President of Ukraine. Powers of the central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport safety Central executive authority in charge of implementing the state policy in the field of maritime and river transport safety: keeps records and carries out analysis the causes of catastrophes, accidents, incidents in sea and river transport; develops preventing measures against catastrophes, accidents and incidents in maritime and river transport and monitors its implementation; keeps records of fires in maritime and river transport; performs monitoring of the conclusion of contracts of compulsory liability insurance of entities of transportation of dangerous goods by maritime and river transport; performs supervision of compliance with the requirements for the prevention of environmental pollution by maritime and river transport; performs state supervision (monitoring) of compliance with legislation requirements in maritime and river transport; prepares proposals for the appointment of conformity assessment authorities for conformity assessment in the field of maritime and river transport, participates in the development of draft technical regulations and other legislative acts on technical regulation in this area, organises training and recurrent training of certification specialists compliance; keeps a log book of vessels (except fishing vessels) registered in the State Ship Register of Ukraine and the Ship book of Ukraine, publishes these data on its official website; performs state monitoring and supervision over compliance with the legislation on vessel navigation on, in sea and river ports, territorial and internal waters, on inland waterways of Ukraine; performs safety monitoring of transportation of cargo, passengers and baggage on maritime and river vessels; performs monitoring and supervision over compliance with the rules of use of maritime and river transport, public order protection and traffic safety in maritime and river transport, maintenance and operation of small size vessels bases (facilities), as well as compliance with the rules on the safety of inland waterway vessels ways and on providing the safety of cargo shipped by maritime and river transport; performs monitoring of navigation and hydrographic support of navigation in the territorial sea, inland sea waters and on inland waterways of Ukraine; registers vessels in the State Ship Register of Ukraine with the issuance of a certificate of navigation under the State Flag of Ukraine (ship patent) and registers vessels in the Ship Book of Ukraine with the issuance of a vessel ticket and issues certificates of exclusion of relevant vessels from the State Ship Register of Ukraine and the Ship Book of Ukraine (except fishing vessels) in accordance with the procedure specified by legislation; performs monitoring over the implementation of international agreements of Ukraine on shipping safety and prevention of environmental pollution from vessels flying the State Flag of Ukraine, regardless of ownership (except for fishing vessels); performs Port State Control over the compliance of vessels calling at the ports of Ukraine, the territorial sea and inland waters, regardless of their flag, with maritime safety standards and prevention of environmental pollution from vessels; inspects for readiness of emergency rescue services, general security services of the departmental fire protection on maritime and river transport to carry out operations to prevent and eliminate the consequences of accidents, fires, terrorist acts, piracy, disasters and other incidents; performs monitoring over the training, recurrent training, professional development and graduation of personnel of maritime, river and small-sized vessels, employees of maritime and river transport coastal enterprises, whose work is related to the navigation safety and prevention of environmental pollution from ships; issues Navigator licence of a small-sized (small) vessel, a merchant vessel, which is allowed to sail on navigable inland waterways of Ukraine, state sear pilot licence, state river pilot licence, vessel traffic service operator pilot licence; agrees on the rules of small-sized vessels navigation of on water bodies; organises training and testing of knowledge of officials whose functional responsibilities include the organisation of work to ensure the safety of navigation on maritime and river transport and state supervision; performs registration small-sized (small) vessels stationing bases and posting the information on its official website; performs supervision over the operation of the vessel traffic service and pilotage service; performs the state supervision over compliance with rules of registration of operations with hazardous substances on vessels, sea installations; issues the synopsis of the vessel; performs monitoring of functioning of radio navigation and surveillance systems operation of the marine mobile service, including the Global Maritime Distress Communication System and to provide safety; performs monitoring over emergency rescue operations, search and rescue at sea in the area of responsibility of Ukraine; performs monitoring and supervision over ensuring the functioning of the system remote identification and monitoring over the location of vessels under the State Flag of Ukraine; performs the general management and control over the graduation of navy specialists; registers foreign navigation lines and posts relevant information on its official website; issues ship documents in accordance with the legislation; performs the functions of an expert organisation on the safety of navigation, prevention of environmental pollution from ships, provides expert opinions on these issues on draft rules, regulations, plans and projects for construction, reconstruction and modernisation of maritime and river transport, including waterways, ports, hydraulic engineering structures of the water transport complex of various functional purposes, used to ensure the safety of navigation, navigable locks, bridges, underwater and surface crossings, vessel stationing bases, shore communication facilities, navigation, surveillance, etc. Unified transport system of Ukraine The unified transport system of Ukraine comprises of: general use transport (railway, maritime, river, automobile and air service, as well as urban electric transport, including subway); industrial railway transport; departmental transport; pipeline transport; general use communication lines. Railway transport and its contents Railway transport comprises railway transport of enterprises that carry out the transportation of passengers, cargo, baggage, mail, railway transport rolling stock, railway communication lines as well as industrial, construction, trading and supply enterprises, educational institutions, technical schools, children's preschool institutions, healthcare, physical culture and sport, culture institutions; planning and design organisations, industrial railway transport companies and other enterprises, institutions and organisations regardless of ownership type in charge of its operations and development.
