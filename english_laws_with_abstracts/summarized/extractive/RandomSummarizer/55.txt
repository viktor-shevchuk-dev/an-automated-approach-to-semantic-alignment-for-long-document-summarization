The user of oil and gas potential subsoil, who has a special permit for oil and gas production, shall have the right to search for and explore new oil and gas deposits within the subsoil site provided to him/her. An oil and gas potential subsoil user who has duly complied with the requirements and conditions provided for in the special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil and the oil and gas potential subsoil use agreement shall have the right to extend the validity of this permit. Article 24. Revocation of the special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil shall be carried out by the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of geological study and rational use of subsoil, and in the cases provided for in paragraphs four - seven, part one of this Article, in case of disagreement of the potential subsoil user it shall be carried out in court. In case of refusal of the user of oil and gas potential subsoil or failure to receive a response from him/her within a month, the central government body, implementing public policy in the field of geological study and rational use of subsoil shall announce a tender for a special permit for geological study of oil and gas potential subsoil, including research and development at this plot. Possession and disposal of geological information on oil and gas potential of subsoil Geological information obtained (purchased) at the expense of the State Budget of Ukraine is a state property and shall be used in accordance with the current legislation of Ukraine.
