Barter operation in the sphere of foreign economic activity is one of the types of export and import operations executed by a barter agreement or an agreement with a mixed form of payment envisaging partial payment of export (import) supplies in kind between the subject of foreign economic activity of Ukraine and a foreign business entity which provides for a balanced exchange of goods, works, services in any combination, not mediated by the movement of funds in cash or non-cash form. Goods imported under a barter agreement shall be subject to import into the customs territory of Ukraine within the terms specified in such agreement, but not later than 180 calendar days from the date of customs clearance (the date of execution of the customs declaration for export) of goods actually exported under the barter agreement, and in the case of export under a barter agreement of works and services – from the date of signing the act or other document confirming the performance of works, delivery of services. The date of import of goods under a barter agreement into the customs territory of Ukraine shall be considered to be the date of their customs clearance (the date of execution of the customs declaration for import), and in the case of import under a barter agreement of works or services – the date of signing an act or other document confirming the performance of works, delivery of services; 4. In the case when instead of supplies of goods (works, services) imported under a barter agreement, a foreign counterparty fulfils its obligations by means of transferring funds to the account of a subject of foreign economic activity of Ukraine - a party to the agreement, the basis for removing from the control of the customs body the issue of the import part under the barter agreement shall be the submission by the specified subject to the customs body of a certificate of the authorized bank on the receipt of funds into the account of this agreement within the time limits stipulated by the legislation of Ukraine for an amount equivalent to the value of goods (works, services) specified in the agreement as well as copies of additional agreements that determine the change of the nature of the agreement. The breach by a subject of foreign economic activity of Ukraine of the terms of import of goods (performance of works, delivery of services) imported under a barter agreement, stipulated by part one of Article 2 of this Law, entails the recovery of default interest per each day of delay in the amount of 0.3 per cent of the value of non-received goods (works, services) imported under a barter agreement. Subjects of foreign economic activity of Ukraine who have conducted the export or import of works or services under a barter agreement shall be obliged to notify the customs authorities (if goods are imported or exported under such an agreement) or tax authorities (if works or services are imported or exported under such an agreement) of the fact of exporting goods (works, services) within five working days from the date of signing an act or other document certifying the performance of works or services. If the export of goods (works, services) under a barter agreement is carried out before the enactment of this Law, the terms of import to the customs territory of Ukraine of goods (works, services) imported under a barter agreement shall not be renewed or interrupted.
