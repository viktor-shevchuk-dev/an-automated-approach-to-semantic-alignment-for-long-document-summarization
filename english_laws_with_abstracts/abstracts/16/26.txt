According to Article 17 of the Law, the effective term of the concession agreement for an object of the fuel and energy complex is determined by the parties and may be no less than ten years and no more than fifty. The object of the fuel and energy complex must be transferred to the concessionary no later than six months after the agreement has been concluded. A protocol of acceptance and delivery is drawn up by the representatives of the concessionary and the concessioner to certify the transfer of the object.
