The Code envisages setting for the employees the labor norms - norms of production, time, service and quantity. The labor norms are set for the employees according to the reached level of technic, technology, organization of production and labor. Introduction, change and review of labor norms are performed by the owner upon agreement with the representatives of trade union. The labor norms are set for an indefinite period and operate until the moment of their review. The owner shall notify the employees of the introduction of new and change of current labor norms at least one month before.
