The article 10 of the Law, which determines the peculiarities of transfer of technologies to enterprises, institutions, organizations of Ukraine, the article 12 of the Law ”State Inspection of Technologies”, article 13 ”State Registration of the Agreement on Transfer of Technologies” and article 19 ”Royalties to the Authors of Technologies and Persons performing their transfer” enters into force six month after this Law enters into force.  The article 20 of the Law ”Using the Funds obtained as a result of transfer of technologies developed or acquired using the funds of the State Budget of Ukraine” and article 22 of the Law that regulates subsidizing of transfer of technologies enter into force January 1, 2007.
