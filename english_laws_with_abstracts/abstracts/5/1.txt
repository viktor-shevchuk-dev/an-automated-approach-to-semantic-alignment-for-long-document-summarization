Article 1 of the Law provides definitions of terms used in it. For example, aquaculture (fishery) is agricultural activity aimed at artificial breeding, keeping and growing aquatic cultures under completely or partially controlled conditions, to produce and sell agricultural products (aquaculture product), produce feed, restore biological resources; selective breeding work, introduction, relocation, acclimatization and re-acclimatization of aquatic organisms, replenishing stocks of water biological resources, preserving their biodiversity, and providing recreational services.
