Rights and obligations of passengers, the procedure for making trips and the procedure for paying the fair shall be determined by the Rules for Using Municipal Electric Transport that shall be approved within the limits of the relevant powers by the central body of executive power in the area of the residential services sector and the central body of executive power in the area of transport that shall guarantee passengers the right to receive quality and safe transport services according to the present Law, timely and reliable information regarding transport services, compensation for damaged inflicted at the time of using municipal electric transport according to the legislation.
