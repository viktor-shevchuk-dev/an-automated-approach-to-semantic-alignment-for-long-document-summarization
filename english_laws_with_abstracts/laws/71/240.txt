3. If the market operator believes or has grounds to believe that the foodstuffs it has imported (transferred) into the customs territory of Ukraine, produced, processed or has in circulation do not meet the safety requirements, it shall immediately initiate the withdrawal of such foodstuffs from the circulation and, within the period not exceeding two working days, inform in writing the competent authority of the detected non-compliance. If such foodstuffs could potentially reach consumers, the market operator shall inform consumers of the reasons for their withdrawal and, where necessary, recall foodstuffs if other measures are insufficient to achieve an adequate level of consumer health protection.
