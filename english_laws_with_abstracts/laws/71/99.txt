93) “food for special medical purposes” means a specially designed and produced product that is consumed under medical supervision. This product is intended to partially or completely replace the routine diet of patients with a limited, weakened, or impaired ability to take, digest, or absorb routine foodstuffs or certain nutrients contained therein or their metabolites.
