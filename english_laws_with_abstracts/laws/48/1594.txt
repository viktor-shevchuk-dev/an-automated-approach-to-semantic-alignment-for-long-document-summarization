4. If the amount of penalties is not determined by law, the sanctions shall be applied in the amount stipulated in the agreement. At the same time, the amount of sanctions may be established by the agreement as an interest of the amount of the outstanding part of the obligation or in a certain, defined amount of money, or as an interest of the amount of an obligation, regardless of the degree of its fulfillment, or as a multiple of the value of goods (works, services).
