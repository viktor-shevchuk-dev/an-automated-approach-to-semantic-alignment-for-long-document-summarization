Under a major construction contracting agreement, one party (a contractor) shall on its own and by its own means upon an order of the other party (a customer) construct and commission to a customer within the established time period specified in the agreement a facility in accordance with the design and estimate documentation or perform construction and other works stipulated in the agreement, and a customer shall submit an approved design and estimate documentation to a contractor, provide it with the construction site, accept the completed construction facilities and pay for them.
