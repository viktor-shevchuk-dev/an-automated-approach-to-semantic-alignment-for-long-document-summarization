Economic activity on the state border of Ukraine (navigation, use of water bodies for timber rafting and other types of water use, construction of hydraulic structures, execution of other works in the internal waters of Ukraine, use of land, forests, wildlife items, geological exploration and other economic activities) shall be conducted with due account of the specific aspects of the regime of the state border of Ukraine in accordance with the legislation of Ukraine and current international treaties, ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine.
