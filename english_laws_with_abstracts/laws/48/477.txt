State-owned enterprises, including economic companies (except banks), in the authorised capital of which the state owns fifty per cent or more of shares (equities) shall obtain domestic long-term (for more than 1 year) and external credit facilities (loans), provide guarantees or be guarantors of such obligations in co-ordination with the central executive body that implements state financial policy, attract domestic short-term (for up to 1 year) credit facilities (loans), provide guarantees or be guarantors of such obligations in coordination with the executive body that manages state property. The procedure for these co-ordinations shall be established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
