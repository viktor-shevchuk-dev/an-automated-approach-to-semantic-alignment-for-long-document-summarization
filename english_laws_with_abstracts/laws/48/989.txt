5. A concern shall be deemed a statutory enterprise association and other organisations, based on their financial dependence on one or a group of the association’s stakeholders, with the centralisation of functions of scientific, technical and industrial development, investment, financial, foreign economic and other activities. Concern’s stakeholders shall endow it with part of their powers, including the right to act on their behalf in relations with government authorities, other enterprises and organisations. Concern’s stakeholders shall not be members of another concern at the same time.
