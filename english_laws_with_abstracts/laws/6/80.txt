A non-profit organisation, as well as a charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions), or a territorial community shall use the funds raised through public fundraising with the use of a charitable telecommunication message, exclusively for the purposes specified in the agreement with the telecommunications operator. The use of such funds for financing administrative and other expenses of a non-profit organisation, including charitable organisation (except for political parties and credit unions), or a territorial community, which is not provided for in the agreement, shall be prohibited.
