1. Persons serving their sentences in correctional facilities and juvenile correctional facilities shall be provided with adequate living conditions that comply with the sanitary and hygienic standards. As a rule, convicts are kept in block-type premises. The minimum living space allowance per convict shall be four square metres; in medical treatment facilities at correctional facilities, in correctional facilities designed to detain and treat convicts with tuberculosis, in the in-patient department, the living space allowance per convict shall be five square metres.
