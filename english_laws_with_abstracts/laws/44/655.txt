3. Changing the detention conditions of the convict by transferring them to a correctional facility of another security level shall be carried out by the central executive authority implementing the state penal policy, upon submission by the administration of the correctional facility agreed with the head of the department (division) of the central executive authority implementing the state penal policy in the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, oblast, the city of Kyiv and Kyiv oblast. If such a submission provides for the transfer of the convict to a penitentiary institution with a high level of security, it shall be agreed upon by the monitoring committee.
