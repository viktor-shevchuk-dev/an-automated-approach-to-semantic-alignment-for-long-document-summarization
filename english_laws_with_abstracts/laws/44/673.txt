1. Regime in correctional facilities and juvenile correctional facilities shall mean the procedure established by law and other regulatory legal acts for the execution and serving of a sentence, which ensures the isolation of convicts; constant supervision over them; performance of the duties imposed on them; exercise of their rights and legitimate interests; safety of convicts and staff; separate detention of different categories of convicts; different detention conditions for convicts depending on the type of facility; and changes in the detention conditions for convicts.
