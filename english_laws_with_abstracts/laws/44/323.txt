Upon receipt of a copy of the sentence the commander of the military unit shall issue a corresponding order specifying the amount of deductions to the revenue of the state from the monetary allowance of the convicted service person, the period during which they cannot be promoted to a higher position, to a higher military rank, as well as the period which will not be counted towards their length of the service for the assignment of the next military rank.
