In the event when in the exclusive (maritime) economic zone of Ukraine there have been collisions of ships, grounding or other maritime accident, or actions related to such accident that may lead to serious negative consequences for the coastline and to the related interests of Ukraine (including fishery), in accordance with the norms of international law the specially authorised bodies of Ukraine have the right, to take protective measures against pollution or against the threat of its contamination, taking into account the size of actual or possible damage.
