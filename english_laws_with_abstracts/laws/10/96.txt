1. Border crossing points of the state border of Ukraine shall be opened on the territory of seaports and marine terminals, through which international communication is carried out, by the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and in the manner prescribed by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine. The boundaries of the border crossing points on the territory of seaports shall be determined by the Ukrainian Sea Ports Authority. The boundaries of the border crossing points on the territory of marine terminals shall be determined by the owners of marine terminals in agreement with the state border guard authority and the customs authority.
