“hydraulic structures of seaports (hydraulic structures) are engineering structures (port water, mooring berths, piers, other types of berthing structures, moles, dams, breakwaters, other coastal protection structures, underwater structures of man-made and natural origin, including channels, operational water areas of berths, anchoring berths) located within the territory and water area of the seaport and designed to ensure the safety of navigation, manoeuvring and berthing of vessels”;
