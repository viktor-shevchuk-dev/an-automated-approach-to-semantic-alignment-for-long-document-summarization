The term for receiving a document on the payment of the state duty for certificate issuance and the fee for publication on certificate issuance shall be prolonged but not more than for six months, provided that a relevant request is submitted and the fee for submission thereof is paid prior to the expiry of the specified term. If the term for receiving a document on the payment of the state duty for certificate issuance and the fee for publication on certificate issuance is missed for serious reasons, rights of the applicant in respect of the application shall be restored in the event that a relevant request is submitted within six months upon expiry of the specified term together with the document on the payment of the state duty for certificate issuance and the fee for publication on certificate issuance and the fee for submitting the request.
