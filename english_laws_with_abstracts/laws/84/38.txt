The customs authorities shall carry out relevant customs formalities required for the release into free circulation on the customs territory of Ukraine of pesticides and agrochemicals based on the List of agrochemicals permitted for import into the customs territory of Ukraine, production, trade, application and advertising without their state registration according to the Annex to this Law, as well as relevant permits or information on state registration of pesticides and agrochemicals received from the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of environmental protection, with the use of a single-window system according to the Customs Code of Ukraine.
