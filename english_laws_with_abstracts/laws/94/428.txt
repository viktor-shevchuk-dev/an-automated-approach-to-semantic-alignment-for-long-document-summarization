If after three years from the date of state registration or the date when the use of a variety was terminated, a variety in Ukraine is not used or is used to an insignificant extent and if any person may refer to the owner of the variety with a proposal to conclude a licence agreement and the owner of the variety refuses to do so without a valid reason, that person may refer the matter to court and may claim the grant of a compulsory licence for use of the variety.
