If an employment agreement or the agreement of order between the employer and the breeder provides for the right to submit an application only by the employer, the breeder shall, unless otherwise provided for in the employment agreement (contract) or the agreement of order on the creation of variety, within 60 days from the date of receipt of the notification from the author of the variety to submit to the Competent Authority an application for a variety or to transfer the right to submit an application to another person or to decide on keeping information about the variety confidential or as a trade secret. Within the same term, the employer shall enter into a written agreement with the author of the variety on the amount and terms of payment of fair remuneration (royalties) based on the economic value of the variety and its other benefits unless otherwise provided for in the employment agreement (contract). The Competent Authority may establish minimum rates for specified remuneration.
