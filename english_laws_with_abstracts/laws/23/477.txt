10. Monitoring of the effectiveness of the applied ban to place the products on the market shall consist in tracking by the market supervision authorities of compliance with such a ban in the retail network. If the results of this monitoring show that the products banned to be placed on the market are placed on the market, the market supervision authority shall require from a manufacturer of these products to withdraw them from circulation and/or recall them. In case of establishing the fact of regular placement on the market of the products banned to be placed on the market, the market supervision authority shall consider the issue of inspecting the characteristics of such products at the manufacturer.
