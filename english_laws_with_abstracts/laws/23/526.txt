If the market supervision authority has established that these products do not meet the established requirements, having received a notification about the suspension of customs clearance of the products, it shall take appropriate measures provided for herein, which may include a ban to put such products into circulation. If a decision on a ban to put the products into circulation is made, the relevant market supervision authority shall apply to the customs authority that suspended customs clearance with a request not to release such products for free circulation and introduce in the invoice or any other relevant document accompanying these products when they are put into circulation, or to the electronic information system of the customs authorities, the note to read as follows: “Products do not meet the established requirements — release for free circulation is prohibited in accordance with the Law of Ukraine “On State Market Supervision and Control of Non-Food Products”.
