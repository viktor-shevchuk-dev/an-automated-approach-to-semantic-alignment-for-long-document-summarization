If technical regulations establish special requirements for market supervision in relation to specific product types, aimed at achieving the same goals as the provisions of this Law, the provisions of this Law shall apply only to those aspects of market supervision that are not covered by such special requirements. If the laws of Ukraine and regulatory acts issued in accordance therewith establish special requirements for control of products, the provisions of this Law shall apply only to those aspects of control of products that are not covered by these special requirements.
