5. The customs authority shall notify the market supervision authority, which areas of responsibility relate to market supervision of the relevant type of products, of the suspension of customs clearance of such products. The notification of the customs authority shall indicate: products which customs clearance has been suspended, reasons and period for which customs clearance has been suspended, name and location of a manufacturer, an authorised representative (if any) and an importer of the products, as well as other necessary information certifying the fact of violation.
