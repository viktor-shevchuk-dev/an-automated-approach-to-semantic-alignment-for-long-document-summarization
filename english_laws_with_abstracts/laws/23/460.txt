15. If it is necessary to take urgent restrictive (corrective) measures, in order to prevent or avoid risks to the public interest, the market supervision authority may make a decision on appropriate restrictive (corrective) measures without submitting (sending) a draft of this decision to a business entity. In this case, the business entity affected by the decision shall have the right to submit (send) its explanations, objections and/or information to such a decision at any time after its adoption, but before the expiration of the specified period for the execution of such a decision. The market supervision authority shall immediately consider the explanations, objections and information received from a business entity and, following the results of such consideration, assess the adequacy and proportionality of made decision.
