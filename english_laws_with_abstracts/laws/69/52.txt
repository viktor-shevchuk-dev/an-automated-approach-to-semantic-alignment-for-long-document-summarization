Lessees of land plots shall be obliged to inform the lessor in writing about the exchange of their rights of use of land plots within five days from the day when the right of sublease has been registered. The written notification shall specify the cadastral number of the land plot (if any), the period for which the sublease contract has been concluded, and the person who obtained the land plot in sublease. The written notification shall be sent to the lessor by registered mail with a return receipt requested or delivered to them personally against written acknowledgement.
