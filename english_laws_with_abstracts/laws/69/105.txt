The land lease contract shall be concluded in a written form and may be subject to notarial certification upon the will of either party. The owner of the land plot shall have the right to set a requirement concerning the notarial certification of the land lease contract, as well as to cancel such a requirement. Setting (cancellation) of the requirement shall be a unilateral deed subject to notarial certification. Such a requirement is an encumbrance of property rights to the land plot and shall be subject to state registration in the manner prescribed by law.
