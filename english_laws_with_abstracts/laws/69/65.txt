The evaluator shall be appointed by the initiator of the exchange of rights of use. The cost of valuation services shall be paid by the initiator of the exchange of rights to use land plots. If the owner or user of the land plot does not agree with the valuation of the size of property damage carried out by the evaluator appointed by the initiator of the exchange of rights of use, it shall have the right to involve another evaluator to conduct a new valuation or to review the report on the previous expert monetary valuation of land plots. In this case, the costs for such services shall be borne by their customer.
