The court, commercial court or the chairman of the Maritime Arbitration Commission may, as a condition for arresting a vessel or extending an arrest previously imposed, compel the person so claiming to provide security for the maritime claim in an amount and under conditions determined by the court or the chairman of the Maritime Arbitration Commission in respect of any loss, which the proprietor or charterer of the vessel may suffer through the unreasonable arrest of the vessel or excessive security for the maritime claim, and for which such person may be liable.
