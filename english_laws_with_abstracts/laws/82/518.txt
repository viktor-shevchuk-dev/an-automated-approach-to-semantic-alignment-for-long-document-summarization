If the sunken property constitutes a direct threat to the safety of navigation, life or health of people, pollution of the natural environment, and the owner of the sunken property fails to raise it within the time period set by the harbour master in accordance with this Article, the seaport administration shall be entitled to take necessary actions to raise the sunken property immediately and, if necessary, to destroy or remove it by other means at the expense of the owner of the sunken property.
