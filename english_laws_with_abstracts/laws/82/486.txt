The maritime agent shall perform formalities and actions related to the vessel’s arrival, stay and departure, assist the master of the vessel in establishing contacts with the port services, local executive authorities, in arranging the supply and maintenance of the vessel in the port, arrange customs documents and cargo documents, collect freight and other amounts to pay the shipowner’s claims arising out of the contract for carriage, pay the amounts related to the stay in the port under the shipowner’s and master’s order, attract cargo for the sea lines, collect the freight, perform freight forwarding, hire the crews to work on the vessels, act on behalf of the cargo owner and as a contracting party of the participants in the cargo-carrying in direct intermodal traffic.
