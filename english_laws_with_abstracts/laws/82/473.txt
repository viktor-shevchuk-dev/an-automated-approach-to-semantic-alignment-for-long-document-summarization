If the master of the vessel being piloted by the vessel traffic control service does not follow the recommendations of the pilot operator of the vessel traffic control service, the pilot shall have the right to refuse to continue radar piloting of the vessel, and a record thereof shall be made by technical means. However, even after refusal to conduct the radar piloting of the vessel, the pilot operator of the vessel traffic control service shall remain in contact with the vessel and, if the master needs information necessary for safe navigation, the pilot operator of the vessel traffic control service shall provide it.
