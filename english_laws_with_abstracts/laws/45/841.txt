4. On the day of state registration of changes in the information in the United State Register related to a change in the membership of a limited liability or additional liability company or a change in the amount of their membership interests, a state registration entity shall issue (send by mail with an enclosure list) an abstract from the United State Register to the applicant, the company, persons who were listed in the register as members of the company before the registration action, and to persons specified in the register as members of the company after the registration action. If a state registration entity is aware of e-mail of persons to whom this abstract shall be sent, it shall additionally send a copy of the abstract by e-mail to such persons on the same day.
