Obtaining a licence by the operating organisation shall be the basis for commencing activities at a particular stage of the life cycle of a nuclear facility or radioactive waste storage facility, including all facilities located within the site of the nuclear facility or storage facility and being technologically related. The licence shall establish conditions and restrictions for such activities, works and operations, stipulate a technological complex and site boundaries to which this licence applies. One licence may be issued for a complex of several nuclear facilities or radioactive waste storage facilities located within a single site.
