Article 136. The representative body of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea shall be the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, deputies of which are elected by secret ballot on the basis of universal, equal, and direct suffrage. The term of office of the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, deputies of which are elected in regular elections, shall be five years. The termination of office of the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea shall entail termination of office of its deputies.
