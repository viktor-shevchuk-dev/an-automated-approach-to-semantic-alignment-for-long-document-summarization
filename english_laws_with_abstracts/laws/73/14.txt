Application (motion) shall mean citizens’ appeals with a request to promote the exercise of their rights and interests enshrined by the Constitution and current legislation or notification of violations of current legislation or shortcomings in the activity of enterprises, institutions, organisations irrespective of the form of ownership, Members of Parliament of Ukraine, councillor, officials, as well as expressing the opinions on improving their activity. Motion shall mean a written request for recognising person’s status, rights or freedoms, etc.
