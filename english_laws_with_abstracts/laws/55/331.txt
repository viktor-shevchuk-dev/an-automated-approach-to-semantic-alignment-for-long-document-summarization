Employees who provide production processes in the oil and gas industry shall be required to undergo special training and testing of knowledge (certification) in accordance with the requirements of the law. The list of jobs and positions of employees who shall undergo special training and certification shall be approved by the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the field of industrial safety and mining supervision, in coordination with the central government body, ensuring the formation and implementation of public policy in the oil and gas complex.
