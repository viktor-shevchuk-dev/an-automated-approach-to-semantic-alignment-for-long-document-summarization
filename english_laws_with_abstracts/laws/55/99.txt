Upon expiration of the special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil, the state property provided for use to the owner of this permit shall be returned to the state. Buildings, equipment and other assets created by the owner of a special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil in the process of conducting business in the oil and gas industry shall be his / her property and after the expiration of the special permit for the use of oil and gas potential subsoil can be used by him at its discretion.
