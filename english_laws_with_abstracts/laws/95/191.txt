To that end, by virtue of scientifically substantiated medical, physiological and sanitary-hygienic requirements, uniform minimum standards of wages, pensions, scholarships, social assistance and other proceeds of the population shall be established; the most vulnerable social groups shall be provided with the physical and free food, clothing, medicines and other basic necessities; a set of measures shall be taken to meet the living needs of refugees, homeless people and other people who do not have a certain place of residence; free medical and social care shall be provided to people in a difficult financial situation threatening their life and health.
