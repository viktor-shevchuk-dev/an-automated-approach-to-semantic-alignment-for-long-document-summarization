Heat-generating entities, regardless of organisational and legal forms and forms of ownership, shall have the right to access the mainstream and local heating networks, provided that the technical specifications for connection are met. A business entity engaged in the transportation of thermal energy shall be obliged to connect a heat-generating entity that intends to carry out activities for the production of thermal energy upon meeting the technical specifications and concluding a contract for connection to the heating network. Disputes on the scope of work specified in the technical conditions for connection shall be considered in accordance with the law.
