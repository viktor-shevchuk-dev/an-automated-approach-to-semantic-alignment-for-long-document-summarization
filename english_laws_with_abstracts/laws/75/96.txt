The sign or tablet with information about the registered denomination of the person, marks for goods and services that belong to this person, the type of its activity (unless it appears from the registered denomination of the person), the working hours, that is placed on the inner surface of the person's own or provided for use premises, on the outer surface of the building or structure not higher than the first floor or on the floor where the person's own or provided for use premises are located, near the entrance to such premises, shall not be considered as advertising.
