The entity of international transfers of goods, which has been granted a permit, conclusion or International Import Certificate, shall submit to the central government body, implementing the public policy in the field of State Export Control, written reports on the results of negotiations specified in Article 18 of this Law, as well as on the actual export and import of goods specified in these documents, and on the use of these goods for the stated purposes. The form of the report and the terms for its submission shall be determined by the central government body, which ensures the formation of public policy in the field of State Export Control.
