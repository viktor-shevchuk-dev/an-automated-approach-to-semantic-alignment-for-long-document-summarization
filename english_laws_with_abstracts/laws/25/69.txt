3. This Law shall regulate relations associated with the initiation of public-private partnership, selection of a private partner, preparation for conclusion, determination of the content of the agreement, conclusion and execution of agreements concluded under the public-private partnership in accordance with Part 1 of this Article, unless another procedure for selection of a private partner, preparation for conclusion, determination of the content of the agreement, conclusion and execution of such agreements is determined by the law governing the relevant implementation form of public-private partnership. If the agreement to be concluded under the public-private partnership is a mixed contract, relations on the initiation of public-private partnership, preparation for the conclusion of a contract and selection of a private partner are regulated by the provisions of this Law.
