At the request of officials of state authorities, local governments, courts, bodies of the National Police, prosecutors, bodies of the Security Service of Ukraine, lawyers, notaries, parking inspectors information from the Unified State Register of Vehicles related to the exercise of their powers under the law is provided for the subject (owner, proper user of the vehicle) or the state number plate in written or electronic form through direct access to the Unified State Register of Vehicles subject to the identification of the relevant official based on his/her electronic digital signature. The procedure for accessing the Unified State Register of Vehicles is established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
