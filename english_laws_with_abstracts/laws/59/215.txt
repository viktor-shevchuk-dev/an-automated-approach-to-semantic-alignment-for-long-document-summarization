In accordance with the programs of development and improvement of transport, the Verkhovna Rada of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, regional, Kyiv and Sevastopol city councils approve regional programs of road transport development, which determine the development of the network of public bus routes, in the relevant territory and its infrastructure, renewal and replenishment of the bus park, construction of roads, a list of rural settlements that will be provided with bus communication with district centers, funds to cover the losses of road carriers serving unprofitable public routes, as well as safety measures for transportation.
