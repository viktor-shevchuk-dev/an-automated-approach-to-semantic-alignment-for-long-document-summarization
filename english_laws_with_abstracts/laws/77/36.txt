Bribing of supplier's employee, acting official shall be understood as giving or offering to a supplier's employee, acting official, directly or via third parties by a buyer's (customer's) competitor material or other types of benefits for improper performance or non-performance by an employee, official of the supplier of his official duties that arise from concluding or are related to the conclusion of a supply agreement between the supplier and the buyer (customer) for goods, provision of services, any other type of agreement or for non-conclusion of an agreement.
