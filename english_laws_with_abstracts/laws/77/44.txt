Dissemination of misleading information is understood as a statement by a business entity, directly or via the third party, to another person or a number of persons or an undefined number of persons, including by means of advertisement, of an incomplete, inaccurate, false information, in particular as a result of the chosen way of its presentation, omission of separate facts or with unclear wording that influenced or may influence the intentions of such persons to purchase (order) or market (sell, supply, complete, provide) goods, works, services from such a business entity.
