The application for state registration of a medicinal product, subject to procurement based on the results of a procurement procedure conducted by a specialised procurement organisation for the implementation of a procurement agreement between the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare and the relevant specialised procurement organisation, shall be accompanied by materials of registration dossier; methods of quality control for medicinal product (finished product); a report on evaluation of medicinal product drawn up by the regulatory authority of the country where this medicinal product is registered, or a report on evaluation of medicinal product issued by the World Health organisation (if any, if the medicinal product is requalified by the World Health Organization); instruction for medical use of medicinal product, written in language under the requirements of this Law; a sample of original package of medicinal product; translations of package label text of medicinal product and instruction for medical use of medicinal product into Ukrainian, signed by the applicant or his authorised representative. Fee for state registration of a medicinal product subject to procurement based on the results of a procurement procedure conducted by a specialised procurement organisation for implementation of a procurement agreement between the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state policy in the field of healthcare and the relevant specialised procurement organisation shall not be paid.
