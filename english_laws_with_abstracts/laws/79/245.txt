The requirements of this Article shall not apply to an individual authorised to make procurements in healthcare, purchases medicinal products according to the List approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine in relation to medicinal products included in such a List. In the absence of a licence to conduct business activities on the wholesale trade in medicinal products in an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare, compliance with the legal requirements to license business activities during storage, transportation of medicinal products purchased by an individual authorised to make procurements in the field of healthcare, the legislation on the quality control of medicinal products during wholesale trade shall be ensured by engaging the business entities licensed to conduct business activities on the wholesale trade in medicinal products in compliance with the legislation in the field of public procurements.
