1. Funds received as a result of the transfer of technologies developed for budget funds (in the form of periodic and/or one-time payments (royalties)), the developer organisation being the holder of the property rights to such technologies under Article 11 of this Law, shall be used by the developer organisation to pay remuneration to the authors of technologies and/or intellectual property items, for the development of innovation activities and transfer of technologies of the organisation, in particular, payment for foreign patenting.
