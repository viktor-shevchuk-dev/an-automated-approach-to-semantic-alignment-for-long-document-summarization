Payment of unemployment benefits, including one-time payment for unemployed persons to set up business undertakings and financial assistance during occupational training, retraining or advanced training in excess of the amount and duration prescribed by the Law of Ukraine “On Mandatory State Social Unemployment Insurance”, shall be made in accordance with the procedure provided by the legislation for unemployment benefit provision, including one-time payment for unemployed persons to set up business undertakings by the unemployed parties, and financial assistance during occupational training, retraining or advanced training, and at the expense of funds provided in the State Budget of Ukraine for social protection of employees who are being retired due to Chornobyl NPP decommissioning.
