The decision to refuse admission to professional certification and refusal to issue a qualification certificate may be appealed to the appeal commission of the central executive authority implementing state policy in the field of architecture, or self-regulatory organisation in the field of architectural activity (in case of delegated powers) and/or in court. The procedure for setting up an appeal commission of the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of architecture and a self-regulatory organisation in the field of architectural activity and consideration of complaints is determined by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine in the Procedure for Professional Certification.
