2. It shall not be allowed to gather, store, use and disseminate confidential information about a person without his/her consent, except for the cases determined by law, and only in the interests of the national security, economic welfare and protection of human rights. The confidential information on an individual shall include, inter alia, the data about his/her ethnicity, education, marital status, religious beliefs, health condition, as well as the address, date and time of birth.
