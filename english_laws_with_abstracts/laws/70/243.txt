To facilitate film-making in Ukraine and viewers' access to works of the cinematographic heritage of the television organisation, which carry out television broadcasting or multichannel broadcasting with the use of the radio frequency resource under the relevant licence, other film exhibitors other than television organisations, shall be obliged to screen national films, other films made by cinematographic entities of Ukraine and works of the national cinematographic heritage for at least 15 per cent of the total monthly time of film screening until 1 January 2022, and at least 30 per cent of the total monthly time of film screening after 1 January 2022.
