9. A person who received a notification on dismissal of the appeal against denial to issue documents for resolving the issue of recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, and failed to exercise the right to appeal against it in court within five business days, must leave the territory of Ukraine within the determined period unless such a person has other lawful grounds for staying in Ukraine provided for by the Law of Ukraine “On the Legal Status of Foreigners and Stateless Persons”.
