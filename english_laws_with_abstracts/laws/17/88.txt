7. Identity documents, as well as documents and materials that may serve as evidence of existing conditions for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, shall be attached to the application for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection. If an applicant has no identity documents, or if such documents are forged, the person must specify this circumstance in the application for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, and explain the reasons for such circumstances.
