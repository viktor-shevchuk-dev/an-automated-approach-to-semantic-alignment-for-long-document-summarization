3. Foreigners and stateless persons recognised as refugees by other countries, which are parties to the 1951 Convention relating to the Status of Refugees and the 1967 Protocol relating to the Status of Refugees, and who stay in Ukraine, cannot be removed or forcibly returned to countries where they may be subjected to torture and other cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment, or to ones from which they may be removed or forcibly returned to countries where their life or freedom are endangered for reasons of religion, ethnicity, nationality (citizenship), being a member of a particular social group or political opinion.
