13. If a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection decided to deny recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, an authorised officer of such a central executive authority shall send or hand a written notification of such a decision to a person in whose respect such a decision was made, specifying the reasons for the denial and explaining the appeal procedure. If a person appeals against such a decision, the certificate on the application for protection in Ukraine shall be extended.
