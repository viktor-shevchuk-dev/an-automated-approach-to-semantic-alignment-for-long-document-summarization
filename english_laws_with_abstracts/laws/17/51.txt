2. A refugee or a person in need of subsidiary protection or person granted temporary protection cannot be removed or forcibly returned to countries where they may be subjected to torture and other cruel, inhuman or degrading treatment or punishment, or to ones from which they may be removed or forcibly returned to countries where their life or freedom are endangered for reasons of religion, ethnicity, nationality (citizenship), being a member of a particular social group or political opinion, or for other reasons recognised by international treaties or international organisations, to which Ukraine is a party, as someone who cannot be returned to their countries of origin.
