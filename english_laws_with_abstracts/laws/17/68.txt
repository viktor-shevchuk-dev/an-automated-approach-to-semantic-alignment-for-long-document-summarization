5. If a person temporarily staying in Ukraine on legal grounds, and during such stay conditions specified in clauses 1 or 13, part 1, Article 1 of this Law have arisen in the country of his/her nationality or previous permanent residence, owing to which he/she cannot return to the country of his/her origin; and if such person has the intention to be recognised as a refugee in Ukraine or person in need of subsidiary protection, he/she must file an application with a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection before the expiration of the period of his/her stay on the territory of Ukraine
