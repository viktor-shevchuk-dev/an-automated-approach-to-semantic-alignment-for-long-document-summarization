3. If a child separated from the family is crossing or has crossed the state border of Ukraine and applies for recognition as a refugee or person in need of subsidiary protection, or the same was informed by other persons who are not her/his legal representatives, officers of the State Border Guard Service of Ukraine must immediately notify a central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection as well as the child protection services thereof. A central executive authority implementing the state policy on refugees and persons in need of subsidiary or temporary protection together with the child protection services must take measures to provide for temporary accommodation of such a child in a relevant child care centre or family.
