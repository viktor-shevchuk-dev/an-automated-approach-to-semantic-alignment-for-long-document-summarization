Where the agreement is executed through accession, it shall comprise a public part of the agreement and an individual part of the agreement, by signing of which the client accedes to the agreement as a whole. The public part of the financial services agreement shall be made public and accessible for perusal by clients on the financial services provider’s own website, and shall be delivered to the client at his choice in a manner that would allow for the date of furnishing of the agreement to be established, using the contact details provided by the client. All versions of the public part of the agreement must be stored on the financial services provider’s own website, with an indication of their duration.
