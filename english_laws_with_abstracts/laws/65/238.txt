6. An objection against a decision of the NIPO on an application is examined in accordance with the regulation of the Appeals Chamber within 2 months from the date of receiving of the objection and the document on the payment of the fee for the submission of the objection, within the scope of arguments presented in the objection and during its consideration as well as taking into account the response of the applicant if it was received within the established time. The period of consideration of an objection is extended on the initiative of the applicant, but for not more than 2 months if a relevant request is submitted and the fee for its submission is paid before the expiry of this period.
