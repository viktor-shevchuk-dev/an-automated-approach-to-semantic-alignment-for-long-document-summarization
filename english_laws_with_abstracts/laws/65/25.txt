Legislation of Ukraine on the legal protection of geographical indications consists of this Law, Laws of Ukraine “On Protection Against Unfair Competition”, “On Protection of Rights to Trademarks”, “On Consumer Rights Protection”, “On Advertising”, laws regulating preparation to registration, use and protection of geographical indications with respect to agricultural products (agricultural goods), food products, wines, flavoured wine products, alcoholic beverages, and control over such geographical indications, and other regulatory acts.
