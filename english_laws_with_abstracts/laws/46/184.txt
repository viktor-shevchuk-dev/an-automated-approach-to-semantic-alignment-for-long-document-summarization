The information displayed during the auction shall be determined by the order of auctions for the support quota distribution. After the auction ends, the information provided by the auction participants shall be simultaneously disclosed according to the procedure for conducting auctions on the support quota distribution. The criterion for selecting the winners of the support quota distribution auction shall be the price specified in the closed bid. Bidders who have offered the lowest price bids within the part of the annual support quota distributed at the auction shall be recognised as the winners of the auction for the declared amount of capacity.
