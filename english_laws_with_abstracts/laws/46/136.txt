The state shall guarantee the enshrining in law for the entire “green” tariff validity period and the period of support for producers of electricity from alternative energy sources, which by the auction acquired the right to support, the requirements for the purchase in each billing period of electricity produced at the electric power facilities, including on the power plants construction stages (start-up complexes) that use alternative energy sources (and only electricity generated by micro, mini and small hydroelectric power plants if hydro energy is used), at the set “green” tariff or at auction price taking into account the premium established under this Article, in the amounts and in the manner specified in Articles 68, 71 and 74 of the Law of Ukraine “On Electricity Market”, as well as for payments for such electricity in the certain period and for funds.
