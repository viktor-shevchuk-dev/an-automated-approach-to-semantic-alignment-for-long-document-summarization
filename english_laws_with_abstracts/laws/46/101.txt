The “green” tariff for electricity produced by consumers, including by energy cooperatives, from solar energy and wind energy on combined wind and solar generating systems with an installed capacity not exceeding 150 kW, shall be set at the level of the retail tariff for consumers of the second voltage class as of January 2009, multiplied by the factor of “green” tariff for electricity produced by consumers, including energy cooperatives, from solar energy and wind energy on combined wind and solar generating systems, the installed capacity of which does not exceed 150 kW.
