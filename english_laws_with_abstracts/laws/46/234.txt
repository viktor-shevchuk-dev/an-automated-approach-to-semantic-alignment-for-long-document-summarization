The state shall guarantee that the “green” tariff shall be set for business entities under part 3 of this article, with the legislation of Ukraine effective as of the date when the Law of Ukraine “On Amendments to Certain Laws of Ukraine on Improving the Conditions of Support for Electricity Production from Alternative Energy Sources” was enacted to be applied throughout the term of the “green” tariff, in addition to legislation that reduces or abolishes taxes or fees, weakens the regulation of business activity, simplifies state supervision (control) in the field of business activity, mitigates liability or otherwise improves the position of business entities, which should be applied from the date when such legislation is entered into force.
