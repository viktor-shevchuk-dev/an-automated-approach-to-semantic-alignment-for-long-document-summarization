1. Where public prosecutor or defence counsel does not appear in court session upon notice in criminal proceedings in which the participation of the defence counsel is mandatory, the court shall postpone the trial, fixe date, time and place of a new court session, and take measures to ensure their appearance in court. At the same time, where the reason for non-appearance is invalid, the court shall raise the issue of liability of the public prosecutor or defence counsel who has failed to appear before the bodies which are authorised by law to initiate disciplinary proceedings against them.
