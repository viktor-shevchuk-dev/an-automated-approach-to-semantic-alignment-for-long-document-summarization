1. Public prosecutor or investigator upon approval of public prosecutor shall have the right to lodge a motion to enforce a measure of restraint to the individual who was apprehended without ruling granting permission for apprehension, on the suspicion in the commission of criminal offence, with the local court within whose territorial jurisdiction the pre-trial investigation agency is located, and if this proves impossible within a period specified in part 2 of Article 211 hereof, with the local court within whose territorial jurisdiction the person was apprehended. In the event of detention without a ruling on the permission to detain a person on suspicion of committing a criminal offence within the jurisdiction of the High Anti-Corruption Court, the above ruling shall be submitted to the High Anti-Corruption Court.
