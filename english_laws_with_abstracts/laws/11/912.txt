2. Physical evidence or a document produced voluntary or pursuant to a court decision shall be kept by the party to criminal proceeding to which it has been released. The party to criminal proceeding to which physical evidence or a document has been provided shall preserve it in the condition acceptable for the use in the criminal proceeding. Physical evidence that has been obtained or seized by investigator or public prosecutor shall be examined, photographed and described in detail in the examination report. Prosecution shall preserve physical evidence according to the procedure established by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
