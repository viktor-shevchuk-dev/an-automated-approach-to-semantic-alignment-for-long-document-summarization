Wherever the accused or victim is holding or held a position of a judge or an officer of the High Anti-Corruption Court and where a criminal proceeding falls within the jurisdiction of this court, such criminal proceeding in the first instance shall be conducted by a court appeals whose jurisdiction extends to Kyiv, and in that case the court judgments are to be appealed to the court of appeals, which shall be determined by the panel of judges of the Criminal Court of Cassation of the Supreme Court.
