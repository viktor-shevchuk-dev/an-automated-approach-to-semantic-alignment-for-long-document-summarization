1. The public prosecutor or the investigator with the concurrence of the public prosecutor shall have the right to approach the investigating judge in the course of pre-trial investigation or the court in the course of judicial proceedings with a motion for suspension of a person from his/her office. Public prosecutor shall have the right to apply for suspension of a person from office to the government authorities specified in part 3 of Article 154 hereof, and for dismissal from office of the Chairperson of the National Agency for the Prevention of Corruption and his/her Deputy, the Prosecutor General or the Deputy Prosecutor General –Head of the Specialised Anti-Corruption Prosecutor's Office – shall have the right to apply for suspension from office.
