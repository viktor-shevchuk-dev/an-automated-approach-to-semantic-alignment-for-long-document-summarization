1. Before examination of a witness, presiding judge shall establish his/her identity and find out his/her relation to the accused and the victim. In addition, presiding judge shall find out whether the witness received the instructions about the rights and duties of a witness, and whether he understands them, and if necessary, explains them to him, as well as find out whether he does not refuse to give testimony on the grounds specified by this Code, and warns him about the criminal liability for refusal to give testimony and for knowingly giving misleading testimony.
