{The provision of part 5 of Article 176 has been recognised inconsistent with the Constitution of Ukraine(unconstitutional), in accordance with the Decision of the Constitutional Court No. 7-r/2019 of 25 June 2019} Restrictive measures in the form of personal commitment, personal bail, house arrest, bail shall not be applied to persons suspected or accused of committing crimes under Articles 109–114-1, 258–258-5, 260, 261 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine
