9. Investigator or public prosecutor shall have the right to simultaneously interview two or more persons who have already been interviewed, to clarify the reasons for discrepancies in their testimonies. At the start of such interview, it shall be established whether the summoned persons know each other, and what are the relations between them. Witnesses shall be advised on criminal liability for refusal to give testimony and for giving knowingly false testimony, and victims shall be advised on criminal liability for giving knowingly false testimony.
