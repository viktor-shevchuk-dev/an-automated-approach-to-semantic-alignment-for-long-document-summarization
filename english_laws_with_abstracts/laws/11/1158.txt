6. The whistleblower shall have the right to represent his/her interests during the consideration of the issue of payment of remuneration to him/her personally and through a representative, a lawyer (including anonymously, but until the issue of payment of remuneration is resolved). In order to protect the personal data of an anonymous whistleblower after its disclosure to the court, in particular, security measures such as ensuring the confidentiality of personal information and/or a closed court proceedings may be taken.
