During the pre-trial investigation, the decisions of the investigating judge on refusal to grant the complaint against the decision to close the criminal proceedings or against the decision of the investigator or public prosecutor to refuse to grant the request to close the criminal proceedings may also be appealed o the grounds of clause 9-1, part 1 of Article 284 hereof, on cancellation of the notice of suspicion or refusal to grant the complaint on the notice of suspicion, return of the complaint on the decision, act or omission of the investigator or public prosecutor or refusal to open proceedings thereon.
