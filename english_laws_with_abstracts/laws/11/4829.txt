Criminal proceedings for the crimes listed in clause “b” of the Agreement between Ukraine and the Kingdom of the Netherlands on international legal co-operation in connection with crimes related to the downing of Malaysia Airlines flight MH17 on 17 July 2014, concluded on 7 July 2017 in the city of Tallinn, shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of this Code, with due regard to the provisions of clause 4, §2 “Final Provisions” of Section 4 of the “Law of Ukraine On Amendments to the Economic Procedure Code of Ukraine, the Civil Procedure Code of Ukraine, the Administrative Procedure Code of Ukraine and other legislative acts”.
