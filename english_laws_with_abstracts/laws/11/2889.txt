1. Where the summoned victim duly notified on the date, time and place of the court session, does not appear in court, the court having heard the opinion of the participants to the court proceedings, shall decide to conduct the proceedings without the victim or to postpone the trial, depending on to what extent it is possible to ascertain all circumstances during trial in his/her absence. The court shall be entitled to impose a pecuniary penalty on the victim in cases and in accordance with the procedure provided for by Chapter 12 of this Code.
