2. Whenever an investigating judge receives information from any sources whatsoever, which gives ground for a reasonable suspicion that within the court’s territorial jurisdiction there is a person who has been deprived of his liberty without valid court’s decision, or has not been released from custody after the payment of bail in accordance with the procedure laid down in this Code, such judge shall issue a ruling by which to order any government authority or official in whose custody the person is kept to immediately bring this person to the investigating judge in view of verifying grounds for deprivation of liberty.
