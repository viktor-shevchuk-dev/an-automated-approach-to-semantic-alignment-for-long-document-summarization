4. In conducting of individual procedural actions in the territory of Ukraine at the request (commission) of the competent authorities of foreign states within the scope of international cooperation provisions of this Code shall apply. If requested by a competent authority of a foreign state, in the course of such procedural actions the procedural law of this foreign state may apply where this is provided for by the international treaty, ratified by the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine, and where there is not such international treaty of Ukraine, this request shall be consistent with the current Ukrainian legislation.
