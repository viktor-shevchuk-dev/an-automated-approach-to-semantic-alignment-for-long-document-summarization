3. If a criminal offence, the pre-trial investigation of which was conducted by the territorial branch of the National Anti-Corruption Bureau of Ukraine (except for criminal offences that are subject to the jurisdiction of the High Anti-Corruption Court), has been committed within the territorial jurisdiction of the local anti-corruption office of Ukraine, the criminal proceeding shall be conducted by a court territorially closest to the court at the location of the respective territorial office of the National Anti-Corruption Bureau of Ukraine, another political unit (Autonomous Republic of Crimea, oblast, city of Kyiv or Sevastopol).
