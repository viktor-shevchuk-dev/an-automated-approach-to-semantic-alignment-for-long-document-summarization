1. Participants to the criminal proceedings, as well as non-participants as to whom the court has decided on their rights, freedoms, interests, or duties, shall not be restricted in the right to obtain both written and oral information in court with regard to the outcome of judicial proceedings, and also as for their their right to familiarise themselves with procedural decisions and to obtain copies thereof. No one shall be restricted in his/her right to obtain information in court on the date, time, and place of his/her court hearing and on the decisions taken therein, except as otherwise provided for by law.
