2. Where, when requesting legal aid in a foreign state it is necessary to perform a procedural action, which in Ukraine requires the permission of a public prosecutor or court, such procedural action shall have the respective permission of the public prosecutor or court in the manner prescribed by this Code, only where this is stipulated by an international treaty or is a mandatory condition for the provision of such assistance under the law of the requested party. The validity of such permission is not limited, and a duly certified copy of the permission shall be attached to the records of the request.
