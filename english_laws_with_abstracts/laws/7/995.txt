If the credit rating of the bank, which is the custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution (other than the venture fund) in the form of securities, is fallen to the speculative level according to the National rating scale or has lost its validity, the collective investment institution (other than a venture capital fund), within three months of the date on which the credit rating level was lowered or lost its validity, shall be obliged to bring its activities into conformity with the requirements of the law in accordance with the procedure established by the Commission.
