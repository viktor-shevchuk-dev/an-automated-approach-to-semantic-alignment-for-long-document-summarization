The custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution of public placement shall be a bank licensed to act as a custodian of securities by the Commission in accordance with the established procedure. Entering into agreement with the custodian of the assets of the collective investment institution of private placement shall not be mandatory. In the case a corporate fund or an asset management company of a unit fund of a private placement enters into such an agreement, the custodian of the assets of such collective investment institution may be a legal entity, licensed by the Commission in accordance with established procedure to act as a custodian of securities.
