1. The written notice of the general meeting of the corporate fund and agenda thereof shall be sent to each participant of the corporate fund listed in the list of participants of the corporate fund which is executed to dispatch the relevant notices on holding the general meeting. Such list shall be executed in accordance with the procedure established by the laws on the depository system on the date specified by the supervisory board and, in the case of an extraordinary general meeting at the request of the participants of the corporate fund in cases envisaged by Article 30 hereof — on a date determined by the participants of the corporate fund, requesting it.  The date may not be set prior to the day of the resolution to hold the general meeting and may not be set earlier than 60 calendar days before the day of the general meeting.
