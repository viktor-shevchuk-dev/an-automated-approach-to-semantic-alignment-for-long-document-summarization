Agricultural products imported (transmitted) into the customs territory of Ukraine shall be subject to radiological, and in case of import of state control objects – goods of the first – fifth, seventh, tenth, twelfth, fourteenth – sixteenth, nineteenth, twenty-first, twenty-third groups under the Ukrainian Classification of Goods of Foreign Economic Activity (UKT ZED) – to state control that is carried out by the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of safety and certain indicators of foodstuff quality, and the central executive authority implementing the state policy in the field of veterinary medicine. Import of foodstuffs and related materials into Ukraine shall be carried out following the procedure established by law.
