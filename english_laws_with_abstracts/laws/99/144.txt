If a religious organisation fails to rectify the identified deficiencies within the established period, all documents received by the central executive authority that implements the state policy in the field of religion, oblast, Kyiv and Sevastopol City State Administrations, and in the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, by the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, except for the application for registration of the charter (regulations) of a religious organisation (amendments thereto), shall be returned to it with a cover letter explaining the reasons for such return.
