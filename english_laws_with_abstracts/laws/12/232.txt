Public organisations legalised on the day of entry into force of this Law by notification of establishment, within six months from the date of entry into force of this Law shall determine the person (persons) authorised to represent a public association, subject to the requirements of Part 6 of Article 9 of this Law and submit information on this person (persons), provided for in Clause 3, Part 2 of Article 16 of this Law, to the authorised body for registration.
