4. Public organisations, their unions (associations, other associations of public organisations), legalised on the day this Law enters into force by registration or notification of the establishment, do not, respectively, require re-registration or re-submission of documents for communication. Charters (regulations) of public organisations, unions of public organisations shall be brought into line with this Law within five years from the date of its entry into force. Amendments to the charter (regulation) related to the entry into force of this law are registered without charging a fee for registration actions within five years from the date of entry into force of this Law.
