This type of raw material belongs to Category A. Developed ferrous metallurgy with significant deposits of manganese ores makes Ukraine the world's leading producer of manganese products (ferromanganese, silicomanganese, metallic manganese, etc.). In terms of total reserves of manganese ores, Ukraine ranks second after the Republic of South Africa and first in terms of proved reserves. Industrial reserves of manganese ores are concentrated in the South Ukrainian manganese ore province, including deposits of the world's largest Nikopol manganese ore basin.
