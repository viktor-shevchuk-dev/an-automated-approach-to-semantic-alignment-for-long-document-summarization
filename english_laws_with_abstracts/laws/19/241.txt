This type of raw material belongs to Category D. Ukraine is one of the leaders in the European subcontinent in terms of scandium resources. The state balance of mineral reserves considers scandium reserves as a concomitant component in four complex deposits: Zhovtorichynsk, Stremyhorod, Torchynsk and Zlobychy deposits. All of them are located within the Ukrainian Crystal Shield and are associated with Precambrian bedrock complexes, their residual soil and placers.
