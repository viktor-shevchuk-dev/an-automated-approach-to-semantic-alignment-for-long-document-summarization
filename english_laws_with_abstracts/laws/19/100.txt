This type of raw material belongs to Category A. In total, there are 54 iron ore deposits in Ukraine, of which 22 are in operation. Rich iron ores and ferruginous quartzites are extracted from the deposits of Kryvyi Rih, Kremenchuk and Bilozirka iron ore basins. Proven reserves amount to 28 billion tons. The world's largest proven reserves (more than 10 billion tons) are distributed among four countries: Russia, Ukraine, Brazil and China. Their total share in the world's proven reserves is 67.2 per cent (139 billion tons).
