This type of raw material belongs to categories C and D. Ukraine occupies one of the leading places among the CIS countries regarding the number of established manifestations of bentonites. There are approximately 100 deposits and manifestations of bentonite on its territory. The most significant bentonite deposits are found in the Transcarpathian, Cherkasy, Ternopil, Khmelnytskyi oblasts and the Autonomous Republic of Crimea. However, most of the known deposits are not of industrial significance due to the insignificant reserves of bentonite or their occurrence's difficult mining and geological conditions.
