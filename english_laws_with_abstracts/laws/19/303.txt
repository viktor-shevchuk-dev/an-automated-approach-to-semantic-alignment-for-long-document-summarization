The state balance of mineral reserves includes 14 deposits of flux limestones, the total reserves of which amount to 2.46 billion tons, including 11 flux limestones (non-magnetic) and 3 complex non-magnetic and magnesia limestones. There are seven dolomite deposits on the state balance sheet, of which four are being developed. With significant total reserves of refractory dolomites, the total volume of which is 380 million tons, Ukrainian enterprises are experiencing a shortage of high-quality vintage varieties, the volume of which is only 36 million tons.
