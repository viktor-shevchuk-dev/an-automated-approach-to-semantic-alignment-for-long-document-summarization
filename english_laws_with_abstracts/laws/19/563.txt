The State Service of Geology and Mineral Resources of Ukraine will carry out activities within the framework of the Intergovernmental Council of the CIS countries to solve the problems of mineral resources use of the CIS countries and develop priority measures for co-ordination and development of co-operation in the field of study, exploration and use of mineral resources. The State Service of Geology and Mineral Resources of Ukraine will also participate in the activities of the Association of Geological Services of European countries to study foreign, primarily European, experience in organising geological services, get acquainted with the latest scientific and technical developments in the field of geological exploration of subsurface resources, harmonise legislation in the field of mineral resources use.
