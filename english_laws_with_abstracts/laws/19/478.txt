On the Ukraine territory, the accumulation of solid household and industrial waste, discharges of polluted waste-water into water bodies, emissions into the air of mining and industrial enterprises continue, which in some regions exceed the maximum permissible concentrations and protective capabilities of soils, rocks and underground water. All this caused contamination of the main aquifers' underground waters for domestic drinking water supply (the Donets Basin, Kryvyi Rih Basin, the Carpathian region, Crimea, Pryazovia, Prydniprovia, etc.).
