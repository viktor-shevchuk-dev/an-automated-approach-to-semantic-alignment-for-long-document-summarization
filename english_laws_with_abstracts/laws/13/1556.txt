1. Within 20 days from the date of withdrawal of the processing permit of goods in the customs territory of Ukraine, the enterprise shall complete the starting processing operations, and goods placed under the customs processing regime in the customs territory shall within 30 days from the date of withdrawal of the processing permission, be exported outside the customs territory of Ukraine or declared under another customs regime in the manner prescribed by this Code. If the completion of the started processing operations within 20 days is impossible without causing irreversible damage to the goods or technological equipment, goods processing shall be completed in accordance with the technological scheme of processing. In this case, the goods shall, within 10 days from the date of actual completion of their processing be exported outside the customs territory of Ukraine or placed under another customs regime.
