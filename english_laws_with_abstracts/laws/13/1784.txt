11. The absence of the entry summary declaration at the Ukrainian border checkpoint at the moment of performing the customs formalities shall be considered grounds for a refusal to release the goods and commercial means of transport transporting them across the customs border of Ukraine and grounds for bringing the carrier to administrative responsibility under this Code. The passage of goods and commercial means of transport transporting them across the customs border of Ukraine shall be performed after the submission of the entry summary declaration to the customs authority and performing the necessary customs formalities determined by the risk analysis of such declaration.
