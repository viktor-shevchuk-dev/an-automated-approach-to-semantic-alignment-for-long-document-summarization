6. The customs authorities shall be legally liable for any loss or damage to goods, commercial means of transport stored by them. Costs incurred by the customs authorities for storage of goods, whose customs clearance is suspended in accordance with Articles 399, 400 and 401-1 of the Code, shall be reimbursed by the holder. In the case provided for in part 4 of this Article, such liability shall be borne by the management of entities, where goods are transferred by the customs authorities to such entities for storage.
