The goods are released under the declared customs regime under provisional customs declaration based on currency exchange rates defined under Article 3-1 of this Code, tariff and non-tariff regulation of import-export activity existing at the date customs authority accepted provisional customs declaration for customs clearance. If the declarant doesn't have the exact information required to determine customs duties, the customs duties under provisional customs declaration are chargeable based on the highest customs duty of those the goods can be eligible to.
