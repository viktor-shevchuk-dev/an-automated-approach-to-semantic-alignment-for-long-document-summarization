Based on the technological features of processing of individual goods, the pre-established period of their processing on a written application of the enterprise, which issued a permit for processing, may be extended by the customs authority to which the relevant processing permission has been issued, including beyond the period provided for in part 1 of this Article. In case of refusal to extend such a period, the customs authority shall without delay notify to the requesting person who applied for its extension the reasons and grounds for such refusal in writing or electronic form.
