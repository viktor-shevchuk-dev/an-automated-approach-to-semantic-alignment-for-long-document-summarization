8. Customs authority accepts preliminary customs declaration if the verification has established that the declaration includes required product information and is completed with required documents or copies including electronic documents or (scanned) copies of paper documents approved by the digital signature of the declarant or authorised person. If original documents are not available at the submission of preliminary customs declaration copies of these documents can be submitted. Receiving customs official registers the receipt of declaration by applying respective personal identification and other identifications (declaration number, date and time of receipt, etc.) including the use of informational technologies.
