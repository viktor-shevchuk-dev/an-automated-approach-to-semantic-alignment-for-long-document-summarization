3. In case of illegal import of goods into the customs territory of Ukraine, the taxation base shall be the customs value of such goods, their quantity and other indicators determined by the law, which are used to determine the taxation base, on the day of assessment of customs payments under part 1 of this Article. If it is impossible to determine the amount of customs payments due as a result of failure to provide the customs authority with accurate information about the nature of goods, their name, quantity, country of origin and customs value, the amount of customs payments shall be determined based on the higher of the rates of customs payments, quantity or value of goods that can be determined based on the available information.
