When goods are placed under the customs regime of re-import in accordance with clause 3, part 2 of Article 78 of this Code, the export duties paid on export of such goods shall be repaid to the persons that paid them or to their legal successors, in accordance with the procedure provided for in this Code. When goods are placed under the customs regime of re-import in accordance with clause 3, part 2 of Article 78 of this Code the excise tax and value-added tax shall be collected in accordance with the Tax Code of Ukraine.
