1. For the purposes of cabotage transportation of goods, a document containing a list of goods intended for such transportation, information on a vessel that will carry out such transportation, as well as the name of the Ukrainian port or ports where the unloading of these goods is to be carried out shall be provided to the customs authority. Upon completion of customs formalities such document shall be the ground for one cabotage transportation of the goods specified therein. The customs authority that completed the customs formalities shall notify the customs authorities of its appointment. In unloading the goods at the place of appointment a list of goods subject to unloading at that point shall be presented to the customs authority.
