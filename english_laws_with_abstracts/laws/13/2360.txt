2. Customs declaration and other documents shall be submitted to customs authority in electronic or paper form per this Code. Paper customs declaration shall be supported by an electronic copy. Invoice or any other document establishing goods value shall be submitted to customs authority together with the customs declaration. In cases identified by this Code, a customs value document shall be submitted. The declarant or authorised person shall specify the information required by Part 3 of Article 335 of this Code provided in declaring procedure. At the customs authority's request, the declarant or authorised person must provide original or duly certified copies of these documents unless original copies are not provided by law.
