9-2. Temporarily, within 270 days from the date of entry into force of the Law of Ukraine “On Amendments to the Customs Code of Ukraine and Certain Other Legislative Acts of Ukraine on Import of Vehicles into the Customs Territory of Ukraine”, customs clearance of vehicles for their free circulation in the customs territory of Ukraine, classified under heading 8703 according to UKT ZED and imported into the customs territory of Ukraine in the period between January 1, 2015 and the date of entry into force of this Law and being in the customs regime of temporary import or transit, shall be carried out with the completion of customs formalities in accordance with the temporary procedure approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine.
