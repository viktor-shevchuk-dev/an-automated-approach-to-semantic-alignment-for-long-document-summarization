2. The norm set forth by part 1 of this Article shall be applied if a person importing goods into the customs territory of Ukraine enters Ukraine at most once in one day. In order to ensure compliance with this condition, officials of state border security who carry out passport control at checkpoints across the state border of Ukraine shall inform when carrying out such control customs officials about citizens who enter Ukraine more than once during one day. If goods in quantities not exceeding the limits established in part 1 of this Article are imported into the customs territory of Ukraine by a person who enters Ukraine more than once during one day, such goods shall be subject to written declaration in the manner prescribed for citizens, accompanied by the submission of documents issued by government agencies for customs control and customs clearance of such goods, and subject to import duty at a rate of 10 per cent and value-added tax at a rate set by the Tax Code of Ukraine.
