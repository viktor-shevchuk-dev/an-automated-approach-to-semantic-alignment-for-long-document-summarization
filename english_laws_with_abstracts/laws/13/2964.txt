1. Goods moved across the customs border of Ukraine with their packaging and marking together with commercial means of transport carrying them across the customs border of Ukraine shall be presented in the unaltered state for customs control, and the documents for the goods and means of transport shall be submitted to the customs authorities at the Ukrainian border checkpoints and any other places within the customs territory of Ukraine, designated by the customs authorities, for customs control and customs clearance not later than three hours upon arrival of such goods at the Ukrainian border checkpoint or any other places, designated by the customs authorities.
