4. Customs authority must provide an opportunity to the declarant or authorised person to independently register in electronic customs clearance system submission and time of customs declaration or replacement document submitted under the existing law, and paper documents required for customs clearance. In the case of electronic customs declaration, the customs authority must provide information about the date and time of receipt of the electronic customs declaration or document that replaces it under the existing law. Failure to provide such an opportunity is deemed a lack of action which can be appealed under Chapter 4 of this Code.
