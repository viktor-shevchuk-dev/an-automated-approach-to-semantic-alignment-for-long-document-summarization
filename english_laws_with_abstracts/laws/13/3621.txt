1. Citizens are allowed to place in the transit customs regime personal use vehicles for the purpose of transit through the customs territory of Ukraine, subject to their written declaration in the manner prescribed for citizens, and payment of cash collateral in the amount of customs duties payable upon importation of such vehicles into the customs territory of Ukraine for free circulation to the account of the customs authority hat allowed the clearance of such vehicles at the customs territory of Ukraine. These requirements shall not apply to vehicles permanently registered with the relevant registration authorities of a foreign country, as evidenced by the relevant document, and moved by non-residents.
