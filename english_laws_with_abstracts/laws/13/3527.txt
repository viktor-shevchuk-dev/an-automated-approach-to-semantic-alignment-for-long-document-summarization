Goods, the total invoice value of which exceeds the equivalent of 10 000 Euros (except for those specified in part 4 of this Article), when exported (shipped) by citizens outside the customs territory of Ukraine are subject to the written declaration as provided by Ukrainian legislation for enterprises, accompanied by the payment of export duty in cases established by the laws of Ukraine, and submission of relevant documents confirming compliance with the established prohibitions and/or restrictions in accordance with the laws of Ukraine on the export (shipment) of goods outside the customs territory of Ukraine.
