1. When considering a case of violation of customs rules, an official is obliged to find out: whether an administrative offence has been committed, whether the person is guilty of committing it, whether he is subject to administrative liability, whether there are mitigating and/or aggravating circumstances responsibility, whether there are grounds for release of the person who committed the offence from administrative responsibility, as well as to find out other circumstances that are important for the proper resolution of the case.
