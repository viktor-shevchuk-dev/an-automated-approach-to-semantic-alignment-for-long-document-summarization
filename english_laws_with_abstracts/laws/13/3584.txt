Goods under headings 8701-8707, 8711, 8716 according to UKT ZED, which are subject to state registration, when imported by citizens into the customs territory of Ukraine or shipped into the customs territory of Ukraine to the address of citizens in unaccompanied baggage or cargo for free circulation, regardless of their value, are subject to written declaration and customs clearance in the manner prescribed by the central executive authority in charge of shaping and implementing the state fiscal policy, with import duties at full rates according to the Customs Tariff of Ukraine, excise tax and value-added tax at the rates set by the Tax Code of Ukraine.
