In the customs regime of temporary import with conditional partial relief from customs duties under the provisions of Annex E to the Convention on Temporary Import( Istanbul, 1990) may be placed goods (other than excisable once), that are not referred to in Articles 105, 189 of this Code, as well as Annexes B.1-B. 9, C, D to the Convention on Temporary Admission ( Istanbul, 1990), or those that fail to meet the requirements of these Annexes.
