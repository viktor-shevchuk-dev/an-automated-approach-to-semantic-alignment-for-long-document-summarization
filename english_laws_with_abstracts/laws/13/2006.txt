1. Goods moved (sent) across the customs border of Ukraine by international mail and express mail shall be declared by the postal operator, express carrier or declarant or the person authorised by him/her. In this case, goods, which are not subject to customs duties, as well as correspondence, letters and postcards, literature for the blind, printed materials, international express mail of documentary nature shall be declared orally under cover of transport documents. Other goods moved (sent) across the customs border of Ukraine by international mail and express mail shall be subject to a written declaration.
