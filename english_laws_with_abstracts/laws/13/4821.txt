Exemption from import duty shall be granted within the period of the investment project implementation to the extent of the total amount of state support unused at the times of the goods' customs clearance and specified in a special investment agreement concluded in accordance with the Law of Ukraine “On State Support of Investment Projects with Significant Investments in Ukraine”, taking into account the data from the Register of investment projects with significant investments in terms of the amount of such support, as well as the date and time of entry of the relevant information into such Register.
