Operations with goods placed under the customs regime of a free customs zone and located in the territories of free customs zones of commercial type, shall be limited to simple warehouse operations necessary to ensure their preservation, i.e movement of goods within a free customs zone for rational placement, cleaning, airing, maintenance of optimal storage temperature, drying (including creation of a heat flow), rust control, pest control, inventory. The shredding of batches, formation of shipments sorting, packing, repackaging, marking, loading, unloading, reloading and other similar operations shall also be allowed.
