When goods are placed under the customs regime of re-export in accordance with clause 5, part 1 of Article 86 of this Code, the amount of import duties paid at the time of import of these goods shall be repaid to persons who had paid them or to their legal successors in accordance with this Code. When goods are placed under the customs regime of re-import in accordance with clause 5, part 1 of Article 86 of this Code the excise tax and value-added tax shall be collected in accordance with the Tax Code of Ukraine.
