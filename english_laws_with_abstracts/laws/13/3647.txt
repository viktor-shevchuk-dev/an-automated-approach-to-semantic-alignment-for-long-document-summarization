1. Diplomatic mail and consular bag of foreign states moved across the customs border of Ukraine shall not be subject to breaking seal or holding. If there are sufficient grounds to believe that the consular bag contains goods not specified in part 3 of this Article, the customs authority may require breaking the seal of the suitcase by authorised persons of that foreign state in the presence of customs officials. In case of refusal to have the seal broken, such suitcase shall be returned to the place of departure.
