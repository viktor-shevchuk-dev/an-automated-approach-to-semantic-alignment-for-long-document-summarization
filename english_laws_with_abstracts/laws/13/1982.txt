6. The period for storage of international express mail under customs control may not exceed 30 days. Upon written request of the declarant (representative), such period may be extended (but not more than for 15 days) by the customs authority, in whose operation area the storage of international express mail is located, or the customs authority that carries out customs clearance at the location of the recipient. In case of refusal to extend the storage period, the customs authority shall immediately, in writing or in electronic form, inform the declarant (representative) about the grounds and reasons for such refusal.
