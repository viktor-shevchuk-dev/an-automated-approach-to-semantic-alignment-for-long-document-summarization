Foreign economic agreement (contract) shall be concluded by the foreign economic activity entities or their representatives in a simple written or electronic form unless otherwise provided for by international agreement of Ukraine or law. In case of export of services (except for transport ones), a foreign economic agreement (contract) may be concluded by accepting a public proposal on the agreement (offer) or by exchanging electronic messages, or otherwise, in particular, by issuing an invoice, including in an electronic form, for the services delivered. A representative's authority to conclude a foreign economic agreement (contract) may arise from a mandate (power of attorney), constituent documents, agreements and other grounds that do not contradict this Law. Actions that are carried out on behalf of a foreign entity of foreign economic activity by a foreign economic activity entity of Ukraine duly authorised to do so shall be deemed to be the actions of this foreign entity of foreign economic activity.
